Why Raw Dog Food Is Perfect for Summer Active Dogs

Raw diets provide dogs with nutrient-dense, whole-food nutrition that supports muscle development, joint health, and sustained energy—all critical factors for dogs engaging in summer activities. Unlike processed kibble, raw food contains natural enzymes and bioavailable nutrients that are easier for dogs to digest and utilize. This means better energy conversion and improved recovery after play sessions.

Smallbatch Pets' raw food options are specifically formulated to meet these demands. Frequently Asked Questions

Is raw dog food safe for summer feeding?

Yes, raw dog food is safe when handled properly. Smallbatch Pets' products are flash-frozen to preserve freshness and prevent bacterial growth. Simply thaw in the refrigerator and serve. How much raw food should I feed my active dog in summer?

Active dogs typically require 2-3% of their body weight in raw food daily, though this varies by individual metabolism and activity level. Smallbatch Pets provides detailed feeding guidelines based on your dog's size, age, and activity level. Consult their nutrition experts for personalized recommendations.
