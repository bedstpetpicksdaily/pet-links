Frequently Asked Questions

Are frozen raw pet meals safe for summer storage?

Yes, when stored properly in a freezer at 0°F or below, frozen raw meals remain safe and nutritious. Smallbatch Pets provides clear storage guidelines to ensure optimal freshness and food safety throughout summer months.

Can I transition my pet to lightly cooked meals from traditional kibble?

Absolutely. Lightly cooked options like those from Smallbatch Pets serve as an excellent transition food. Introduce them gradually by mixing with your pet's current food over 7-10 days to allow digestive adjustment.

What's the difference between freeze-dried and frozen raw treats?

Freeze-dried treats have moisture removed through a preservation process, making them shelf-stable and travel-friendly. Frozen raw maintains original moisture content and requires refrigeration but offers maximum nutritional density and are ideal for summer gifting.

Is bone broth appropriate for all pet ages?

Bone broth from quality brands like Smallbatch Pets is generally safe for pets of all ages. However, consult your veterinarian before introducing it to puppies, kittens, or pets with specific health conditions.

How long do Smallbatch Pets frozen raw meals last in storage?

When properly frozen, Smallbatch Pets raw meals typically remain fresh for 2-3 months. Check product packaging for specific guidelines and thaw in refrigerator 24 hours before serving.
