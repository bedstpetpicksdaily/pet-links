Frequently Asked Questions

What temperature should outdoor pet furniture be able to handle?

Quality outdoor pet furniture should withstand temperatures up to 120°F and beyond. AECOJOY's products are engineered with UV-resistant materials and ventilation systems designed for extreme heat, making them suitable for most climates.

How often should I clean outdoor pet furniture?

Monthly cleaning is recommended, or every two weeks in high-use situations. Use mild soap, water, and a brush to remove dirt and debris. Ensure complete drying before your pet uses the furniture again to prevent mold growth.

Are elevated pet beds safe for all dog sizes?

Yes, elevated pet beds accommodate various dog sizes when properly sized. AECOJOY offers multiple dimensions and weight capacities, so you can select the perfect fit for your specific pet's needs and preferences.

Can outdoor pet crates be left outside year-round?

While quality outdoor crates like those from AECOJOY resist weathering, storing them under a cover or in a garage during off-seasons extends their lifespan and maintains appearance.

What features should I prioritize for hot weather pet furniture?

Prioritize ventilation, elevation, shade capability, and UV-resistant materials. These features work together to keep your pet cool and comfortable while protecting furniture from sun damage.
