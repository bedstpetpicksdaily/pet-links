Frequently Asked Questions

Are PetTreatery treats suitable for all pet ages?

PetTreatery offers treats specifically formulated for puppies, adult dogs, senior dogs, and kittens. Always check the age recommendations on the packaging and consult your veterinarian if your pet has specific health concerns.

What makes premium treats worth the investment?

Premium treats like those from PetTreatery use natural, high-quality ingredients without artificial fillers, colors, or preservatives. They often provide better nutritional value, support digestive health, and use sustainable sourcing practices that benefit both pets and the environment.

Can I use these treats for training during summer?

Absolutely! PetTreatery's freeze-dried options are particularly good for training because they're lightweight, portable, and won't melt in hot weather. Their single-ingredient treats are also ideal for positive reinforcement without adding excessive calories.

Do PetTreatery treats help with any specific health concerns?

Many PetTreatery treats include functional ingredients like probiotics for digestive health, omega-3s for coat quality, and joint-support formulas. However, if your pet has specific health concerns, consult your veterinarian before introducing new treats.

How should I store premium pet treats during summer?

Store treats in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Freeze-dried treats are shelf-stable but should be kept in airtight containers. For extended storage during hot months, consider keeping them in the refrigerator or freezer.
