How does grain-free pet nutrition help dogs during summer?

Grain-free formulations reduce digestive heat and metabolic stress, helping your dog maintain stable body temperature. They also improve nutrient absorption and support better hydration, making them ideal for summer feeding.

Can I transition my dog to Zeal Pet Food immediately?

It's recommended to transition gradually over 7-10 days, mixing increasing amounts of premium raw nutrition with your dog's current food. This allows their digestive system to adjust smoothly while maximizing the benefits of the raw dog food.
