Key Differences and Considerations

When comparing AECOJOY's portable versus stationary models, weight capacity, setup time, and durability are primary factors. Portable crates typically accommodate smaller to medium-sized dogs comfortably, while stationary models can support larger breeds. Setup time differs dramatically—portable AECOJOY crates deploy in minutes, whereas stationary models require proper assembly but offer permanent setup advantages. Consider your dog's behavior, travel duration, and destination when making your selection. AECOJOY's product specifications clearly outline weight limits, dimensions, and recommended use cases to guide your purchase decision.
