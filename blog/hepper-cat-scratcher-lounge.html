Frequently Asked Questions

Is the HEPPER Cat Scratcher Lounge suitable for large cats?

Yes, the HEPPER Cat Scratcher Lounge is designed to accommodate cats of various sizes. However, very large or heavy breeds may find the fit snug when using it as a lounge. Always check the product dimensions on the Amazon listing before purchasing to ensure it's the right size for your cat.

How long does the HEPPER Cat Scratcher Lounge last?

Durability depends on how frequently and aggressively your cat uses it. Thanks to its reversible design, you can flip it over once one side becomes worn, effectively doubling its lifespan. Most cat owners report getting several months to over a year of use before needing a replacement.

Does the HEPPER Cat Scratcher Lounge come with catnip?

Some versions or bundles available on Amazon may include catnip to help attract your cat to the scratcher. Check the specific product listing on Amazon for details on what's included with your purchase.

Is cardboard safe for cats to scratch and chew?

Yes, corrugated cardboard is generally considered safe for cats. While cats may occasionally ingest small amounts of cardboard fibers during scratching, this is typically harmless. If your cat excessively chews or eats large amounts of cardboard, consult your veterinarian.
