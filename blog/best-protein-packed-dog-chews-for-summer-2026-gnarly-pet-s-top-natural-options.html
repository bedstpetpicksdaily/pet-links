Why Protein-Packed Dog Chews Matter for Summer

During summer months, dogs need adequate protein to maintain muscle tone, especially if they're spending time outdoors hiking, swimming, or playing fetch. Protein-packed chews provide concentrated nutrients in a format that keeps dogs occupied during peak heat hours when outdoor activity should be limited. High-protein treats also support healthy skin and coat development, which is crucial during shedding season.

Gnarly Pet specializes in natural dog chews made from real meat and wholesome ingredients, ensuring your dog gets premium nutrition without harmful fillers.
