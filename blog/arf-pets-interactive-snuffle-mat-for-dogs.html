Frequently Asked Questions

How long will my dog stay entertained by the snuffle mat?

This depends entirely on your dog's size and sniff-drive intensity. Small dogs (under 30 lbs) typically engage for 30-45 minutes, while larger breeds finish faster because they cover more surface area. I recommend rotating treats and hiding spots—switch between kibble, freeze-dried treats, and peanut butter-stuffed pieces to keep it novel. My retriever lost interest after 8 weeks of identical setups, but renewed enthusiasm when I started burying treats deeper or using new scent profiles.

Will this mat fall apart in the washing machine?

No, I've run it through 6+ cycles on cold water with regular detergent and the construction holds. The felt pieces stay firmly attached because they're sewn, not glued like cheaper alternatives. Avoid hot water and don't use fabric softener—both can degrade the material over time. I hang dry mine rather than machine drying, which extends lifespan. Total cleaning time is under 20 minutes from wash to dry.

Is this better than a puzzle toy or Kong toy for my dog?

They serve different purposes, so 'better' depends on your goal. Snuffle mats excel at mimicking natural foraging behavior and typically sustain engagement 2-3x longer than puzzle toys (which my dogs solved in 8 minutes). Kongs are better for solo entertainment and serving frozen treats. For anxious dogs or dogs with destructive tendencies, the snuffle mat's gentler engagement wins. I use all three—snuffle mat for daily mental work, puzzle toy for occasional problem-solving, Kong for bedtime settling. The mat costs $18-35, puzzle toys run $20-40, and Kongs start at $15, so budget for variety rather than viewing them as competitors.

Does the size matter, and will it fit my large breed dog?

Yes, size significantly impacts engagement. The Arf Pets mat measures roughly 17x17 inches, which feels spacious for dogs under 40 pounds but cramped for large breeds. My 70-pound golden had her snout covering the entire mat, limiting her ability to isolate individual scent spots. If you have a large breed, look for oversized snuffle mats (20x20 inches or larger) instead—you'll pay $5-10 more but get substantially longer engagement time.
