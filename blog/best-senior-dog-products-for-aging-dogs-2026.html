Frequently Asked Questions

What products help senior dogs with joint pain and arthritis?

Orthopedic memory foam dog beds, joint supplements containing glucosamine and chondroitin, and elevated food bowls are among the best products to ease joint pain in aging dogs. Dog ramps or steps also reduce stress on arthritic joints by helping seniors get onto furniture or into cars without jumping. Look for brands like FurHaven, Zesty Paws, and PetSafe, which consistently rank highly for senior dog joint support in 2026.

What should I feed my aging dog to keep them healthy?

Senior dogs thrive on high-quality, easily digestible protein sources with reduced calories to prevent obesity, which worsens joint and organ stress. Look for senior-formula dog foods with added omega-3 fatty acids, antioxidants, and fiber to support brain health, coat condition, and digestion. Top-rated options in 2026 include Hill's Science Diet Senior, Royal Canin Aging, and Purina Pro Plan Bright Mind for dogs seven years and older.

How do I know if my dog is considered a senior and needs special products?

Most dogs are considered seniors between ages seven and ten, though larger breeds like Great Danes may reach senior status as early as five or six years old. Signs your dog may need senior-specific products include slowing down on walks, difficulty climbing stairs, weight changes, cloudiness in the eyes, or more frequent accidents indoors. Once you notice these changes, it's a good time to consult your vet and transition to products designed for aging dogs.

What are the best mobility aids for dogs that can't walk well?

Dog wheelchairs, no-slip booties, harnesses with rear support handles, and non-slip rugs or floor mats are the most recommended mobility aids for senior dogs struggling to walk. Brands like Walkin' Wheels and Help 'Em Up Harness are highly rated for dogs with rear leg weakness or degenerative myelopathy. Placing non-slip mats throughout your home on hardwood or tile floors is one of the simplest and most affordable ways to immediately improve your senior dog's mobility and confidence.
