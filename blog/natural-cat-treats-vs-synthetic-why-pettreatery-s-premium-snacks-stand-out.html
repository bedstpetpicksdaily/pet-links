Understanding Natural vs. Synthetic Cat Treats

Natural cat treats are made from real, recognizable ingredients like chicken, fish, turkey, and vegetables. These treats contain minimal processing and avoid artificial flavors, colors, and preservatives. Synthetic treats, conversely, often rely on by-products, fillers, and chemical additives to achieve texture and taste. While they may be more affordable, synthetic treats lack the nutritional density that supports your cat's optimal health.
