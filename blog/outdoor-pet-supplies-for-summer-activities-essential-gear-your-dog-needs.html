Frequently Asked Questions

What makes outdoor pet supplies different from regular indoor pet beds?

Outdoor pet supplies are specifically engineered to withstand weather conditions, UV exposure, and moisture. They feature water-resistant or waterproof materials, improved drainage, and enhanced durability compared to indoor furniture. AECOJOY's outdoor collection specifically addresses these requirements with weather-tested designs.

How do I choose the right size outdoor dog bed for my pet?

Measure your dog from nose to tail when fully stretched, then add 4-6 inches for comfort. Consider your dog's sleeping position—side sleepers need longer beds, while curled sleepers need less space. AECOJOY offers multiple size options to accommodate all dog breeds from small to extra-large.

Can outdoor dog crates be left outside overnight?

Most quality outdoor crates like those from AECOJOY can withstand overnight outdoor conditions, but consider weather forecasts and temperature. In extremely hot or cold conditions, bringing crates inside is recommended. Always ensure proper ventilation and protection from direct elements.

What temperature is too hot for dogs to be outside?

Dogs generally struggle when temperatures exceed 85°F, especially during activity. Above 90°F, heat exhaustion risks increase significantly. This is why shade structures and ventilated beds from AECOJOY are essential for summer outdoor time—they help manage heat exposure and keep your dog safe.

How often should I clean outdoor pet furniture?

Clean outdoor pet supplies weekly during regular use, or after exposure to dirt, mud, or water. Most AECOJOY products feature removable, washable covers for easy maintenance. Check manufacturer guidelines for specific cleaning instructions to preserve material longevity.
