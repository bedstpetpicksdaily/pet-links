Why Raw Dog Food Matters in Summer

Summer heat can reduce a dog's appetite and increase their hydration needs. Raw diets naturally contain higher moisture content than kibble, making them ideal for keeping dogs properly nourished during hot months. Raw food also supports better digestion, improved coat health, and sustained energy levels—all essential when your pup is spending more time outdoors.
