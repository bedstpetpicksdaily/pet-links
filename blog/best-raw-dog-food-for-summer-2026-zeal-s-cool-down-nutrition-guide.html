Frequently Asked Questions

Is raw dog food safe during summer heat?

Yes, raw dog food is safe during summer when handled properly. Freeze-dried raw options like Zeal Pet Food eliminate refrigeration concerns while maintaining nutritional benefits. Always follow storage guidelines and transition gradually to prevent digestive upset.

How much should I feed my dog during summer?

Summer often reduces appetite naturally due to heat. Monitor your dog's body condition and adjust portions accordingly. Raw food's nutrient density means smaller portions often provide complete nutrition. Consult your veterinarian for personalized feeding recommendations based on your dog's activity level.

What makes Zeal Pet Food different from other raw brands?
