Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I give my active dog treats during outdoor activities?

For outdoor activities, offer small treats every 1-2 hours depending on activity intensity and duration. Natural treats like those from PetTreatery should account for about 10% of your dog's daily calorie intake. Always bring fresh water and monitor your dog's energy levels.

Are PetTreatery treats suitable for dogs with sensitive stomachs?

Yes, PetTreatery offers limited-ingredient and grain-free options specifically formulated for dogs with food sensitivities. Their natural ingredients are less likely to cause digestive upset compared to treats with artificial additives. Always introduce new treats gradually to your dog's diet.

What makes natural dog treats better than regular commercial treats?

Natural treats contain real meat and whole ingredients without artificial preservatives, colors, or fillers. They provide better nutritional value, are easier to digest, and reduce the risk of adverse reactions. Dogs also find them more palatable and satisfying.

How should I store PetTreatery treats for outdoor trips?

Store treats in an airtight container or their original packaging to maintain freshness. For outdoor activities, use a sealed bag or container in your backpack. Most natural treats remain fresh for weeks when properly stored away from heat and moisture.

Can puppies eat PetTreatery natural dog treats?

PetTreatery offers treats suitable for different life stages. Always check the product label for age recommendations. Puppies typically need smaller-sized treats and may benefit from softer formats during teething. Consult your vet if your puppy has specific nutritional requirements.
