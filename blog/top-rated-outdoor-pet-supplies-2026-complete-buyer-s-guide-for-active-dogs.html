Frequently Asked Questions

What makes outdoor pet supplies different from indoor options?

Outdoor pet supplies feature weather-resistant materials, enhanced durability against UV exposure and moisture, and designs optimized for variable temperatures. They're built to withstand constant environmental exposure while maintaining comfort and safety for your dog.

How do I choose the right size dog crate for outdoor use?

Measure your dog's length from nose to tail and height from paw to head. The crate should allow your dog to stand, turn around, and lie down comfortably. AECOJOY offers crates in various sizes specifically designated for small, medium, and large breeds.

Are AECOJOY pet beds machine washable?

Most AECOJOY pet bed covers are removable and machine washable, making maintenance convenient. Always check product specifications, as some outdoor beds may have specific cleaning instructions to preserve weather-resistant treatments.

Can outdoor pet furniture handle extreme weather conditions?

Quality outdoor pet furniture like AECOJOY's products withstands typical outdoor weather. However, during extreme conditions (heavy storms, intense heat), it's recommended to move furniture to shelter or use protective covers for extended equipment life.

What's the best way to maintain AECOJOY outdoor pet supplies?

Regularly rinse with fresh water, allow to air dry completely, and store cushions indoors during off-season. Periodically inspect for wear and apply protective treatments as needed to maintain weather-resistant properties.
