Frequently Asked Questions

Can I add supplements to cold-pressed dog food safely?

Yes, absolutely. Cold-pressed dog food is specifically designed to work synergistically with quality supplements. However, ensure supplements are species-appropriate and consult your veterinarian about dosing, especially if your dog is on medication or has existing health conditions.

What supplements work best with Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed food?

Omega-3 fish oil, probiotics, joint support supplements (glucosamine/chondroitin), and antioxidant formulas are excellent complements. Nextrition Pet's natural formulation enhances the bioavailability of these supplements, making them more effective.

Is summer the best time to introduce dog supplements?

Summer is an excellent time due to increased activity and heat-related stress. However, supplements provide year-round benefits. Starting in summer allows you to assess how your dog responds before maintaining the regimen through colder months.
