Frequently Asked Questions

How do cooling dog beds actually work in hot weather?

Cooling dog beds use gel-infused memory foam that absorbs and dissipates body heat without electricity. The technology works similarly to cooling pillows for humans—the gel remains cooler than ambient temperature and pulls heat away from your dog's body, providing instant relief. AECOJOY's cooling beds maintain this technology throughout their lifespan without degrading.

Are AECOJOY outdoor crates suitable for year-round use?

Yes, AECOJOY outdoor crates feature weather-resistant materials and enhanced ventilation that adapt to seasonal needs. While specifically engineered for summer heat management with reflective surfaces and airflow optimization, their durable construction and protective design make them suitable for extended outdoor use throughout the year with proper maintenance.

What size AECOJOY cooling bed should I choose for my dog?

AECOJOY offers cooling beds in multiple sizes: Small (for dogs under 25 lbs), Medium (25-50 lbs), Large (50-90 lbs), and Extra-Large (90+ lbs). Measure your dog's length from nose to tail and compare with product dimensions. Choosing a size that allows your dog to stretch fully maximizes comfort and cooling benefits.

How often should I clean AECOJOY cooling bed covers?

AECOJOY cooling bed covers are machine-washable and should be cleaned every 1-2 weeks during heavy use or when visibly soiled. Regular washing maintains hygiene while preserving the moisture-wicking and cooling properties. The gel-infused core should never be submerged but can be spot-cleaned as needed.
