Understanding Freeze-Dried Pet Food: What Makes It Different

Freeze-drying is a preservation method that removes 98% of moisture from raw ingredients while retaining nutritional value. This process creates lightweight, shelf-stable meals that rehydrate quickly and maintain the nutrient density of raw diets. For active dogs, this means concentrated protein, essential amino acids, and bioavailable nutrients in every serving. Zeal Pet Food utilizes this technology to transform premium raw ingredients into convenient meal options without synthetic additives or preservatives.
