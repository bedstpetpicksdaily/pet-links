Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I use the FURminator Deshedding Tool on my pet?

For best results, FURminator recommends using the deshedding tool 1 to 2 times per week for 10 to 20 minutes per session. Avoid overuse, as excessive brushing can irritate the skin or thin the coat too much over time.

Is the FURminator safe to use on all dog and cat breeds?

The FURminator is safe for most breeds with an undercoat, but it is not recommended for pets with particularly sensitive skin, skin conditions, or for breeds with single-layer coats such as Poodles or Shih Tzus. Always consult your vet if you're unsure whether it's appropriate for your specific pet.
