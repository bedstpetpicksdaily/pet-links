Is this appropriate for cats, or is it only for dogs?

How does this compare to professional grooming tools?

This is consumer-grade equipment, not professional-level. Does the cordless battery actually last through a full grooming session?

Yes, 90 minutes is genuinely achievable on a single charge for standard grooming. However, if you're grooming multiple large dogs back-to-back, you'll need to swap to the second battery.
