Why Frozen Treats Matter in Summer

During hot summer months, dogs are susceptible to dehydration and heat exhaustion. Frozen treats serve a dual purpose: they provide mental stimulation through the enjoyment of eating, while also helping to regulate your dog's body temperature from the inside out. Unlike regular treats, frozen options encourage slower consumption, keeping your pup engaged longer while allowing their body to cool down gradually.

PetTreatery's premium dog treats are ideal candidates for freezing. Made with natural ingredients and free from artificial additives, these treats maintain their nutritional integrity even when frozen. You can serve them straight from the freezer for an instant cool-down snack that your dog will absolutely love.

Best Frozen Treat Ideas for Summer

The simplest approach is to take PetTreatery's existing treats and freeze them. Their natural dog treats work wonderfully when chilled, creating a longer-lasting chew that keeps dogs occupied. You can also create DIY frozen treats by mixing PetTreatery's smaller snacks with dog-safe ingredients like plain yogurt or pumpkin puree, then freezing them in ice cube trays.

Another excellent option is to soak PetTreatery's treats in bone broth (unsalted) and freeze them. This adds flavor, hydration, and essential nutrients while creating an irresistible summer snack. Your dog gets the taste they love plus the cooling benefits they need during scorching days.

Hydration-Focused Healthy Snacks

Beyond frozen treats, incorporating hydrating snacks into your dog's summer routine is essential. PetTreatery offers natural pet snacks that complement a hydration-focused diet. Treats made with ingredients like sweet potato, chicken, and vegetables provide moisture content while delivering essential nutrients that support overall wellness during the hot season.

Mix hydrating snacks with PetTreatery's premium selection throughout the day to ensure your dog stays cool and well-nourished. The variety keeps mealtime interesting while providing the balanced nutrition active dogs need during summer activities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I freeze any dog treat?

Most dog treats can be frozen, but PetTreatery's natural treats are specifically formulated to maintain their quality and nutritional value when frozen, making them an ideal choice for summer snacking.

How often can I give my dog frozen treats in summer?

Frozen treats can be given daily during summer months as part of your dog's total treat allowance (which should be no more than 10% of daily calories). PetTreatery's premium snacks are nutritionally balanced for regular inclusion in your dog's diet.

Are PetTreatery treats safe for dogs with sensitive stomachs?

Yes, PetTreatery specializes in natural pet treats without artificial additives or fillers that commonly cause digestive upset. Always introduce new treats gradually, and consult your vet if your dog has specific dietary restrictions.

What's the best way to serve frozen treats?

Serve frozen treats directly from the freezer on hot days, or thaw them slightly for dogs with sensitive teeth. You can also stuff PetTreatery treats into puzzle toys and freeze them for extended entertainment.
