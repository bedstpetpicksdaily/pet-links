Frequently Asked Questions

Are freeze-dried treats appropriate for raw food diets?

Yes, freeze-dried treats are ideal for raw food diets. The freeze-drying process removes moisture while preserving nutrients and enzymes, maintaining the nutritional integrity of raw feeding. Zeal Pet Food's freeze-dried options offer convenience without compromising the raw food philosophy.

Can I give my cat raw food treats during summer?

Absolutely. Cats thrive on raw diets, and summer presents excellent opportunities for variety. Zeal Pet Food offers premium freeze-dried and raw options specifically formulated for cats, featuring appropriate protein ratios and essential nutrients for feline health.

How should I store raw and freeze-dried treats in summer heat?

Freeze-dried treats require no refrigeration and remain stable in warm temperatures, making them perfect for summer. Raw treats should be kept frozen and thawed as needed. Always follow storage guidelines on packaging and keep all treats in cool, dry locations away from direct sunlight.

What makes Zeal Pet Food's grain-free nutrition different?

Zeal Pet Food focuses on whole prey models and nutrient-dense ingredients rather than grain substitutes. Their grain-free approach means no peas, potatoes, or legumes that compromise digestive health—just premium proteins and organs that raw-fed pets require.

Are raw treats safe for puppies and kittens?

Raw diets can support puppies and kittens, but introduce treats gradually and consult your veterinarian about age-appropriate portions. Zeal Pet Food provides options designed for different life stages, ensuring safe, nutritious supplementation from youth through adulthood.
