Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to see health improvements from premium natural pet food?

Most pets show visible improvements within 4-6 weeks of switching to premium natural food like Zeal. Changes include shinier coats, increased energy, better digestion, and improved body condition. Some benefits, like improved skin health, may take 8-12 weeks to fully manifest.

Is Zeal Pet Food suitable for all ages and health conditions?

Zeal offers formulas for different life stages and dietary needs. Their grain-free and raw options work well for pets with sensitivities. However, always consult your veterinarian before switching to a new food, especially for pets with specific health conditions or medications.

What makes freeze-dried pet food better than traditional kibble?

Freeze-dried pet food retains more nutrients than traditional kibble because it's processed at low temperatures. It contains no synthetic preservatives, offers superior digestibility, and provides species-appropriate nutrition closer to what pets eat in nature. It's also convenient and less messy than raw food.

Can I mix Zeal Pet Food with my pet's current diet?

Yes, Zeal products work excellently as meal toppers or supplements. Many pet owners transition gradually by mixing new food with existing diet over 7-10 days to avoid digestive upset. This approach also lets you test if your pet enjoys Zeal before committing fully.

How should I store Zeal's raw and freeze-dried products?

Raw dog food should be stored in the freezer and thawed in the refrigerator before serving. Freeze-dried products can be stored in a cool, dry place but last longer when refrigerated after opening. Always follow storage instructions on packaging for optimal freshness and safety.
