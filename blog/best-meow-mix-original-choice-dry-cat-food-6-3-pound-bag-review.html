Frequently Asked Questions

Is Meow Mix Original Choice dry cat food good for cats?

Meow Mix Original Choice is a budget-friendly option that most cats enjoy due to its variety of flavors and crunchy texture. However, it contains corn and soy fillers rather than meat as primary ingredients, so it's better suited as an occasional food rather than a premium daily diet for optimal nutrition.

How long does a 6.3 pound bag of Meow Mix cat food last?

A 6.3 pound bag typically lasts 2-3 weeks for an average adult cat, depending on the cat's weight and how much you feed daily. Most cats require about 1/4 to 1/2 cup per day, so check the feeding guidelines on the bag for your specific cat's needs.

Does Meow Mix Original Choice cause digestive problems in cats?

Some cats may experience digestive upset or loose stools when switching to Meow Mix due to its high carbohydrate content and artificial ingredients. If your cat has a sensitive stomach, it's best to introduce this food gradually mixed with their current food, or consider a higher-quality alternative.

What are the main ingredients in Meow Mix Original Choice dry cat food?

The primary ingredients are corn, soybean meal, and meat by-products rather than whole meat proteins. It also contains artificial colors and flavors to appeal to cats, making it less nutritionally dense than premium brands that use real meat as the first ingredient.
