Frequently Asked Questions About Dog Training

What is the best age to start dog training?

The best time to start training is as early as possible—ideally when puppies are 7-8 weeks old. Young dogs learn faster and are more receptive to positive reinforcement, though adult dogs can absolutely learn new behaviors at any age with patience and consistency.

How long does it typically take to train a dog?

Basic obedience training usually takes 4-6 weeks with consistent daily practice of 10-15 minutes per session. Complex behaviors and behavioral issues may take several months, and remember that training is an ongoing process throughout your dog's life for maintenance and continued learning.
