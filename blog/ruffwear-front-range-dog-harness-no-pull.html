Frequently Asked Questions

What size should I choose for my dog?

Ruffwear Front Range harnesses come in multiple sizes from XS to XL. Measure your dog's girth and refer to the sizing chart on Amazon to ensure the best fit. A proper fit should allow one finger of space between the harness and your dog's body.

Is this harness suitable for all dog breeds?

Yes, the Ruffwear Front Range works for most dog breeds when properly sized. It's particularly beneficial for dogs that pull excessively, including puppies in training and large, energetic breeds.

How do I properly use the front-clip feature?

Attach your leash to the front D-ring located on the harness's chest area. When your dog pulls, the front-clip design naturally redirects them toward you, discouraging pulling without causing discomfort.

Can I machine wash the Ruffwear Front Range harness?

Hand washing is recommended to extend the harness's lifespan. Use mild soap and water, then air dry completely. Avoid machine washing as it may damage the reflective trim and hardware.
