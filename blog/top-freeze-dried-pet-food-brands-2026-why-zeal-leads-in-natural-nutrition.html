What Makes Freeze-Dried Pet Food Superior?

Freeze-drying is a preservation method that removes 98% of moisture while maintaining nutritional integrity. Unlike heat-processed kibble, freeze-dried pet food doesn't require synthetic binders, fillers, or preservatives. This process makes it ideal for pets with sensitive stomachs and those requiring a raw diet alternative. The result is a lightweight, shelf-stable food that delivers raw-like nutrition in convenient, shelf-safe portions.
