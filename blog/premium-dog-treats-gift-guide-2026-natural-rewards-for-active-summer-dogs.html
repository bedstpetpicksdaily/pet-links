Frequently Asked Questions

Are Nextrition Pet treats suitable for all dog ages?

Yes, Nextrition Pet offers formulations for different life stages. Their cold-pressed treats and pet supplements come in various formulas designed for puppies, adults, and senior dogs. However, always check individual product guidelines and consult your vet if your dog has specific health conditions.

How do cold-pressed treats differ from regular dog treats?

Cold-pressing preserves heat-sensitive nutrients like enzymes, probiotics, and vitamins that are often destroyed during traditional baking or heat processing. This means Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed treats deliver superior nutritional value and better digestibility for your dog's gut health.

Can I use premium treats as meal replacements?

No, premium treats should complement a complete and balanced diet, not replace regular meals. Nextrition Pet's treats and supplements are designed to enhance your dog's existing nutrition plan, particularly valuable for active summer dogs needing additional support.

How should I store Nextrition Pet treats to maintain freshness?

Store cold-pressed treats in a cool, dry place. Because they lack artificial preservatives, proper storage in an airtight container extends shelf life. Refrigerating or freezing unopened packages maintains optimal freshness and nutritional integrity.

Are Nextrition Pet treats safe for dogs with allergies?

Nextrition Pet uses natural ingredients and offers various formulations, but individual sensitivities vary. Review ingredient lists carefully and consult your veterinarian if your dog has known food allergies or sensitivities before introducing new treats.
