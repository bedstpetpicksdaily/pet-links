Frequently Asked Questions

How long does one grooming session need to be?

Most owners report 10–15 minutes is enough to see visible results. The key is consistency—using it twice weekly beats a single 30-minute session. Your dog gets a gentle massage-like experience, so they actually cooperate, which saves you time and frustration.

Will this work on my short-haired dog, or is it just for double coats?

It works on both, but differently. Short-haired dogs benefit from the surface-level hair removal and the massaging effect. Double-coated dogs (Labs, Goldens, Huskies) see more dramatic results because there's more loose undercoat to pull out. Owners of short-haired breeds notice cleaner coats and less shedding, though the difference is subtler than with thick-coated breeds.

How do I wash and store these gloves?

Rinse them under warm water and let them air dry. Some owners use a lint roller to quickly remove collected hair before washing. They're designed to withstand months of regular use—storage is simple since they take up barely any space. No special maintenance required, which is part of why owners keep coming back to them.
