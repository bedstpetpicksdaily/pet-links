Why Raw Food Diet Helps Dogs Stay Cool

Raw dog food diets closely mimic what dogs would eat in nature, containing higher moisture content than conventional kibble. This moisture is vital during summer months when dehydration becomes a real concern. Raw meals absorb more efficiently, reducing the metabolic heat generated during digestion—a process called the thermic effect of food. When your dog's digestive system works less hard to break down food, less internal heat is produced, naturally keeping body temperature lower.

Zeal Pet Food's raw dog food formulations are carefully crafted with premium, grain-free ingredients that support optimal digestion and nutrient absorption. Their natural pet food approach eliminates unnecessary fillers and inflammatory ingredients that can stress the digestive system during hot weather.

Freeze-Dried Treats: Convenience Meets Nutrition

Freeze-dried treats offer an excellent middle ground between raw feeding and convenient snacking. The freeze-drying process removes moisture while preserving nutrients, creating lightweight, shelf-stable treats that maintain raw food benefits. These treats are perfect for summer cooling strategies—simply add water to create a cooling snack or offer them frozen for a refreshing treat. They're also portable for outdoor adventures and travel.

Zeal Pet Food's freeze-dried pet food range provides premium nutrition in convenient formats. Their grain-free freeze-dried treats contain real, recognizable ingredients without artificial additives, making them ideal for health-conscious pet owners seeking summer nutrition solutions.

Hydration and Nutrient Strategy

Beyond raw and freeze-dried options, consider adding moisture-rich whole foods to your dog's diet during summer. Watermelon, cucumber, and bone broth are excellent, dog-safe additions that boost hydration. When combined with Zeal Pet Food's premium raw formulations, these additions create a complete cooling nutrition strategy. Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch my dog to raw food during summer?

Yes, but introduce raw food gradually over 7-10 days to allow digestive adjustment. Start by mixing small amounts of Zeal Pet Food raw formulations with your dog's current food, gradually increasing the ratio. This slow transition minimizes digestive upset while allowing your dog to reap summer cooling benefits.

Are freeze-dried treats safe for all dogs?

Freeze-dried treats from reputable brands like Zeal Pet Food are safe for most dogs. However, consult your veterinarian if your dog has specific health conditions or allergies. Their grain-free, natural formulations are designed for digestive sensitivity and optimal nutrition.

How much raw food should I feed in summer?

Follow feeding guidelines based on your dog's weight and age, typically 2-3% of body weight daily. Zeal Pet Food provides clear feeding recommendations on their packaging. During summer, maintain consistent portions while increasing water and moisture-rich foods as supplemental treats rather than meal replacements.

What's the difference between raw and freeze-dried dog food?

Raw food contains natural moisture and must be refrigerated, while freeze-dried removes water for shelf stability. Both retain nutritional benefits from Zeal Pet Food's premium ingredients. Choose based on your lifestyle—raw for home feeding, freeze-dried for convenience and portability.
