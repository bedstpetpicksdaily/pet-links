Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use these on a matted coat?

No. If your dog's coat is severely matted, use a dematting comb first or see a groomer. These shears cut through healthy coat easily but will snag on mats and can damage the hair. Think of them as maintenance tools, not rescue equipment.

How often do I need to sharpen them?

With normal home grooming (once or twice monthly), most owners report 6-12 months before noticeable dulling. Professional sharpening runs $15-25, or you can buy affordable sharpening stones. This is cheaper than even two professional grooming sessions.

Are these safe for beginners?

Yes, if you take your time and watch tutorial videos first. The curved blades are more forgiving than straight ones when you're learning. Never rush or groom an anxious pet—grooming should be calm. Many owners start by just trimming paw pads and sanitary areas before attempting full-coat work.
