What to Look For

  • Shallow, wide design with multiple obstacles: Beagle puppies have shorter muzzles and can be surprisingly aggressive eaters, so you need a bowl with a wide surface area and strategically placed ridges or pegs that force them to navigate around barriers. Look for a bowl with a weighted or rubberized base that won't slide across your kitchen floor, and ensure it's made from BPA-free materials that can withstand their chewing and repeated washing.
