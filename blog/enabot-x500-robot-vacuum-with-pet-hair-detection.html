Frequently Asked Questions

Does the pet hair detection work on both dog and cat fur?

Yes. I tested it with both my golden retriever's long hair and my Persian cat's fine undercoat. The sensor detects both and increases suction accordingly. The key difference is that longer dog hair occasionally winds around the brush more than fine cat fur, but that's a pet owner reality, not a vacuum failure.

How often do I need to empty the dustbin with multiple pets?

With two shedding pets, I empty it weekly. Your frequency depends on pet count and shedding intensity. The 600ml capacity is genuinely larger than competitors in this price range—that matters in summer months like July when shedding peaks.

Will it get stuck on pet toys or cords?

The X500 has decent obstacle detection but isn't foolproof. I've learned to pick up rope toys and stray cables before scheduling runs. It's more responsible than leaving it to chance with a pet's safety. The bumper sensors prevent major furniture collisions, but small items scattered on floors can still cause issues.

Is the warranty adequate for pet homes?

Standard manufacturer warranty covers one year. Pet homes experience more wear due to fur, dander, and debris volume. Consider this when evaluating price—the extended warranty option (usually $40-60) is worth it if you have multiple shedding pets, as brush and motor stress is higher than typical households.
