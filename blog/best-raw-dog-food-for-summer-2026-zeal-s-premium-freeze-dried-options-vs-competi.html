Frequently Asked Questions

Is freeze-dried raw dog food safe for summer storage?

Yes, freeze-dried raw dog food like Zeal's premium options is shelf-stable and doesn't require refrigeration until opened, making it ideal for summer. Once opened, store in an airtight container in a cool, dry place or refrigerate for extended freshness.

How does Zeal Pet Food compare nutritionally to traditional kibble?

Zeal's freeze-dried raw food contains higher bioavailable nutrients and natural enzymes that support digestive health better than traditional kibble. The raw format preserves vitamins and minerals often lost in high-heat processing, delivering superior nutritional value.

Can I transition my dog to Zeal freeze-dried food in summer?

Yes, transition gradually over 7-10 days by mixing increasing amounts of Zeal's freeze-dried food with your dog's current diet. Summer transitions can be smoother since freeze-dried food is gentle on digestive systems and requires less adjustment time than other raw options.

Is Zeal Pet Food grain-free?

Yes, all Zeal Pet Food formulations are grain-free, using premium whole-food ingredients and vegetables instead of grains, making them suitable for dogs with grain sensitivities.
