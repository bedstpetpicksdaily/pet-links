Frequently Asked Questions

Are PetTreatery treats suitable for dogs with allergies?

Yes, PetTreatery offers limited-ingredient formulas and grain-free options specifically designed for dogs with sensitivities. Check the product descriptions for allergen information and consult your veterinarian for personalized recommendations.

How should I store premium pet treats during summer?

Store treats in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. For freeze-dried treats, an airtight container in the pantry is ideal. In extremely hot climates, consider refrigeration to maintain freshness and prevent degradation.

What's the difference between PetTreatery's freeze-dried and traditional treats?

Freeze-dried treats retain more nutritional value and natural flavors, making them highly concentrated and palatable. Traditional baked treats are longer-lasting and better for extended chewing. PetTreatery offers both, allowing you to choose based on your pet's preferences and needs.

Can I use PetTreatery treats for training?

Absolutely. PetTreatery treats are perfect for training due to their strong natural flavors and variety of sizes. For training sessions, you can break larger treats into smaller pieces or choose their pre-portioned training-size options.

Do PetTreatery treats contain artificial flavors or preservatives?

No. PetTreatery is committed to all-natural formulations. All treats are made without artificial flavors, colors, or chemical preservatives, relying instead on natural preservation methods and fresh ingredients.
