Is Zeal Pet Food suitable for all dog breeds?

Yes, Zeal offers formulations designed for different life stages and sizes. Their grain-free and raw options work for most dogs, though it's always best to consult your veterinarian when introducing new food, especially for pets with existing health conditions.

What makes Zeal's freeze-dried food different from raw?

Freeze-dried food retains the nutritional benefits of raw feeding while offering convenience and longer shelf life. It requires minimal preparation—just add water—making it an excellent option for busy pet owners who want raw nutrition without the storage demands.
