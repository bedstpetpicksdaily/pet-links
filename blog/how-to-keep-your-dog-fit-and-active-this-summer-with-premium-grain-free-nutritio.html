Frequently Asked Questions

Is grain-free food better for active dogs?

Yes, grain-free formulas eliminate unnecessary fillers and provide concentrated protein sources that support muscle development and sustained energy. They're typically easier to digest, allowing dogs to extract maximum nutrition for their active lifestyle.

What's the difference between raw and freeze-dried dog food?

Raw dog food is uncooked and unprocessed, retaining all natural enzymes and nutrients. Freeze-dried food uses a preservation method that removes moisture while maintaining nutritional integrity, offering similar benefits with extended shelf life and easier handling.

How much should I feed my active dog in summer?

Active dogs may require 10-20% more calories than sedentary pets. Monitor your dog's body condition score and adjust portions based on activity level. Consult your veterinarian for personalized feeding recommendations aligned with Zeal Pet Food's premium formulas.

Can grain-free food help with digestive issues?

Many dogs experience improved digestion on grain-free diets, as grains can be harder to break down. Premium grain-free options like Zeal Pet Food use digestible, whole-food ingredients that support gut health and nutrient absorption.

Is Zeal Pet Food suitable for all dog breeds and ages?

Zeal offers specialized formulas for different life stages and health needs. Always check product labels for age-appropriate recommendations and consult your veterinarian to find the best Zeal option for your dog's specific requirements.
