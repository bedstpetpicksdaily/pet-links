Which PetTreatery treats are best for dogs with sensitive stomachs?

PetTreatery offers several grain-free and limited-ingredient options specifically formulated for sensitive digestive systems. Do PetTreatery treats work well as training rewards?

Absolutely! Many PetTreatery treats come in small, bite-sized portions that are perfect for training sessions. Their lower-calorie options are ideal for frequent rewarding without overfeeding, and the high palatability means dogs are eager to earn them during training exercises.
