Frequently Asked Questions

What makes cold-pressed dog food better than traditional kibble?

Cold-pressed processing uses low temperatures to preserve nutrients, enzymes, and beneficial compounds that are often destroyed during traditional high-heat kibble production. This results in better digestibility and more bioavailable nutrients for your dog.

Is Nextrition Pet food suitable for dogs with sensitive stomachs?

Yes. Nextrition Pet's gut-healthy formulas are specifically designed for digestive wellness. Their natural ingredients and absence of artificial additives make them an excellent choice for dogs with sensitive digestion or food sensitivities.

How should I store Nextrition Pet cold-pressed food?

Cold-pressed food should be stored in a cool, dry place or refrigerated after opening. Because it lacks artificial preservatives, it has a shorter shelf life than traditional kibble. Always check the expiration date and follow the storage instructions on the packaging.

Can I mix Nextrition Pet food with other brands?

Yes, you can mix Nextrition Pet food with other brands, though gradual transitions work best. Start with a small ratio of Nextrition Pet food and increase over time to allow your dog's digestive system to adjust to the new nutrition.
