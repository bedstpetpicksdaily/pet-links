Frequently Asked Questions

Is Zeal raw dog food safe for my pet?

Yes. Zeal Pet Food follows strict safety protocols and food handling standards. Their raw dog food is formulated to minimize pathogen risks while maintaining nutritional integrity. Always follow proper storage guidelines and handle raw pet food with the same food safety practices you'd use for human meals.

How does Zeal's freeze-dried pet food compare to raw dog food?

Zeal's freeze-dried pet food offers the nutritional benefits of raw dog food with added convenience and longer shelf life. Freeze-drying preserves nutrients and enzymes while eliminating moisture, making it easier to store and serve. Both options deliver superior nutrition compared to traditional kibble.

Is Zeal grain-free pet nutrition suitable for all dogs?

Zeal's grain-free formulations are ideal for dogs with grain sensitivities or allergies. However, not all dogs require grain-free diets. Consult your veterinarian to determine if grain-free pet nutrition is appropriate for your specific pet's health needs.

How does Zeal Pet Food pricing compare to competitors?

While Zeal is premium-priced compared to mass-market brands, it's competitively positioned against comparable quality brands like Stella & Chewy's and Primal. The superior nutritional density often means feeding less food, offsetting the higher per-unit cost.

Can I feed Zeal raw dog food and cat food to my multi-pet household?

Absolutely. Zeal Pet Food offers both dog food and cat food formulations specifically tailored to each species' nutritional requirements. Their grain-free options work well for multi-pet households seeking premium natural nutrition.
