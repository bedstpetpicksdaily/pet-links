Frequently Asked Questions

What size portable dog crate should I choose for summer travel?

Choose a crate that allows your dog to stand, turn around, and lie down comfortably. For most dogs, measure length (nose to tail base) and height (top of head to floor), then select the next size up. AECOJOY offers small (up to 15 lbs), medium (15-40 lbs), large (40-70 lbs), and extra-large (70+ lbs) options.

How do I keep my dog cool in a portable crate during summer?

Look for crates with maximum mesh ventilation like AECOJOY's designs. Add a cooling pad inside, ensure proper airflow in your vehicle, park in shade, and never leave your dog unattended in hot conditions. Offer water regularly and consider mesh crate covers that provide shade without blocking ventilation.

Can portable dog crates be used for permanent home setups?

While designed for travel, quality portable crates from AECOJOY can absolutely serve as home crates when needed. However, their collapsible nature means less solid structure than traditional hard-sided crates. They're ideal for both temporary setups during house guests or permanent use for small spaces and apartments.

How long does it take to set up AECOJOY portable crates?

Most AECOJOY portable crates feature quick-pop technology, expanding fully in 30-60 seconds without tools or assembly. Collapsing is equally quick, making them practical for frequent travelers.

Are portable dog crates TSA-approved for air travel?

Most soft-sided portable crates aren't approved for cabin travel, but many airlines accept them as checked baggage. Check your airline's specific pet transport policy. For flying with pets, hard-sided IATA-approved crates are typically required in the cabin.
