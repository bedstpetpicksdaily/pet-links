Frequently Asked Questions

Is raw dog food safe for summer feeding?

Yes, when sourced from reputable brands like Zeal Pet Food with proper handling protocols. Raw food actually offers superior digestibility and nutrient bioavailability compared to processed kibble. During summer, maintain proper storage temperatures and follow feeding guidelines to ensure food safety.

What are freeze-dried treats and why are they ideal for active dogs?

Freeze-dried treats are fresh ingredients preserved through a dehydration process that removes moisture while retaining nutritional value. They're lightweight, portable, shelf-stable, and perfect for training rewards or emergency nutrition during outdoor summer adventures with your active dog.

Why choose grain-free pet nutrition for my dog?

Grain-free formulations eliminate common allergens and fillers while maximizing protein and nutrient density. For active dogs, this means better sustained energy, improved digestion, and optimal muscle recovery—exactly what summer athletes need.

How do I transition my dog to Zeal Pet Food?

Start by gradually mixing small amounts of Zeal's raw or freeze-dried food with your dog's current diet over 7-10 days, slowly increasing the ratio. Monitor digestion and energy levels. Most dogs adapt quickly to the superior nutrition and show visible improvements within weeks.
