Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Inupi collar actually waterproof, or does it just resist splashes?

It's genuinely waterproof—IP67 rating on most models means it survives full submersion up to 1 meter for 30 minutes. That said, intentionally dunking it repeatedly will eventually degrade the seals. Light rain, pool days, and muddy play are no problem. Avoid saltwater if possible, and rinse after beach trips.

How long does the rechargeable battery actually last between charges?

User reports cluster around 7-10 days of regular use (5-10 corrections per day). Heavy daily training can drop this to 5-6 days. The charging takes 2-3 hours via USB cable. If you forget to charge and need it immediately, you're stuck—there's no quick-swap battery option like cheaper collar models offer.

Is this collar safe for puppies or small dogs under 10 pounds?

The collar itself fits dogs 8 pounds and up, but safety depends more on your training method than the hardware. Start at stimulation level 1 (very mild) and never go above level 10 for puppies or sensitive breeds. Many trainers recommend waiting until 6+ months old before introducing any shock collar, regardless of brand. Consult a certified trainer if you're unsure.
