Pet Products Are Selling Out Fast on Prime Day — Here's What You Need to Know

Prime Day is the perfect opportunity to stock up on essentials for your furry companion at steep discounts. With popular pet products flying off digital shelves, waiting until the last minute could mean missing out on the deals your pet actually needs.

Why This Matters

Pet product inventory during major sales events moves quickly, especially for bestselling items that pet owners rely on regularly. Whether it's food, toys, grooming supplies, or comfort items, the most popular products tend to sell out first. If you've been considering upgrading your pet's supplies or trying new products, Prime Day offers a rare window to save significantly while stock lasts.

What Pet Owners Should Know

  • Focus on essentials first—food, medications, and basic care items should be your priority before discretionary purchases
  • Check expiration dates carefully, especially on perishable items like treats and fresh food
  • Read customer reviews specifically from other pet owners to ensure quality and suitability for your pet
  • Consider buying slightly ahead if your pet has a favorite item with consistent high ratings
  • Prime Day deals refresh throughout the event, so check back if your first choice sells out

Bottom Line

Plan your shopping list ahead of time, prioritize essentials, and don't hesitate to purchase when you find highly-rated products at good prices—they won't stay in stock for long.

Frequently Asked Questions

What pet products are on sale during Amazon Prime Day?

Amazon Prime Day features deep discounts on popular pet products including automatic feeders, GPS pet trackers, interactive toys, orthopedic dog beds, and grooming tools. Categories like pet food, flea and tick treatments, and smart pet cameras also see some of the biggest markdowns of the year. It's one of the best times to stock up on everyday essentials or splurge on a big-ticket item your pet has needed.

Is Amazon Prime Day worth it for buying pet supplies?

Yes, Prime Day is one of the best shopping events of the year for pet owners, with discounts often ranging from 20% to 50% off on top brands like Furbo, PetSafe, Chewy-compatible items, and more. Many of the deals rival or beat Black Friday prices, especially on premium items like automatic litter boxes and GPS collars. Just make sure to compare prices beforehand using a tool like CamelCamelCamel to confirm you're getting a genuine deal.

What are the best pet deals to look for on Prime Day 2024?

Some of the hottest pet deals to watch for on Prime Day include automatic pet feeders, Roomba-style pet hair vacuums, DNA testing kits for dogs, and high-quality calming beds. Tech-forward products like the Furbo dog camera and Whistle GPS tracker historically see significant price drops during Prime Day. Setting up a deal alert on Amazon ahead of time ensures you won't miss limited-time lightning deals in the pet category.

Do you have to have Amazon Prime to get pet deals on Prime Day?

Yes, Amazon Prime Day deals are exclusively available to Amazon Prime members, so you'll need an active membership to access the discounted pet products. However, if you're not already a member, Amazon offers a free 30-day trial that you can start just before Prime Day to take full advantage of the savings. After the trial, membership costs $14.99 per month, though the pet savings alone during Prime Day can easily offset that cost.
