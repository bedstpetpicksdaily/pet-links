Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a dog crate suitable for summer?

Summer-appropriate dog crates feature open-mesh or wire construction that promotes airflow, elevated floors that prevent heat buildup, and lightweight materials that don't absorb heat. Ventilation is the most critical factor, allowing cool air circulation and preventing temperature spikes inside the crate.

Are AECOJOY dog crates good for outdoor use?

Yes, AECOJOY's outdoor pet supplies line includes UV-resistant crates designed for patios and decks. Their materials withstand sun exposure and moisture while maintaining superior ventilation. Many models feature weather-resistant components perfect for summer weather.

Can I use a summer dog crate for crate training year-round?

Absolutely. While AECOJOY's summer crates excel in warm months, their durable construction and removable pads make them suitable for year-round training. You can add insulation or blankets during cooler seasons to maintain the same crate's functionality.

How do I keep my dog cool inside a crate during summer?

Choose a ventilated crate like AECOJOY's models, place it in a shaded location, ensure proper airflow around the crate, provide access to fresh water, and never leave your dog in direct sunlight. Some owners use cooling pads or fans for additional temperature management.

What size dog crate do I need?

Your dog should stand, turn around, and lie down comfortably inside the crate. AECOJOY offers multiple sizes from small breeds to large dogs. Measure your dog's length and height to ensure proper fit, as oversized crates defeat training purposes.
