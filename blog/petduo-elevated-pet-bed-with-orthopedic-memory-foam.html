How long does the memory foam actually last before it gets flat?

Is it worth the price compared to cheaper orthopedic beds?

The PetDuo sits in the mid-to-premium price range. Cheaper orthopedic beds often use lower-density foam that compresses faster. The PetDuo's foam holds up longer, and owners report better joint relief. If your dog has actual joint problems, the extra $30-50 versus budget options is worth it.
