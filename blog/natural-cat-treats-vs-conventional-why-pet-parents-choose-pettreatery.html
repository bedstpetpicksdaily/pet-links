Understanding the Difference: Natural vs. Conventional Cat Treats

Conventional cat treats often contain artificial preservatives, colors, and flavors designed to appeal to pet owners rather than cats themselves. These products typically rely on fillers like corn, soy, and by-products that offer minimal nutritional value. While they're usually more affordable, they can contribute to digestive issues, allergies, and weight gain in cats over time.

Natural cat treats, on the other hand, prioritize real meat, whole grains, and recognizable ingredients. PetTreatery's natural pet snacks exemplify this approach, featuring real proteins as the first ingredient and avoiding artificial preservatives, colors, and flavors entirely. Each treat is crafted with your cat's nutritional needs in mind, not just shelf appeal.
