Frequently Asked Questions

How often do I need to clean an automatic pet water fountain?

It is recommended to clean your pet water fountain thoroughly at least once every one to two weeks. This includes disassembling all parts, scrubbing the bowl and pump, and rinsing the filter. Regular cleaning prevents algae growth and keeps the water safe and fresh for your pets.

Are stainless steel pet water fountains better than plastic ones?

Yes, stainless steel pet water fountains are generally considered superior to plastic models. Stainless steel is non-porous, meaning it resists bacteria, biofilm, and odor buildup far better than plastic. It is also more durable, scratch-resistant, and a great choice for pets with skin sensitivities or acne caused by plastic bowls.

Will my cat or dog actually use a water fountain?

Most cats and dogs are naturally attracted to moving water because instinct tells them that flowing water is fresher and safer than stagnant water. While some pets may need a short adjustment period of a few days, the vast majority of dogs and cats begin using a water fountain regularly once they discover it.

How long do the filters in a pet water fountain last?

Most replacement filters for automatic pet water fountains last approximately two to four weeks, depending on the number of pets using the fountain and local water quality. It is important to replace filters on schedule to ensure the water remains clean, properly filtered, and free of contaminants.
