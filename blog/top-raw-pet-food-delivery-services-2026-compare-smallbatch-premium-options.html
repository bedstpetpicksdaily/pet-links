Frequently Asked Questions

Is raw pet food safe for dogs and cats?

Yes, when sourced and handled properly. Raw diets provide bioavailable nutrients and closely mimic ancestral eating patterns. Smallbatch Pets maintains rigorous food safety protocols, and their products undergo testing to ensure safety and quality.

What's the difference between freeze-dried and frozen raw food?

Frozen raw meals are kept at cold temperatures and require thawing before feeding. Freeze-dried options remove moisture while preserving nutrients, requiring no refrigeration and offering convenient portioning—ideal for travel or busy schedules.

Can I transition my pet from kibble to raw food?

Yes, but gradual transitions work best. Start by mixing small amounts of raw food with current meals, increasing raw portions over 7-10 days. Smallbatch Pets offers lightly cooked options as a gentler transition bridge for sensitive digestive systems.
