Frequently Asked Questions

How often do I actually need to use this thing?

For peak shedding season (like July), 2-3 times per week yields noticeably fewer loose hairs in your home. During off-season, once weekly is maintenance-level grooming. Some owners with double-coated breeds do it daily during spring and fall transitions and swear by it for keeping shedding manageable.

Will my dog hate it?

Most dogs tolerate it after the first few sessions, especially if you start gently and reward with treats. Some actually seek it out because it feels good when you're working on matted or itchy areas. Anxious dogs might need more patience, but it's not painful—it's closer to an intense brush than anything uncomfortable.

Can I use this on mats and tangles?

The fine-toothed side works on mild matting, but this isn't a dematting tool. Severe mats need professional grooming or a dedicated dematting comb. Use this for prevention and maintenance on an already-brushed coat, not rescue work on neglected tangles.
