Why Grain-Free Dog Food Matters in Summer

Grain-free dog food has gained traction among pet owners who recognize that dogs are facultative carnivores with nutritional needs best met through protein-rich, whole-food diets. During summer months, when heat can affect digestion and appetite, grain-free formulations provide easily digestible nutrition that keeps dogs cool and energized without the inflammatory response some dogs experience from grains.

Many dogs struggle with grain-based kibble during hot weather—lethargy, digestive upset, and reduced appetite are common concerns. Zeal Pet Food addresses these challenges with their premium raw and freeze-dried options, which deliver concentrated nutrition in smaller portions, reducing digestive load while maximizing nutrient absorption.
