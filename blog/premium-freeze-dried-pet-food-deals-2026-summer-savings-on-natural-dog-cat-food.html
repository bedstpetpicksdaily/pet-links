Why Freeze-Dried Pet Food Matters: Understanding Premium Nutrition

Freeze-drying is a preservation method that removes moisture while retaining nutrients, enzymes, and natural flavors—making it superior to traditional kibble. Unlike heavily processed commercial pet foods, freeze-dried options maintain the nutritional integrity of raw ingredients without requiring refrigeration. This makes premium freeze-dried pet food an ideal choice for pet owners seeking natural, grain-free nutrition that supports optimal digestion, coat health, and energy levels in both dogs and cats.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is freeze-dried pet food safe for all dogs and cats?

Yes, freeze-dried pet food like Zeal's natural formulations is safe for most pets. However, consult your veterinarian before switching to a raw or grain-free diet, especially if your pet has existing health conditions. Always introduce new foods gradually over 7-10 days.

How long does freeze-dried pet food stay fresh?

Properly stored freeze-dried pet food remains fresh for 12-18 months. Keep it in a cool, dry place away from moisture and sunlight. Once a bag is opened, reseal it tightly and use within 30 days for optimal freshness.
