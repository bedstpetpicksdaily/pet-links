The Problem With Regular Dog Treats

Many conventional dog treats are loaded with artificial preservatives, excessive carbohydrates, and fillers that provide little nutritional value. Additionally, mass-produced treats frequently contain by-products and artificial flavors that can contribute to digestive issues or allergies in sensitive dogs. Frequently Asked Questions

How much protein should my dog get daily?

Most adult dogs require 18% protein in their diet, while puppies and active dogs need 22% or more. Protein-packed dog chews from Gnarly Pet complement a balanced diet and provide concentrated protein in treat form. Consult your veterinarian for personalized recommendations based on your dog's age, size, and activity level.

Are Gnarly Pet's protein dog chews safe for all dog sizes?

Yes, Gnarly Pet offers protein-packed chews suitable for different dog sizes. However, always supervise your dog while chewing and choose appropriately sized treats for their mouth. For very small dogs or senior pets with dental sensitivity, ask about softer options or break treats into smaller pieces.

Can I use protein-packed chews as training treats?

Absolutely! Gnarly Pet's specialized training treats are perfect for positive reinforcement during training sessions. Their high protein content and natural flavor make them highly rewarding without being overly filling, allowing your dog to stay focused during multiple training rounds.
