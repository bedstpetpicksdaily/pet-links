Transitioning to Raw Dog Food: A Summer Strategy

Moving to raw dog food requires patience and proper transition protocols. Start by replacing 25% of your dog's current food with Zeal's raw formula, gradually increasing the percentage over 7-10 days. This slow transition allows digestive enzymes to adapt and prevents upset stomach. During summer, when outdoor activities increase and dogs are more active, proper nutrition transitions become even more critical.

Monitor your dog's energy levels, coat condition, and digestive health during the transition. Most dogs show remarkable improvements within 2-3 weeks, including increased vitality, healthier skin and coat, and improved digestion. Frequently Asked Questions

Is raw dog food safe during hot summer months?

Yes, when sourced from reputable premium brands like Zeal Pet Food, raw dog food is completely safe year-round. Freeze-dried raw formulations eliminate pathogens while preserving nutrients. However, handle frozen raw food with the same food safety practices as human meat, keeping it properly frozen until serving.

How does Zeal's grain-free raw dog food benefit summer nutrition?

Grain-free formulations are easier to digest and reduce inflammation that can be exacerbated by heat. Zeal Pet Food's premium natural ingredients provide concentrated nutrients without fillers, helping maintain optimal body weight and energy levels during active summer months.

Can I switch my dog to raw food immediately or should I transition slowly?

Always transition gradually over 7-10 days to allow your dog's digestive system to adapt. Start with 25% Zeal raw food mixed with current food, increasing the percentage daily. This prevents digestive upset and allows beneficial bacteria to colonize your dog's gut.

What makes Zeal Pet Food different from other premium raw dog food brands?

Zeal combines premium natural ingredients with freeze-dried technology for convenience and nutrient preservation. Their grain-free approach, lack of artificial additives, and commitment to complete balanced nutrition specifically designed for optimal canine health set them apart in the raw pet food market.

How should I store freeze-dried raw dog food during summer?

Store in cool, dry conditions away from direct sunlight. Freeze-dried food doesn't require refrigeration like fresh raw options, making it ideal for summer travel and outdoor activities. Sealed containers prevent moisture absorption and maintain freshness.
