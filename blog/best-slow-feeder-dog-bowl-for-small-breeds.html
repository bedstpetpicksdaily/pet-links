What to Look For

  • Appropriately sized compartments or ridges – Small breed dogs have smaller mouths, so look for bowls with smaller obstacles and dividers. Oversized slow feeders designed for large breeds won't effectively slow down a Chihuahua or Pomeranian, and your pet may become frustrated trying to eat around them.
  • Non-slip base and durable material – Small dogs may push their bowls around during meals, especially if they're frustrated by the design.   • Dishwasher-safe construction – You'll be washing this bowl frequently, and hand-washing tiny crevices is tedious.
