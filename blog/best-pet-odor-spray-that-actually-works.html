Frequently Asked Questions

Do enzyme pet odor eliminators actually work?

Yes. Enzyme cleaners break down the proteins in pet urine, feces, and vomit at a molecular level — permanently eliminating the odor rather than just masking it. Regular cleaners only mask the smell temporarily.

Can enzyme cleaners be used on carpet?

Yes. Enzyme pet odor eliminators are safe for most carpet, upholstery, hardwood, and tile surfaces. Always test on a small hidden area first with new surfaces.

Why does my pet keep peeing in the same spot?

Pets return to spots where they can still smell previous accidents. Standard cleaners leave invisible odor traces that you can't smell but your pet can. An enzyme cleaner that fully neutralizes the odor is the only reliable solution.

Is enzyme cleaner safe around pets and kids?

Quality enzyme pet cleaners are non-toxic and safe for use around pets and children once dry. Always check the label for specific safety information.
