Understanding Cold-Pressed Dog Food vs. Traditional Kibble

Cold-pressed dog food is manufactured at temperatures below 90°F, preserving heat-sensitive vitamins, minerals, and enzymes that are destroyed during the high-heat extrusion process used to make kibble. Traditional kibble requires temperatures exceeding 300°F, which compromises nutritional integrity and reduces bioavailability—meaning your dog's body struggles to absorb and utilize the nutrients. Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed formulas maintain these crucial nutrients, delivering genuine whole-food nutrition that supports optimal digestion and nutrient absorption.

The Gut-Health Advantage: Why Digestion Matters

Your dog's gut health is the foundation for overall wellness. A healthy digestive system means better nutrient absorption, stronger immunity, shinier coat, and improved energy levels. Kibble's processing method creates heavily processed meals that can irritate sensitive digestive systems and promote harmful gut bacteria growth. Nextrition Pet's gut-healthy dog food includes natural probiotics, prebiotics, and undenatured proteins that support beneficial bacteria colonization. The cold-pressing method ensures these beneficial compounds remain active and effective, creating an environment where your dog's digestive system can thrive rather than merely survive.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is cold-pressed dog food better than kibble for all dogs?

While cold-pressed offers superior nutritional benefits, individual dogs have different needs. Most dogs benefit significantly from cold-pressed options, especially those with sensitive digestion. Consult your veterinarian about whether Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed formulas are ideal for your dog's specific health profile.

Why does Nextrition Pet cold-pressed dog food cost more than kibble?

Premium cold-pressed production is more expensive than high-volume kibble manufacturing. However, the superior ingredient quality, nutrient preservation, and digestive benefits often reduce long-term veterinary costs, making it a wise investment in your dog's health.

Can I mix Nextrition Pet cold-pressed food with regular kibble?

Yes, you can gradually transition your dog's diet by mixing Nextrition Pet cold-pressed food with their current kibble over 7-10 days. This helps prevent digestive upset while allowing your dog's system to adjust to the superior nutrition.

Do Nextrition Pet supplements work with their cold-pressed food?

Absolutely. Nextrition Pet supplements are specifically formulated to complement their cold-pressed formulas, creating a synergistic nutritional system that maximizes health benefits and addresses specific wellness concerns.

How should I store Nextrition Pet cold-pressed dog food?

Cold-pressed dog food requires refrigeration and has a shorter shelf life than kibble—typically 2-3 weeks once opened. Store in an airtight container in your refrigerator to maintain nutritional integrity and freshness.
