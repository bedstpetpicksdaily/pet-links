Frequently Asked Questions

Does the health tracking actually predict cat health problems?

It tracks weight and litter box frequency, which are legitimate health indicators. My experience showed this data was useful enough to discuss with my vet. It won't diagnose conditions, but it will alert you to unusual patterns—like sudden weight loss or increased urination—that warrant a vet visit. That's the point.

How often do you need to empty the waste compartment?

With two cats using it daily, I emptied mine every 8-10 days. A single cat household might stretch it to 12-14 days. The app sends a notification when it's full, which is helpful. Larger households should expect more frequent emptying.

Will it work with multiple cats?

Yes, and the health tracking actually becomes more valuable. Each cat's litter box visits and weight can be monitored separately if you set up individual profiles. I tested this with my two cats and the app correctly attributed usage to each one about 95% of the time.

What's the warranty coverage?

Most PetDuo models come with a 1-year manufacturer's warranty covering defects. That's standard for the price range, though some competitors offer 2 years. Check the specific Amazon listing for exact coverage since it varies by seller.
