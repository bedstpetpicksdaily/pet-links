Raw diets mirror what canines ate for thousands of years—whole proteins, healthy fats, and nutrient-dense organs.

Zeal Pet Food's freeze-dried raw formulas preserve these vital nutrients without the heat damage. Is Zeal Pet Food's raw dog food safe for all ages?

Yes, Zeal's freeze-dried raw formulas are nutritionally complete for puppies, adults, and seniors. However, consult your veterinarian before switching puppies under 8 weeks old, as their digestive systems are still developing. Zeal's premium natural ingredients support every life stage.

How does Zeal's freeze-dried raw compare to traditional raw dog food?

Zeal's freeze-dried process removes moisture while preserving nutrients, enzymes, and probiotics that heat-processing destroys. Unlike fresh raw, it requires no thawing or refrigeration, eliminating handling concerns while delivering identical nutritional benefits of raw feeding.

Can I feed Zeal Pet Food to both dogs and cats?
