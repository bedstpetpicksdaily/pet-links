Transitioning to Raw: Tips for Summer Success

Switching to Zeal Pet Food's raw diet during summer is straightforward with proper planning. Begin by gradually introducing raw food over 7-10 days, mixing it with your dog's current food to allow their digestive system to adapt. Monitor your pet for any changes in energy or digestion. Most dogs experience increased vitality, shinier coats, and improved digestion within weeks. Store Zeal's freeze-dried raw food in cool, dry conditions—easy to maintain even in warm summer climates—and always provide fresh water alongside raw meals to support optimal hydration.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is raw dog food safe for summer heat?

Yes, Zeal's freeze-dried raw food is stable in warm conditions and doesn't require refrigeration like fresh raw diets. Store in a cool, dry place and serve at room temperature with fresh water to support hydration during hot months.

How quickly will I see results after switching to Zeal Pet Food?

Most dogs show improved energy levels within 1-2 weeks and noticeable coat improvements within 4-6 weeks. Individual results vary based on the dog's age, activity level, and previous diet.

Can I mix Zeal raw food with regular kibble?

Yes, blending Zeal's raw formulations with your dog's current food is an excellent transition strategy. This allows their digestive system to adapt gradually while increasing raw nutrition intake.
