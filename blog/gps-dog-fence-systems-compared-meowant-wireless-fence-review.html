What Are GPS Dog Fence Systems?

GPS dog fence systems use satellite technology and smartphone apps to create invisible boundaries around your home or property. Unlike traditional wireless fences that rely on buried wires or radio signals with limited range, GPS systems work anywhere with cellular and GPS coverage. Your dog wears a small collar receiver, and when they approach or cross the boundary you've set on the app, they receive a warning tone followed by a gentle vibration or static correction if they continue.

These systems offer flexibility that traditional fences can't match. You can adjust boundaries in real-time, monitor your dog's location continuously, and take your fence with you when traveling. The Meowant wireless fence leverages this technology while incorporating the brand's commitment to pet comfort and ease of use that defines their entire product ecosystem.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Meowant GPS fence work in apartments or small yards?

Yes. The Meowant wireless fence is particularly useful in apartments and small yards since it doesn't require physical fence installation. You can set boundaries around your entire property or specific zones, making it ideal for any living situation with GPS coverage.

How accurate is the Meowant GPS fence boundary?

The Meowant system provides accuracy within 6-10 feet under normal conditions. Accuracy can vary based on GPS signal strength, buildings, and terrain. Urban areas with tall buildings may experience slight variations compared to open spaces.

Is the Meowant wireless fence safe for puppies?

The Meowant fence includes adjustable correction levels, starting with sound warnings. This makes it suitable for puppies and sensitive dogs. However, most trainers recommend waiting until dogs are at least 6 months old and properly trained before using any GPS fence system.

What happens if my dog loses the collar or it runs out of battery?

The Meowant app sends notifications when battery is low, giving you time to charge it. If the collar is lost, you can disable it in the app immediately. Customer support can help locate lost devices, and replacement collars are available at reasonable prices.

Can I use the Meowant fence while traveling?

Yes, one of the biggest advantages of GPS fencing is portability. You can adjust your boundary zones through the app anywhere with an internet connection, making it perfect for road trips, camping, and visits to friends' homes.
