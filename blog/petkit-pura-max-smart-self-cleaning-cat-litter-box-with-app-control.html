Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Petkit Pura Max work with multiple cats?

Yes, and this is where it genuinely shines. I have three cats, and the automatic cleaning cycles handle multiple uses within a few hours without jamming or overflow issues. The weight sensor tracks usage frequency, so you'll get app alerts before problems develop. The one caveat: if you have more than 4 cats, you might want to consider two units or stick with traditional boxes.

How often do you actually need to empty the waste drawer?

With three cats, I empty the drawer every 5-7 days. The app tells you the waste level in real-time, so you're never surprised. The drawer holds roughly two weeks of waste for a single cat, but with multiple cats, plan on weekly maintenance. This isn't passive—you still need to empty it, but it's one quick task instead of daily scooping.

Is the app connectivity reliable, or does it drop frequently?

The WiFi connection has been stable in my testing. I've experienced one disconnection in 90 days, which required a simple app restart. That said, the litter box functions perfectly without the app—automatic cleaning still runs on a timer. The app is genuinely convenient, but not essential for the device to operate, which is how it should be.
