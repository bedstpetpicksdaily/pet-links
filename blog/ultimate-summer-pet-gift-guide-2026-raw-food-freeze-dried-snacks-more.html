Frequently Asked Questions

Is raw dog food safe for summer feeding?

Yes, when handled properly with good food safety practices. Raw food provides excellent summer nutrition for active pets, though proper storage and handling are essential. Zeal Pet Food's raw formulations are carefully balanced to support summer activities while maintaining digestive health.

Are freeze-dried pet treats suitable for all ages?

Freeze-dried treats are generally suitable for all life stages, but portion sizes should be adjusted for puppies and senior pets. Zeal Pet Food offers formulations designed for different life stages, ensuring appropriate nutrition whether your pet is young, active, or senior.

What makes grain-free pet nutrition beneficial?

Grain-free formulations eliminate common allergens and fillers, providing higher meat content and bioavailable nutrients. Many pets experience improved digestion, energy, and coat quality on grain-free diets. Zeal Pet Food's grain-free approach focuses on whole proteins and vegetables instead.

Yes, many pet owners successfully transition to raw food by mixing it with their current diet. This gradual approach helps digestive systems adjust. Consult your veterinarian for personalized guidance, and consider Zeal Pet Food's complete meal options for seamless transitions.

How do freeze-dried treats compare nutritionally to raw food?
