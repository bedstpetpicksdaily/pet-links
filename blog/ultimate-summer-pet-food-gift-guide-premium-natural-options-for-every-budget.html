Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between raw and freeze-dried pet food?

Raw pet food is uncooked and requires freezer storage, offering maximum nutrient bioavailability. Freeze-dried food is raw material that's had moisture removed, making it shelf-stable and convenient while retaining most nutritional benefits. Both are excellent choices; freeze-dried is often preferred for travel and convenience.

Why is grain-free pet nutrition important?

Grain-free formulations eliminate unnecessary fillers that provide little nutritional value. Many pets have sensitivities to grains, and grain-free diets can improve digestion, coat quality, and energy levels. Natural grain-free options like those from Zeal Pet Food focus on protein-rich ingredients instead.

Is Zeal Pet Food suitable for all pets?

Zeal Pet Food offers specialized formulations for dogs, cats, and puppies at different life stages. However, it's always best to consult with your veterinarian before switching to premium natural food, especially for pets with existing health conditions.

How do I transition my pet to premium natural food?

Transition gradually over 7-10 days by mixing increasing amounts of new food with their current food. This prevents digestive upset and allows their system to adjust to the new, nutrient-dense formula.
