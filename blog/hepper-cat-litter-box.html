Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Hepper Cat Litter Box suitable for large cats?

Yes, the Hepper features a spacious interior with a generously sized entrance that accommodates most cat breeds, including larger cats and senior cats with mobility considerations.

How often do you need to clean the Hepper litter box?

Daily scooping is recommended to maintain optimal hygiene and odor control. The top-opening design makes daily maintenance straightforward, though deep cleaning requires more effort due to its enclosed structure.

Does the Hepper litter box really reduce odors?

The enclosed design and premium materials significantly reduce airborne odors compared to open litter boxes. However, regular scooping and quality litter are still essential for best results.

Can multiple cats share one Hepper litter box?

While possible, experts recommend one litter box per cat plus one extra. A single Hepper works best for one cat; multiple cats may lead to territorial issues or inadequate capacity.
