Why Raw Dog Food is Perfect for Summer

Raw dog food offers several advantages during summer months. The natural, unprocessed ingredients provide superior nutrient absorption, meaning your dog gets more nutritional value per serving. This is crucial when hot weather can suppress appetite. Raw diets are also lower in carbohydrates, which helps prevent bloating and digestive upset—common issues when dogs eat less during summer heat. Additionally, raw feeding supports lean muscle maintenance and stable energy levels, even when your pup is less active on scorching days.

Feeding Tips for Summer Success

When introducing Zeal Pet Food or any raw diet during summer, transition gradually over 7-10 days to allow digestive adjustment. Feed during cooler morning or evening hours to maintain food freshness and encourage better appetite. Monitor hydration closely, as raw diets contain moisture that supports kidney health and digestion. Serve appropriate portion sizes—typically 2-3% of your dog's body weight daily—and adjust based on activity level and individual metabolism. Store freeze-dried raw food in a cool, dry place, and always provide fresh water alongside meals.
