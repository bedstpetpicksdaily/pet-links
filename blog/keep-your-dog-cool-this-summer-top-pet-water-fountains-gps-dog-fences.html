Why Pet Water Fountains Matter in Summer Heat

Dogs naturally drink less from still water bowls, which can lead to dehydration during hot summer months. Flowing water fountains stimulate their natural drinking instincts and encourage more frequent hydration. Pet water fountains also filter water continuously, keeping it fresher and cleaner than traditional bowls. Many fountains feature multiple water levels and flow settings, making them suitable for dogs of all sizes and ages. The circulation also helps prevent bacteria buildup and keeps water at a cooler temperature throughout the day.

Quality GPS systems like Meowant's are weather-resistant and function in most conditions. However, extreme storms or dense tree coverage may occasionally affect signal strength. Always have a backup safety plan during severe weather.
