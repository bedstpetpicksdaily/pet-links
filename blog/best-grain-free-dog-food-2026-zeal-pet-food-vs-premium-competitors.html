Frequently Asked Questions

Is grain-free dog food better than grain-inclusive options?

Grain-free isn't universally superior, but it benefits dogs with grain sensitivities or allergies. Many grain-free formulas offer higher protein content and fewer fillers. Consult your veterinarian to determine if grain-free suits your dog's specific health needs.

How does freeze-dried dog food compare to raw dog food?

Freeze-dried food offers raw nutrition benefits with improved safety and convenience. It eliminates pathogens that raw food may contain while preserving enzymes and nutrients. It requires less storage space than raw and has a longer shelf life.

Why is Zeal Pet Food more expensive than other premium brands?

Zeal's pricing reflects superior New Zealand sourcing, minimal processing, and rigorous quality standards. Their freeze-dried and raw formats deliver higher nutritional density per serving than traditional kibble, making the cost-per-nutrient ratio competitive.

Can I transition my dog to Zeal Pet Food gradually?

Yes, gradually transition over 7-10 days by mixing increasing amounts of Zeal with your dog's current food. This prevents digestive upset. Start with 25% Zeal, increasing by 25% every few days until you reach 100%.

Does Zeal Pet Food offer options for cats?

Yes, Zeal produces grain-free cat food using similar premium standards as their dog food lines, including raw and freeze-dried formats designed specifically for feline nutritional requirements.
