How does the Lds2 Pro handle dog hair versus cat hair?

Dog hair typically sheds in larger clumps; the Lds2 Pro's upgraded brush handles this well but needs emptying more frequently (every 2 days in heavy-shedding breeds). Cat hair is finer and more dispersed, which the vacuum actually handles better—expect 3-4 day intervals between emptying. The transition between floor types is smooth thanks to lidar planning, so it doesn't get confused switching from kitchen hardwood to bedroom carpet.
