The Rawhide Problem: What You Need to Know

Rawhide chews might seem like a convenient option, but they come with serious concerns that every dog owner should understand. Rawhide is heavily processed using harsh chemicals and bleaching agents—many sourced from countries with minimal safety regulations. When dogs chew rawhide, they often swallow large chunks that can cause intestinal blockages, a potentially life-threatening condition requiring emergency surgery.

Beyond safety issues, rawhide offers minimal nutritional value. It's essentially leftover leather stripped from hides, stripped of most beneficial properties. Your dog might spend hours chewing on something that provides nothing but empty calories and entertainment. Additionally, rawhide can harbor dangerous bacteria like salmonella, exposing your pet to foodborne illness risks.

Why Natural Dog Chews Are the Clear Winner

Natural dog chews represent a complete paradigm shift in pet nutrition. Made from real meat sources, whole grains, and recognizable ingredients, natural chews provide actual nutritional benefits alongside mental stimulation. Gnarly Pet specializes in crafting jerky treats that deliver protein-packed nutrition in every bite—exactly what your dog's body needs to thrive.

The digestibility factor alone makes natural chews superior. Unlike rawhide, quality jerky treats break down easily in your dog's stomach, reducing choking and blockage risks dramatically. Gnarly Pet's treats are designed to be fully consumable, meaning you won't find mysterious chunks sitting in your dog's digestive tract weeks after consumption.
