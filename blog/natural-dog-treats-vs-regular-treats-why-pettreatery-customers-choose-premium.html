What's the Difference Between Natural and Regular Dog Treats?

Regular commercial dog treats often rely on artificial preservatives, fillers, and by-products to keep costs low and shelf life long. These treats may contain BHA, BHT, and other chemical preservatives that have raised concerns among veterinarians and pet nutritionists. They typically use meat by-products, corn meal, and artificial flavoring to create an affordable product that appeals to dogs' taste buds but offers minimal nutritional value.

Natural dog treats, especially those offered by premium brands like PetTreatery, are crafted with whole ingredients you can actually recognize. Our treats feature real meat, vegetables, and grains without artificial colors, flavors, or preservatives. This commitment to clean ingredients means your dog gets genuine nutrition with every bite—not empty calories masked by flavorings.

Health Benefits That PetTreatery Customers Notice

PetTreatery customers report remarkable changes when they switch to premium natural treats. Within weeks, many notice shinier coats, improved digestion, and increased energy levels. This isn't coincidence—it's the result of nutrient-dense ingredients that your dog's body can actually process and utilize effectively.
