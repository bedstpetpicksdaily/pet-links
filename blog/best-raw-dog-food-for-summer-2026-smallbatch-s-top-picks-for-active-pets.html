Transitioning Your Dog to Raw Food for Summer

If your dog is new to raw feeding, summer is an excellent time to transition. Start by mixing 25% raw food with 75% current diet, gradually increasing the raw proportion over 7-10 days. Frequently Asked Questions

Is raw dog food safe for active dogs?

Yes, raw dog food is safe and beneficial for active dogs when sourced from reputable suppliers like Smallbatch Pets. Raw diets provide bioavailable nutrients, support muscle recovery, and maintain digestive health. Always source from companies with rigorous quality standards.

How do I store Smallbatch frozen raw meals?

Smallbatch frozen raw meals should be stored in your freezer and thawed in the refrigerator before serving. Most meals thaw within 24 hours. Freeze-dried options offer longer shelf life and require no freezer space, making them convenient for travel.

Can I mix raw food with kibble?

Yes, many pet parents successfully transition by mixing raw food with their dog's current diet. Start with 25% raw and gradually increase over 7-10 days. This method helps dogs adjust to raw nutrition while monitoring digestive response.

What proteins does Smallbatch offer?

Smallbatch Pets offers rotating proteins including beef, chicken, turkey, fish, and multi-protein blends. This variety allows you to rotate proteins and find optimal nutrition for your individual dog's needs and preferences.

Is Smallbatch raw food suitable for sensitive stomachs?

Smallbatch raw food is formulated for digestive health with whole food ingredients and no fillers. For dogs with very sensitive stomachs, their lightly cooked meal option offers raw nutrition benefits with gentler digestion.
