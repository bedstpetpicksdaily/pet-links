Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I change the filter in the Catit Flower Fountain?

Catit recommends replacing the filter approximately every 4 weeks to maintain optimal water quality and ensure the triple-action filtration system continues to remove impurities, soften water, and neutralize odors effectively.

Is the Catit Flower Fountain suitable for dogs as well as cats?

Yes! While it is designed with cats in mind, the Catit Flower Fountain works well for small to medium-sized dogs too. Its 3-liter capacity and continuous water flow make it a great hydration solution for multi-pet households.

Is the Catit Flower Fountain easy to clean?

The fountain can be disassembled for cleaning, and most parts are easy to rinse under running water. For a thorough deep clean, it is recommended to fully disassemble and clean all components every 1-2 weeks to prevent algae and bacteria buildup.

Does the Catit Flower Fountain make a lot of noise?

No, the Catit Flower Fountain is equipped with a low-noise pump that operates very quietly. Most pet owners report that the sound is barely noticeable, making it suitable for placement in bedrooms, living rooms, or any quiet area of the home.
