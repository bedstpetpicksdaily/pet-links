Frequently Asked Questions

How long do AECOJOY cooling pet beds stay cool?

AECOJOY gel-infused beds provide active cooling for 4-6 hours when your dog first lies down. The cooling effect is most pronounced during the first 2-3 hours. The gel technology continues to absorb excess body heat throughout the day, keeping the bed cooler than traditional options for the entire sleeping period.

Can AECOJOY pet beds be machine washed?

Yes, most AECOJOY pet beds feature removable, machine-washable covers. Check the specific product instructions, as some models recommend cold water washing and air drying to preserve the gel cooling technology. Regular cleaning helps maintain the bed's effectiveness and extends its lifespan.

Are AECOJOY beds suitable for all dog sizes?

AECOJOY offers pet beds in multiple sizes ranging from small to extra-large. Their sizing accommodates dogs from 10 pounds to over 100 pounds. Make sure to measure your dog and check the product dimensions before purchasing to ensure proper fit and optimal cooling performance.

How does AECOJOY compare to elevated cooling beds?

AECOJOY beds offer gel-based cooling technology plus orthopedic support, making them better for dogs needing joint relief. Elevated beds like Kuranda prioritize airflow but lack memory foam cushioning. AECOJOY is the better choice if your dog needs both cooling and support; elevated beds work better for purely air-circulation needs.
