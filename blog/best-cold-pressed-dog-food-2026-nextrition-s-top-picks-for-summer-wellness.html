Frequently Asked Questions

What makes cold-pressed dog food different from regular kibble?

Cold-pressed dog food uses low-temperature processing that preserves heat-sensitive nutrients, enzymes, and probiotics. Regular kibble undergoes high-heat extrusion, destroying these beneficial compounds. Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed formulas retain up to 30% more bioavailable nutrients, improving digestion and overall wellness.

Is Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed food suitable for dogs with sensitive stomachs?

Yes. Nextrition Pet specifically formulates their cold-pressed range to support gut health and reduce digestive sensitivity. The preserved probiotics and natural ingredients are gentler on sensitive digestive systems than conventional kibble, making them ideal for dogs with dietary issues.

How should I transition my dog to cold-pressed food?

Introduce Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed food gradually over 7-10 days, mixing increasing amounts with current food. This allows your dog's gut microbiome to adjust smoothly. Most dogs transition beautifully, but the gradual approach prevents digestive upset during the switch.

Can cold-pressed dog food be stored like regular kibble?

Cold-pressed food requires cooler storage than conventional kibble due to its minimal processing. Nextrition Pet recommends refrigeration or freezing after opening, and using within the recommended timeframe. This shorter shelf life reflects the product's premium, preservative-free quality.
