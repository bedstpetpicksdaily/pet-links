What is Freeze-Dried Cat Food?

Freeze-dried cat food is a premium pet nutrition option that preserves raw ingredients through a specialized drying process. Unlike traditional kibble or canned food, freeze-drying removes moisture while maintaining the nutritional integrity and natural flavor of raw meat and organs. This process results in lightweight, shelf-stable meals that rehydrate easily and provide the nutritional benefits of raw feeding without the handling complexity.
