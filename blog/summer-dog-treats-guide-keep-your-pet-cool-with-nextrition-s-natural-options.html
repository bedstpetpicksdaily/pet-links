Why Summer Requires Special Dog Treat Considerations

During summer, dogs are more susceptible to overheating, dehydration, and digestive upset from rich or poorly-formulated treats. The heat can slow their metabolism and reduce their appetite, making it crucial to choose treats that are both nutritious and easy to digest. Dogs with sensitive stomachs or existing digestive issues are particularly vulnerable during warmer months. This is where quality matters significantly—premium, natural treats from brands like Nextrition Pet provide the nutritional support your dog needs without unnecessary additives that could aggravate their system.
