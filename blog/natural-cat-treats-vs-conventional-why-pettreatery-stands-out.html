Many conventional treats lack the essential nutrients cats need, serving as empty calories that contribute to weight gain and dental issues.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are natural cat treats more expensive than conventional ones?

Yes, natural treats typically cost more due to higher-quality ingredient sourcing and production standards. However, PetTreatery's premium cat treats offer better nutritional density, meaning your cat stays satisfied longer, potentially reducing overall treat consumption and providing better value long-term.

Can switching to natural treats cause digestive issues?

Most cats transition smoothly to natural treats without issues. However, if your cat has a sensitive stomach, introduce PetTreatery's natural snacks gradually alongside their regular diet to allow their digestive system to adjust over 7-10 days.

How long do PetTreatery's natural cat treats stay fresh?

Without artificial preservatives, natural treats have a shorter shelf life than conventional options. PetTreatery's cat treats maintain optimal freshness for several months when stored in a cool, dry place. Always check packaging for specific expiration dates.

What ingredients should I avoid in conventional cat treats?

Avoid treats containing artificial colors (like Red #40 or Yellow #5), artificial preservatives (BHA, BHT, ethoxyquin), meat by-products, corn meal fillers, and excessive salt or sugar. PetTreatery eliminates all these concerning ingredients in favor of wholesome, recognizable foods.
