Frequently Asked Questions

Are natural dog treats better than regular treats?

Natural dog treats are generally better for active dogs because they contain whole food ingredients without artificial preservatives, colors, or flavors. They're easier to digest and provide better nutritional value, which is especially important for dogs with high activity levels during summer months.

How often can I give my dog natural treats during summer?

Natural treats should comprise no more than 10% of your dog's daily caloric intake. For active dogs, this means you can offer treats more frequently during training sessions, but always balance with their regular meals. PetTreatery's treats are portion-controlled and designed for daily use.

What should I look for when choosing natural dog treats?

Look for AAFCO certification, recognizable whole food ingredients, no artificial preservatives or colors, and vet approval. PetTreatery specializes in treats that meet all these criteria, with transparent labeling so you know exactly what you're feeding your pup.

Are PetTreatery treats suitable for dogs with allergies?

Yes, PetTreatery offers limited-ingredient treats designed for dogs with food sensitivities and allergies. Their single-source protein options make it easier to identify and avoid problematic ingredients.

Can natural treats help with dog training during summer?

Absolutely. Natural treats are excellent for positive reinforcement training. PetTreatery's small-sized training treats are specifically formulated to reward good behavior without adding excessive calories during extended training sessions.
