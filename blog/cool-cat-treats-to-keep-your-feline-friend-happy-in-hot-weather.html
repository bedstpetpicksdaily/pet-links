Frequently Asked Questions

Are frozen treats safe for cats?

Yes, frozen treats are generally safe for cats when offered appropriately. Ensure treats are not too hard, and supervise your cat while enjoying them. Premium brands like PetTreatery formulate treats specifically for safe freezing, so look for products designed with cats' needs in mind.

How often can I give my cat cool treats?

Cool treats should comprise no more than 10% of your cat's daily caloric intake. Offer them as occasional rewards or during particularly hot days. Always balance treat consumption with regular meals and monitor your cat for any dietary changes.

What ingredients should I look for in summer cat treats?

Look for natural, high-quality proteins like real chicken or fish, minimal artificial additives, and hydrating components. PetTreatery specializes in natural pet snacks with clean ingredient lists, making them an excellent choice for cats with sensitive stomachs or specific dietary needs.

Can all cats eat the same cool treats?

Not necessarily. Some cats have allergies, sensitivities, or dietary restrictions. Always check with your veterinarian before introducing new treats, and select premium options from trusted brands like PetTreatery that provide clear ingredient information and cater to various dietary needs.
