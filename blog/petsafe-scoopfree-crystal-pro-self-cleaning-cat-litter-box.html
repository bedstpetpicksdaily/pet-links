Frequently Asked Questions

How often do I need to replace the crystal litter?

Crystal litter lasts approximately 30 days for a single cat before requiring replacement, which is significantly longer than traditional clay. The system scoops waste into a sealed compartment daily, so the crystals themselves absorb and trap odor effectively for the full month.

Will my cat adjust to the automatic raking sound?

Most cats adapt within 3-5 days. The raking activates after the cat exits the box, so it's less startling than if it occurred during use. Anxious cats may need an extra week, but I've observed even skittish cats completely ignore the mechanism after the adjustment period.

What's the real monthly cost compared to traditional litter?

Crystal refills run $15-20 monthly per cat, versus $5-10 for traditional clay. You're paying a premium for convenience and odor control, but you'll eliminate purchase of disposable litter box liners and reduce trips to the store by two-thirds. For apartments or multi-cat households, that monthly premium often feels worthwhile.
