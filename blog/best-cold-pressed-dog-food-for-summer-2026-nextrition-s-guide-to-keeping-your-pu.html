Why Cold-Pressed Dog Food is Perfect for Summer

Cold-pressed dog food is manufactured at low temperatures, preserving the nutritional integrity and natural enzymes that heat-based processing destroys. This gentle method means more bioavailable nutrients, easier digestion, and better absorption—all crucial factors when your dog's body is working harder to regulate temperature in summer heat. Cold-pressed kibble is also less likely to trigger digestive upset, keeping your pup's gut microbiome balanced and healthy throughout the season.

Unlike traditional kibble that relies on high-heat extrusion, cold-pressed formulas maintain the delicate balance of probiotics and prebiotics essential for optimal digestive health. Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed dog food exemplifies this approach, featuring whole meat proteins, organic vegetables, and carefully selected supplements that work synergistically to support immune function and energy levels when your dog needs it most.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between cold-pressed and traditional dog kibble?

Cold-pressed dog food uses low-temperature processing that preserves natural enzymes, probiotics, and nutrients, resulting in better digestibility and bioavailability. Traditional kibble uses high-heat extrusion, which can destroy beneficial compounds. Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed formula maintains nutritional integrity for superior gut health.

Why do dogs need different nutrition in summer?

Summer heat increases your dog's metabolic demands and can suppress appetite while stressing the digestive system. Cold-pressed food with high digestibility and gut-healthy ingredients like probiotics helps your dog absorb maximum nutrition despite eating less, maintaining energy and health through hot months.
