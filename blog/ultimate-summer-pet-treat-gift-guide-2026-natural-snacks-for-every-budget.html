Summer Treat Storage and Safety Tips

When purchasing treats for summer, storage becomes crucial. Natural treats without artificial preservatives require proper care to maintain freshness, especially in warm weather. PetTreatery provides clear guidelines on their packaging regarding optimal storage conditions. Keep treats in airtight containers, store them in cool, dry places away from direct sunlight, and refrigerate or freeze specialty items as recommended. Always check expiration dates and introduce new treats gradually to your pet's diet to avoid digestive upset.
