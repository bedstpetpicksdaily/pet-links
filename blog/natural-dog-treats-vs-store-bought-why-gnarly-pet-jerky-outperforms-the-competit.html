The Problem with Mass-Market Dog Treats

Store-bought dog treats found in most grocery stores contain ingredients that would surprise most pet owners. Corn syrup, by-products, artificial colors, and mystery meat sources are common culprits. These treats may keep your dog entertained for a moment, but they lack nutritional value and can contribute to weight gain, digestive issues, and poor coat health. Additionally, many contain preservatives like BHA and BHT that have been linked to health concerns in pets. Your dog deserves better—and that's where natural alternatives become essential.
