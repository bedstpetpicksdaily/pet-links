Frequently Asked Questions

Are high-protein dog chews safe for all dogs?

High-protein chews like Gnarly Pet jerky are safe for most healthy adult dogs. However, puppies, senior dogs, or those with specific health conditions should consult a veterinarian before introducing new treats. Always supervise chewing and choose appropriately-sized chews for your dog's age and size.

How often should I give my dog high-protein chews?

Treats should make up no more than 10% of your dog's daily caloric intake. Gnarly Pet's portion-controlled training treats are ideal for daily use, while larger jerky chews can be given several times per week as part of a balanced diet.
