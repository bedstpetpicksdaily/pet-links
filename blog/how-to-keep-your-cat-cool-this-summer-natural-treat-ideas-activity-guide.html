Indoor Activities to Keep Cats Cool and Entertained

During summer, outdoor time should be limited to early morning or evening hours when temperatures are cooler. Instead, focus on creating engaging indoor activities that don't generate excess body heat. Interactive toys, puzzle feeders, and window perches with shade are excellent options. You can hide PetTreatery's premium cat treats around your home to encourage natural hunting behaviors indoors, turning treat time into an enriching activity.

Water-based play is another fantastic summer activity. Many cats enjoy batting at water fountains or participating in supervised play with water toys.
