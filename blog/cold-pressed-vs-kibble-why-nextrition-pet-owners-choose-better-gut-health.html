What Makes Cold-Pressed Dog Food Different?

Cold-pressed dog food is manufactured using a low-temperature pressing process that preserves the nutritional integrity of raw ingredients. Unlike traditional kibble, which is baked at extremely high temperatures (often exceeding 300°F), cold-pressed formulas maintain the natural enzymes, vitamins, and probiotics that support digestive health. Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed recipes are crafted with carefully selected, whole-food ingredients that your dog's digestive system can actually recognize and process efficiently.

The cold-pressing method also eliminates the need for excessive binders and fillers commonly found in kibble. This means fewer artificial additives, better ingredient quality, and a formula that closely mimics what dogs would naturally consume in the wild. When you choose Nextrition Pet, you're investing in nutrition that prioritizes your dog's gut microbiome from the ground up.

Kibble's Hidden Impact on Canine Digestion

Traditional kibble undergoes high-heat processing that can destroy beneficial enzymes and heat-sensitive nutrients. This processing method also requires synthetic vitamin supplementation to compensate for nutrient loss—something Nextrition Pet avoids through its gentle cold-pressing technique. Additionally, kibble often contains grain fillers, by-products, and artificial preservatives that can trigger digestive issues, allergies, and inflammation in sensitive dogs.

Many dogs fed exclusively on kibble experience bloating, inconsistent stools, and poor nutrient absorption. The hard, processed nature of kibble can also put strain on your dog's digestive system, requiring more effort to break down compared to the nutrient-dense, digestible formula found in Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed dog food.

Why Gut Health Matters for Your Dog

A healthy gut is the foundation of overall wellness in dogs. Approximately 70% of your dog's immune system is located in their digestive tract, making gut health essential for fighting illness, maintaining a healthy weight, and supporting cognitive function. Cold-pressed nutrition from Nextrition Pet promotes a balanced microbiome, reduces inflammation, and ensures optimal nutrient absorption.

Dogs on a Nextrition Pet cold-pressed diet often show visible improvements within weeks: better coat quality, increased energy, healthier skin, and most importantly, consistent, healthy digestion. By supporting your dog's gut, you're investing in long-term vitality and disease prevention.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is cold-pressed dog food better for sensitive stomachs?

Yes. Cold-pressed dog food like Nextrition Pet's formulas is significantly easier to digest than kibble because it preserves natural enzymes and avoids the inflammatory effects of high-heat processing. Dogs with sensitive stomachs typically show improvement within 2-4 weeks of switching to cold-pressed nutrition.

How long does Nextrition Pet cold-pressed dog food stay fresh?

Nextrition Pet cold-pressed dog food typically has a shelf life of 12-18 months unopened when stored in a cool, dry place. Once opened, it should be refrigerated and used within 3-4 weeks for optimal freshness and nutritional value.

Can I mix cold-pressed food with kibble?

Yes, you can gradually transition your dog from kibble to cold-pressed by mixing the two together over 7-10 days. This helps their digestive system adjust to the new food. However, for maximum gut health benefits, Nextrition Pet recommends feeding cold-pressed as the primary diet.

Does cold-pressed dog food help with allergies and itching?

Many dogs experience reduced allergies and skin issues after switching to cold-pressed nutrition. Nextrition Pet's natural, whole-food ingredients eliminate common allergens and inflammatory fillers found in kibble, often leading to clearer skin and healthier coats.
