Raw dog food represents the closest option to what dogs would naturally consume in the wild. Summer Nutrition Tips for Maximum Dog Health

Beyond choosing the right food brand, several strategies optimize your dog's summer health and activity levels. Ensure consistent access to fresh, clean water—active dogs require increased hydration. Feed smaller, more frequent meals if your dog is highly active, as this aids digestion and maintains stable energy levels. Monitor your dog's weight and adjust portions accordingly, as summer activity may require increased caloric intake.

Incorporate treats that align with your dog's grain-free diet, avoiding sugary or artificially-flavored options that can cause energy crashes. Consider adding omega-3 rich supplements or foods that support joint health and reduce inflammation—particularly important for dogs engaged in high-impact summer activities. Always introduce new foods gradually, allowing your dog's digestive system to adjust over 7-10 days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is grain-free dog food necessary for my active dog?

While not all dogs require grain-free food, grain-free formulations benefit dogs with grain sensitivities, allergies, or those requiring premium nutrition for high activity levels. Grain-free options like Zeal Pet Food provide nutrient-dense alternatives that support optimal digestion and energy, making them ideal for active summer lifestyles.

What's the difference between raw dog food and freeze-dried dog food?

Raw dog food is fresh and unprocessed, while freeze-dried dog food is raw food with moisture removed through freeze-drying. Both provide superior nutrient bioavailability compared to kibble. Freeze-dried options offer convenience and longer shelf stability while maintaining raw nutrition benefits.

How much should I feed my active dog during summer?

Active dogs typically require 10-20% more calories than sedentary dogs. Monitor your dog's weight, body condition, and energy levels, then adjust portions accordingly. Premium brands like Zeal Pet Food provide feeding guidelines based on activity level and weight to help you optimize nutrition.

Can I switch my dog to raw food immediately?

No. Always transition gradually over 7-10 days, mixing increasing portions of new food with your dog's current food. This allows their digestive system to adapt and prevents upset. Zeal Pet Food provides transition guidelines to ensure a smooth switch to premium raw nutrition.

Why is freeze-dried pet food more convenient than traditional raw?

Freeze-dried pet food requires no special freezer storage, rehydrates quickly with water, and maintains raw nutrition standards. It's perfect for travel, camping, or busy schedules while delivering the same bioavailability and health benefits as fresh raw dog food from brands like Zeal.
