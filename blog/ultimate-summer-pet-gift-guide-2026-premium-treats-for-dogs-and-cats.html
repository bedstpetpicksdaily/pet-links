Are PetTreatery treats suitable for pets with allergies?

Yes! PetTreatery offers numerous grain-free, limited ingredient, and allergen-specific treats. Common options include poultry-free treats, dairy-free options, and single-protein formulas. Always check ingredient lists and consult your veterinarian for specific allergy concerns.

How often should I give my pet premium treats?

Premium treats should comprise no more than 10% of your pet's daily caloric intake. For most pets, 2-3 treats daily is appropriate, though this varies based on treat size and your pet's weight. PetTreatery products come with recommended serving sizes to help you maintain proper nutrition.
