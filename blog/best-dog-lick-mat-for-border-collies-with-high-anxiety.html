What Is the Best Dog Lick Mat for Border Collies with High Anxiety?

A dog lick mat is a textured silicone or rubber mat designed to hold spreadable treats like peanut butter, plain yogurt, pumpkin puree, or wet food. Your dog licks the surface to get to the treat, and that repetitive licking motion actually triggers the release of calming hormones in the brain — specifically serotonin and endorphins. It sounds almost too simple, but the science behind it is genuinely solid, and vets and animal behaviorists have been recommending lick mats for anxious dogs for years.

For Border Collies specifically, a lick mat works on multiple levels. These dogs have incredibly active minds that need constant stimulation, and a well-loaded lick mat gives them a focused mental task to complete. The best lick mats for high-anxiety Border Collies feature deep grooves, suction cup bases to prevent sliding, and durable food-safe materials that can handle an enthusiastic working dog's licking power. Freezing the mat overnight takes the calming session from a few minutes to 20 or 30 minutes of blissful, focused quiet.

Frequently Asked Questions

What do you put on a dog lick mat?

Peanut butter (xylitol-free), plain yogurt, pumpkin puree, mashed banana, wet dog food, or cream cheese all work great. Freeze it for a longer-lasting challenge.

Do lick mats help with dog anxiety?

Yes. Licking is naturally calming for dogs — it releases endorphins and reduces cortisol. Lick mats are widely recommended by veterinarians for bath anxiety, vet visits, and separation anxiety.

How do you clean a dog lick mat?

Most lick mats are dishwasher safe or can be rinsed under warm water with dish soap. Silicone mats are the easiest to clean and most durable.

Can puppies use lick mats?

Yes, lick mats are safe for puppies. Use soft fillings like plain yogurt or mashed sweet potato and supervise use until they're comfortable with it.
