What Is the Automatic Pet Feeder with Camera?

An automatic pet feeder with camera is exactly what it sounds like — a smart device that dispenses scheduled meals for your dog while also letting you keep a live eye on them through a built-in HD camera. Most models connect to your home Wi-Fi and pair with a smartphone app, so you can monitor feeding times, adjust portion sizes, and even trigger a manual meal drop no matter where you are in the world.

The best models on the market in 2026 go a step further with two-way audio, meaning you can actually talk to your dog through the feeder's speaker. Some units include motion detection alerts, night vision, and even treat-tossing features. It's part feeding station, part security camera, and part bonding tool — all rolled into one countertop device.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use an automatic pet feeder with camera when I'm away from home?

Yes, most automatic pet feeders with cameras connect to your home Wi-Fi and come with a companion app that lets you monitor, speak to, and feed your pet from anywhere in the world. You can check in via live video, dispense a meal on demand, or stick to a pre-set schedule even when you're traveling or at work. Some models also send push notifications when your pet eats or when the food hopper is running low.

How accurate are automatic pet feeders with cameras at dispensing the right portion size?

Most automatic pet feeders with cameras use rotating drums or conveyor mechanisms that are fairly accurate for dry kibble, typically dispensing within 5-10% of the target portion. Portion accuracy can vary depending on the kibble size and shape, so it's best to test and calibrate your feeder with your specific pet food before relying on it fully. Irregular or oddly shaped kibble may cause jamming or inconsistent portions in some lower-end models.

What is the best automatic pet feeder with camera for multiple pets?

For multi-pet households, feeders like the PETLIBRO Granary or the PetSafe Smart Feed work well because they have large-capacity hoppers and support multiple scheduled feedings throughout the day. However, if your pets eat different diets or one tends to steal the other's food, you may need separate feeders for each pet to ensure proper portion control. Some advanced models pair with RFID microchip readers to open only for the correct pet, making them ideal for homes with multiple animals.

Do automatic pet feeders with cameras work if the Wi-Fi goes out?

Most automatic pet feeders with cameras will continue to dispense food on their pre-programmed schedule even if the Wi-Fi connection drops, as the schedule is stored locally on the device. However, you will lose access to the live camera feed, remote feeding, and app notifications until the connection is restored. It's a good idea to set up a regular feeding schedule in advance as a backup so your pet is never left without food during an outage.
