What Is the Dog Lick Mat for Anxiety Relief?

A dog lick mat is a flat, textured silicone or rubber mat designed to hold spreadable treats like peanut butter, plain yogurt, pumpkin puree, or soft wet food. The grooves and patterns on the mat's surface encourage your dog to lick repeatedly, which activates a natural calming response in their brain. It's not a trick — the repetitive licking motion actually releases endorphins and promotes a sense of relaxation, similar to how some people find comfort in repetitive, focused tasks.

The anxiety-relief aspect comes from redirecting your dog's nervous energy into something productive and soothing. Instead of pacing, barking, or chewing up your favorite throw pillow, your dog is completely absorbed in working every last bit of peanut butter out of those little grooves. Many lick mats are also freezer-safe, which means you can prep them in advance and extend the calming session even longer — a total game changer for longer events like fireworks or vet visits.

Frequently Asked Questions

What do you put on a dog lick mat?

Peanut butter (xylitol-free), plain yogurt, pumpkin puree, mashed banana, wet dog food, or cream cheese all work great. Freeze it for a longer-lasting challenge.

Do lick mats help with dog anxiety?

Yes. Licking is naturally calming for dogs — it releases endorphins and reduces cortisol. Lick mats are widely recommended by veterinarians for bath anxiety, vet visits, and separation anxiety.

How do you clean a dog lick mat?

Most lick mats are dishwasher safe or can be rinsed under warm water with dish soap. Silicone mats are the easiest to clean and most durable.

Can puppies use lick mats?

Yes, lick mats are safe for puppies. Use soft fillings like plain yogurt or mashed sweet potato and supervise use until they're comfortable with it.
