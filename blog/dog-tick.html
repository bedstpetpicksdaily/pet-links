What to Look For

1. Prevention Method Type

  • Oral medications: Systemic protection, convenient, good for dogs that swim frequently

2. Speed of Action

Check how quickly the product kills ticks—ideally within 30 minutes to 2 hours. Faster action means less time for ticks to attach and transmit disease. This is especially critical if your dog spends time in high-risk areas like wooded trails or tall grass.
