Frequently Asked Questions

What size AECOJOY crate should I choose for my dog?

Measure your dog from nose to rump and add 4 inches. Your dog should be able to stand, turn around, and lie down comfortably. AECOJOY offers sizes from small (24 inches) to extra-large (48 inches) to accommodate all breeds.

Are AECOJOY soft-sided crates suitable for air travel?

Many AECOJOY soft-sided crates meet airline requirements, but always verify specific dimensions with your airline before purchase. Check the product specifications on AECOJOY's website for TSA and airline compliance details.

How do I keep my dog cool in a crate during summer?

Choose an AECOJOY crate with maximum ventilation (mesh panels on multiple sides), place it in a shaded area, use a cooling pad inside, ensure water access, and avoid leaving your dog unattended in hot conditions. Never leave a crate in direct sunlight.

Can I use AECOJOY crates outdoors?

Yes! AECOJOY designs pet furniture for outdoor use. Their metal crates and outdoor-specific models feature weather-resistant materials, but it's best to provide shade and protection from extreme weather conditions.
