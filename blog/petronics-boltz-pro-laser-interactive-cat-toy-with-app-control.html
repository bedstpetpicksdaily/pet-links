Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Boltz Pro safe for cats' eyes?

Yes, the laser is FDA-compliant and designed specifically for pet safety. The 15-minute auto-shutoff is the real game-changer here—it prevents your cat from obsessing for hours and eliminates the eye strain risk from prolonged exposure. I'd still avoid shining it directly in your cat's eyes intentionally, but the app-controlled patterns naturally distribute the light in ways a handheld pointer doesn't, making this safer than traditional options.

Will my cat actually play with it unsupervised?

It depends on your cat's play style. My hyperactive tabby will chase patterns unsupervised without hesitation. My cautious rescue cat preferred having me nearby at first, but eventually warmed up to the automated patterns. The random mode seems to trigger hunting instincts better than the scheduled patterns. I'd suggest testing it with supervision first to see how your cat responds before relying on it as a solo entertainment tool.

How does app control compare to the physical buttons?

The physical buttons work fine for basic start/stop, but the app gives you granular control over play patterns, intensity levels, and scheduling. The real advantage is triggering play from anywhere—I've started sessions during my lunch break and watched my cat engage for 10 minutes straight. That said, the app does require your phone and a decent WiFi connection, so the physical buttons are a solid backup when you just want quick, hands-on control.

Is this worth it compared to a regular laser pointer?

Regular laser pointers cost $5-10 and require your active participation. The Boltz Pro costs 3-5 times more but eliminates the arm fatigue factor and offers autonomous play when you're not home. July is actually a great time to invest—many cat owners realize their indoor cats are bored during hot summer months when they're stuck inside. If enrichment is already part of your budget, the Boltz Pro's convenience and safety features make it a smarter choice than traditional pointers.
