Frequently Asked Questions

Are natural dog treats better for active dogs?

Yes, natural dog treats contain higher-quality proteins and nutrients without artificial additives, making them ideal for active dogs. They provide sustained energy, support muscle recovery, and are easier on the digestive system than conventional treats.

How often can I give my active dog treats during summer?

Treats should comprise no more than 10% of your dog's daily caloric intake. For active dogs, you can offer treats more frequently as training rewards, but adjust main meals accordingly. PetTreatery's portion-controlled options make this easy to manage.

What ingredients should I look for in natural dog treats?

Look for treats with real meat (chicken, beef, fish) as the first ingredient, whole grains or grain-free options, and beneficial additions like sweet potato, pumpkin, or omega fatty acids. Avoid treats with artificial colors, flavors, or preservatives. PetTreatery's treats meet all these criteria.

Can natural dog treats help with summer training?

Absolutely. High-value natural treats are excellent for positive reinforcement training. PetTreatery's premium treats motivate dogs during training sessions while providing nutritional benefits that support active learning and performance.

How should I store natural dog treats for summer outings?

Keep natural treats in cool, dry environments. PetTreatery's packaging is designed for portability and protection. For longer outings, store treats in an insulated treat pouch or pack them in a cooler to maintain freshness.
