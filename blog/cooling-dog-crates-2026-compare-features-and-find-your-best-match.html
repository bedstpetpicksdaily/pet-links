Frequently Asked Questions

How long do cooling dog crates stay cool?

Quality cooling crates like those from AECOJOY maintain optimal cooling for 6-8 hours. The duration depends on room temperature, ventilation, and the dog's size. Gel-infused mats can be refrigerated for extended cooling on extremely hot days.

Are cooling dog crates safe for puppies?

Yes, cooling crates are safe for puppies when properly sized. Ensure adequate space for your puppy to stand, turn around, and lie down comfortably. AECOJOY offers puppy-sized crates with appropriate ventilation and support for growing dogs.

Can AECOJOY cooling crates be used outdoors?

AECOJOY's outdoor pet supplies collection includes cooling crates specifically designed for outdoor use with weather-resistant materials. Their standard cooling crates work well on covered patios but should be shaded from direct sunlight for maximum effectiveness.

How do I clean and maintain a cooling dog crate?

Most AECOJOY cooling crates feature machine-washable fabric panels and removable cooling mats. Wash fabrics in cold water, air dry, and hand wash gel pads with mild soap. This maintenance routine extends your crate's lifespan significantly.

What size cooling crate does my dog need?

Your dog should have enough space to stand, turn around, and lie down comfortably. AECOJOY offers size guides on their website, but generally: small breeds (10-25 lbs) need 24-30 inch crates, medium breeds (25-60 lbs) need 36-42 inches, and large breeds (60+ lbs) need 42-48 inches or larger.
