Frequently Asked Questions

Is grain-free dog food necessary for all dogs?

While not all dogs require grain-free food, many benefit significantly from eliminating grains—especially those with sensitivities, allergies, or digestive issues. Grain-free formulas like Zeal Pet Food are particularly beneficial for dogs with inflammatory conditions or skin problems.

How does Zeal's freeze-dried process differ from traditional raw feeding?

Zeal's freeze-drying removes moisture while preserving nutrients and enzymes, eliminating pathogens without heat processing. This makes raw nutrition safe and convenient—no freezer storage required—while maintaining the bioavailability advantages of raw feeding.

What makes Zeal Pet Food more expensive than competitors?

Zeal's premium pricing reflects superior sourcing (grass-fed, wild-caught), advanced freeze-drying technology, smaller batch production, ingredient transparency, and strict quality control. You're investing in genuine premium nutrition, not marketing hype.

Can I transition my dog directly to Zeal Pet Food?

While Zeal's nutrient density allows faster transitions than some foods, we recommend a 7-10 day gradual transition, mixing increasing portions of Zeal with your dog's current food to avoid digestive upset.

Does Zeal Pet Food work for puppies and senior dogs?
