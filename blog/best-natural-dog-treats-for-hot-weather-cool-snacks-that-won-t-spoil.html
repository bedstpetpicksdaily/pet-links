What Makes a Dog Treat Ideal for Hot Weather?

When temperatures rise, your dog's treat preferences and nutritional needs change. The best summer treats should be easy to digest, naturally hydrating, and resistant to melting or spoiling quickly. Look for treats that are low in fat, free from artificial preservatives, and made from wholesome ingredients that won't upset your dog's stomach during warm months. PetTreatery's premium natural dog treats are specially formulated with these considerations in mind, ensuring your pup gets safe, nutritious snacks year-round.

Dehydrated and Air-Dried Options

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I leave natural dog treats out in hot weather?

It depends on the type. Frozen treats should be served immediately, dehydrated treats can sit out briefly, but refrigerated options should not be left unattended in heat. Always check PetTreatery's packaging instructions for specific storage guidelines.

Are frozen treats safe for dogs during summer?

Yes, frozen treats are generally safe and beneficial for dogs in hot weather. They help cool your dog down and satisfy their chewing instinct. However, avoid giving extremely hard frozen treats that could damage teeth. PetTreatery's frozen options are designed with your dog's safety in mind.

How often can I give my dog natural treats in summer?

Natural treats should make up no more than 10% of your dog's daily caloric intake. During summer, you may increase frequency slightly for hydration purposes, but always monitor your dog's overall diet. PetTreatery provides portion guidelines on each product.

What ingredients should I avoid in summer dog treats?

Avoid treats high in fat, artificial preservatives, excessive salt, and xylitol (which is toxic to dogs). PetTreatery specializes in clean-label treats with natural ingredients, making it easy to know exactly what your dog is consuming.
