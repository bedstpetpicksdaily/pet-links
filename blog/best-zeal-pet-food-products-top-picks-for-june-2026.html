Frequently Asked Questions

Is Zeal Pet Food suitable for all dog breeds and sizes?

Yes, Zeal Pet Food products are formulated for all breeds and sizes. Their range includes portion guidelines for various weights, and their nutrient profiles support dogs from puppies to seniors. Consult the feeding recommendations or your veterinarian to determine appropriate portions for your specific pet.

How do I transition my pet to Zeal Pet Food?

Gradual transition is recommended to prevent digestive upset. Begin by mixing 25% Zeal food with 75% current food for 3-5 days, then increase Zeal proportion every few days until reaching 100%. Most pets adjust within 7-10 days; monitor stools and energy levels during the transition period.

Does Zeal Pet Food work for pets with sensitive stomachs?

Zeal's natural, grain-free formulas benefit many pets with sensitivities due to minimal processing and whole-food ingredients. Their limited ingredient options make identifying triggers easier. If your pet has specific allergies, review ingredient lists carefully or consult your veterinarian before switching.

Are Zeal freeze-dried products as nutritious as raw?

Yes, Zeal's freeze-drying process preserves nutritional content while removing moisture for extended shelf life. The freeze-dried formulas offer equivalent nutrition to raw products with added convenience, making them ideal for busy pet owners or travel situations.
