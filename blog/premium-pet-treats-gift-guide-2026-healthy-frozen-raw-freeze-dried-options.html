Frequently Asked Questions

Are frozen raw pet treats safe?

Yes, when sourced from reputable manufacturers like Smallbatch Pets that follow strict safety protocols and testing standards. Raw feeding mimics a pet's natural diet and supports optimal health when handled properly with correct storage and thawing practices.

Can I switch my pet to raw food treats immediately?

Most pets benefit from a gradual transition. Start by mixing raw treats with current food, gradually increasing the ratio over 7-10 days. Smallbatch Pets' lightly cooked options provide an excellent intermediate step for sensitive pets.

How should I store frozen raw pet meals?

Keep frozen raw meals in a dedicated freezer at 0°F or below. Most products thaw in the refrigerator overnight or can be served partially frozen. Never thaw at room temperature to prevent bacterial growth.

Are freeze-dried treats appropriate for all ages?

Freeze-dried treats work well for most pets, but puppies and senior pets with dental issues may need softer options. Consult your veterinarian if your pet has specific health conditions before introducing new treat types.

What's the shelf life of freeze-dried pet treats?

Freeze-dried treats typically maintain quality for 1-2 years when stored in cool, dry conditions away from direct sunlight. Always check manufacturer guidelines for specific products.
