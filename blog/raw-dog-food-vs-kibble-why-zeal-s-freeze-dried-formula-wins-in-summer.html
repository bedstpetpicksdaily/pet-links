Raw Dog Food vs. Kibble: The Summer Problem

Raw dog food diets have gained popularity among health-conscious pet owners who want to provide grain-free pet nutrition and minimize processed ingredients. However, raw meat requires refrigeration and freezer space, making it impractical during family vacations and hot weather. Traditional kibble, while convenient, often contains fillers, artificial preservatives, and loses nutritional potency when exposed to heat and humidity.
