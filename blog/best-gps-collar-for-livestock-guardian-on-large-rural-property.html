What to Look For

  • Real-time GPS with extended range: Your guardian may work 5+ miles from the barn.   • Multi-day battery life and rugged durability: Livestock guardians are working dogs—they swim in stock tanks, navigate thorny brush, and work through weather. Your collar needs 5+ days of battery between charges and an IP67+ waterproof rating with reinforced attachment points that won't snap under a 150-pound dog's movement.
