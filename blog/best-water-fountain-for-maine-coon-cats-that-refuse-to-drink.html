What to Look For

  • Large Capacity and Deep Basins: Maine Coons are big cats with big needs. Look for fountains that hold at least 100 ounces of water and have wide, deep bowls so their large muzzles can drink comfortably without their whiskers touching the sides—a common reason these cats refuse to drink.
  • Strong, Consistent Water Flow: Maine Coons are attracted to moving water, especially cats with low thirst drives. Fountains with multiple flow settings or designs that create visible streams and cascades are far more likely to catch their attention than still water ever will.
  • Heavy-Duty, Tip-Proof Construction: Maine Coons are powerful, playful cats that weigh 10-25 pounds. Your fountain needs to be sturdy enough to withstand their curiosity and strength—lightweight or flimsy designs will be knocked over or damaged, defeating the purpose entirely.
