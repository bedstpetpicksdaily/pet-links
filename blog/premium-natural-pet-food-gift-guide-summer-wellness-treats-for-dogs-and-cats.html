Frequently Asked Questions

Is raw dog food safe for summer feeding?

Yes, raw dog food is safe year-round when handled properly with appropriate food storage and hygiene practices. Summer actually benefits from raw food's high moisture content, supporting hydration. Always thaw frozen raw food in refrigeration and serve fresh portions to maintain quality and safety.

What makes grain-free pet food better for summer wellness?

Grain-free formulations reduce inflammatory responses that can be exacerbated by heat, support stable blood sugar and consistent energy, and often improve digestive efficiency—meaning better nutrient absorption and less digestive stress during active summer months.

Are freeze-dried pet treats convenient for travel?

Absolutely. Freeze-dried treats and meals require no refrigeration, store in compact spaces, and rehydrate quickly with water when needed. They're perfect for camping trips, outdoor adventures, and vacation travel while maintaining complete nutritional profiles.

Why choose Zeal Pet Food over other premium brands?

Zeal Pet Food stands out through transparent ingredient sourcing, commitment to raw and natural formulations, grain-free options for all pet types, and science-backed nutritional research. Their freeze-dried and raw options provide maximum nutritional density without artificial additives.

How do I transition my pet to premium natural food?

Introduce premium natural pet food gradually over 7-10 days, mixing increasing proportions with current food. This slow transition allows digestive systems to adjust. Most pets show improved energy and coat quality within 2-3 weeks of complete transition to premium natural nutrition.
