How do I prepare Zeal freeze-dried raw dog food?

Zeal's freeze-dried formulas are simple to prepare. Add the recommended amount of freeze-dried pieces to your dog's bowl, add warm water, and let it rehydrate for 2-3 minutes before serving. This convenience makes it ideal for busy summer schedules and travel.

Is freeze-dried raw dog food complete and balanced?

Yes, Zeal Pet Food's freeze-dried raw formulas are nutritionally complete and balanced, meeting or exceeding AAFCO standards. Each formula contains appropriate protein, fat, vitamins, and minerals for your dog's specific life stage and activity level.

Can freeze-dried raw food help with dog allergies?

Many dogs with food sensitivities benefit from Zeal's grain-free, limited-ingredient freeze-dried options. The absence of fillers, grains, and artificial additives reduces common allergen triggers. However, consult your veterinarian to identify specific dietary restrictions.
