What Pet Owners Should Know

  • Quality over quantity: One well-made toy or accessory often provides more value and longevity than multiple cheaper alternatives that may fall apart after a few weeks.
  • Individual preferences vary: Just like people, dogs have different preferences for toys, beds, and activities. What works for one dog may not work for another, so consider your pet's age, size, and play style.
  • Safety is paramount: Look for products made with non-toxic materials, sturdy construction, and appropriate sizing to prevent choking hazards or injuries.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best gifts for dog owners in 2026?

Some of the top-rated gifts for dog owners in 2026 include personalized dog portrait prints, high-quality treat dispensing puzzle toys, and durable hands-free leashes. Wirecutter's experts test and review hundreds of products to find items that are both practical and loved by dogs and their owners. Popular picks also include cozy dog beds, stylish water bottles with built-in dog bowls, and subscription treat boxes.

What do dog lovers actually want as gifts?

Most dog lovers appreciate gifts that either improve their dog's life or celebrate their bond with their pet, such as custom name dog tags, breed-specific jewelry, or high-quality grooming kits. Practical gifts like portable water bottles, treat pouches, and car seat covers for dogs are consistently well-received. Sentimental items like custom dog portraits or photo books also rank among the most cherished gifts for dog lovers.

What is a good gift for a new dog owner?

Great gifts for new dog owners include starter kits with essentials like a quality leash, collar, and ID tag, along with a durable chew toy and treat pouch. A highly rated book on dog training or a subscription to a dog training app can also be incredibly helpful for first-time pet parents. Wirecutter recommends focusing on safety and comfort items, such as a well-reviewed dog crate or a plush, washable dog bed.

What are unique dog gifts that aren't boring?

Unique dog gifts that go beyond the typical tennis ball include DNA testing kits like Embark that reveal your dog's breed and health traits, custom illustrated pet portraits from Etsy artists, and snuffle mats that mentally stimulate dogs during mealtime. Interactive puzzle feeders and slow feeder bowls are also creative options that double as enrichment tools. For dog lovers, personalized map prints of where they got their dog or custom bobbleheads of their pet make truly memorable gifts.
