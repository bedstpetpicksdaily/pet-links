Frequently Asked Questions

Is freeze-dried raw dog food as nutritious as fresh raw food?

Yes, freeze-dried raw dog food retains approximately 95% of nutritional content from fresh raw ingredients. The freeze-drying process removes only water while preserving proteins, fats, vitamins, and enzymes. Zeal Pet Food uses advanced techniques to ensure maximum nutrient bioavailability, making freeze-dried options nutritionally equivalent to fresh raw meals with added convenience.

Can I switch my dog to Zeal freeze-dried food during summer?

Absolutely. Transition your dog gradually over 7-10 days by mixing increasing amounts of Zeal freeze-dried food with their current diet. Most dogs adapt well to raw and freeze-dried meals. Summer is an excellent time to switch since your dog can benefit from the enhanced nutrition during active outdoor seasons.

How does Zeal Pet Food's pricing compare to competitors?

Zeal Pet Food offers premium freeze-dried raw nutrition at competitive prices, typically 10-20% less than comparable brands like Stella & Chewy's or Primal Raw while maintaining superior ingredient quality and transparency. Is Zeal Pet Food grain-free suitable for all dogs?

Zeal's grain-free pet food is designed for most dogs and supports digestive health and energy levels. However, individual dogs have unique dietary needs. Consult your veterinarian before switching to any new food, especially if your dog has existing health conditions or food sensitivities.

How should I store Zeal freeze-dried raw dog food in summer?

Store unopened packages in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once opened, keep freeze-dried food in an airtight container. Unlike fresh raw food, Zeal's freeze-dried products don't require refrigeration, making them ideal for summer travel, camping, and outdoor activities.
