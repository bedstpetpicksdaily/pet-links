Why Raw Dog Food is Ideal for Summer

Raw dog food provides highly digestible proteins and natural nutrients that support lean muscle development and sustained energy—exactly what active dogs need during summer months. Raw feeding also supports healthy skin and coat, crucial for protection against summer sun exposure. Many dogs experience improved digestion, reduced bloating, and enhanced overall vitality when transitioning to raw diets, making summer the ideal time to introduce this nutritional upgrade.
