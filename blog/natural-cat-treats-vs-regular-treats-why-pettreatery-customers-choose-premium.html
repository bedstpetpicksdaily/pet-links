What's Really in Regular Cat Treats?

Most conventional pet treats found on store shelves contain ingredients that would surprise many cat owners. Corn meal, soy, artificial colors, and chemical preservatives are common staples in budget-friendly treats. While these ingredients help manufacturers cut costs and extend shelf life, they offer little nutritional value to your cat and may even cause allergic reactions or digestive upset.

Regular treats are often formulated with excessive fillers and by-products rather than whole, recognizable ingredients. Your cat's digestive system evolved to process meat and natural nutrients—not synthetic additives. Over time, feeding low-quality treats can contribute to weight gain, dental issues, and chronic health problems that become expensive veterinary bills.
