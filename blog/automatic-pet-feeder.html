Frequently Asked Questions

Are automatic pet feeders safe for cats and dogs?

Yes, automatic pet feeders are generally very safe for both cats and dogs. Look for models with anti-jam mechanisms, secure locking lids to prevent pets from breaking in, and BPA-free food storage compartments to ensure your pet's safety and food freshness.

How many meals per day can an automatic pet feeder dispense?

Most programmable automatic pet feeders can dispense between 1 and 6 meals per day, depending on the model. Premium models may offer even more flexibility, allowing you to set custom portion sizes and feeding times for each scheduled meal.

Can I use an automatic feeder for both wet and dry food?

Most automatic pet feeders are designed specifically for dry kibble or semi-moist food pellets. Wet food can clog the dispenser mechanism and spoil quickly without refrigeration. If your pet requires wet food, look for a specialized automatic feeder with a sealed, refrigerated tray.

What happens if the power goes out — will my pet still be fed?

Many automatic pet feeders include a battery backup feature that keeps the programmed schedule running during a power outage. When shopping, look for models that specify battery backup support to ensure your pet never misses a meal unexpectedly.
