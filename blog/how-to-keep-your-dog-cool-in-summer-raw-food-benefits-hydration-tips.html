Why Raw Food Helps Keep Dogs Cool in Summer

Raw diets are naturally easier for dogs to digest compared to processed kibble. This efficient digestion generates less metabolic heat, which means your dog's body works less hard to process food—a significant advantage during hot summer months. When dogs eat raw food, their digestive system doesn't have to expend as much energy, resulting in lower internal heat production.

Additionally, raw food has higher moisture content than dry dog food. Smallbatch Pets frozen raw meals contain natural hydration from meat and organ content, helping your dog maintain proper fluid levels without extra effort. Their freeze-dried options are also nutrient-dense, delivering complete nutrition in smaller portions that don't burden the digestive system.

Hydration: The Foundation of Summer Dog Care

Beyond food choices, proper hydration is essential. Always provide fresh, cool water in multiple locations around your home and yard. Consider adding ice cubes to your dog's water bowl, or invest in a dog water fountain that keeps water circulating and cool. Dogs are more likely to drink from moving water sources, making fountains an excellent summer investment.

For added hydration benefits, Smallbatch Pets bone broth is an ideal supplement. This nutrient-rich addition to your dog's meals encourages drinking while providing collagen, minerals, and amino acids that support joint and digestive health. Bone broth can be mixed with water to create an enticing hydration drink your dog will love.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is raw food safe for dogs in summer?

Yes, raw food is safe year-round when handled properly. In summer, ensure raw meals are thawed safely in the refrigerator and served promptly. Smallbatch Pets provides detailed handling instructions with all products.

How often should I give my dog water in summer?

Provide fresh water continuously throughout the day. Most dogs naturally self-regulate, but check water bowls frequently in summer to ensure they're always full and cool. Increase frequency after exercise or outdoor activities.

Can I mix Smallbatch Pets bone broth with regular kibble?

Absolutely! Bone broth enhances any diet and encourages hydration. Mix it with kibble, raw food, or offer it separately as a hydrating treat. It's especially beneficial for older dogs or those with digestive sensitivities.

What are signs my dog is overheating?

Watch for excessive panting, drooling, lethargy, vomiting, or glazed eyes. Provide immediate cooling and contact your vet if symptoms persist. Prevention through proper nutrition and hydration from quality diets like Smallbatch Pets is always best.

Does freeze-dried food retain nutritional value?

Yes, freeze-drying preserves nutrients while removing moisture for shelf stability. Smallbatch Pets freeze-dried meals retain the nutritional benefits of raw food with easier storage and convenience.
