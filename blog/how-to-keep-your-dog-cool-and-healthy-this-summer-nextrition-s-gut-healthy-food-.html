Why Summer Nutrition Matters for Dogs

Summer presents unique nutritional challenges for dogs. Heat stress can reduce appetite, affect nutrient absorption, and disrupt the delicate balance of gut bacteria. Dogs with compromised digestion are more susceptible to summer illnesses like gastroenteritis and heat exhaustion. Quality nutrition becomes even more critical during these months. Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed dog food is designed to maintain nutritional integrity without high-heat processing, preserving beneficial enzymes and probiotics that support gut health when your dog needs it most.

The Benefits of Cold-Pressed Dog Food for Summer

Cold-pressed food offers significant advantages over traditional kibble, especially in warm weather. The cold-pressing process preserves natural nutrients, enzymes, and beneficial bacteria that support digestive health. This means better nutrient bioavailability and improved digestion—critical when summer heat already stresses your dog's system. Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed formulas are made with natural ingredients free from artificial additives, making them gentler on sensitive summer stomachs. Many dog owners report improved energy levels, shinier coats, and better digestion within weeks of switching to cold-pressed nutrition.

Practical Summer Feeding Tips

Beyond choosing the right food, feeding practices matter in summer. Offer meals during cooler parts of the day—early morning or evening—to encourage eating and reduce heat stress. Ensure fresh water is always available, and consider slightly smaller, more frequent meals to prevent digestive strain. Store Nextrition's cold-pressed food properly in cool conditions to maintain freshness and nutritional value. Monitor your dog for signs of heat stress or digestive upset, and consult your vet if you notice changes in appetite or energy levels.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is cold-pressed dog food safe for all dogs?

Yes, cold-pressed dog food is safe for most dogs and can be especially beneficial for those with sensitive digestive systems. However, always introduce new foods gradually over 7-10 days and consult your vet if your dog has specific health conditions.

How much does Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed food cost?

Can I mix cold-pressed food with regular kibble?

Yes, you can gradually mix cold-pressed food with your dog's current diet during the transition period. This helps prevent digestive upset and allows your dog's system to adjust to the new nutrition.

What supplements does Nextrition Pet offer?

How do I store cold-pressed dog food in summer?
