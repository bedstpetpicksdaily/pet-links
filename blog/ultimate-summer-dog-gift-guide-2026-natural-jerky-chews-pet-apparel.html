Frequently Asked Questions

What should I look for in natural dog treats?

The best natural dog treats feature recognizable, single-source ingredients without artificial preservatives, colors, or by-products. Check the ingredient list and choose products from brands that prioritize transparency and quality sourcing, like Gnarly Pet.

Are protein-packed chews safe for all dogs?

While generally safe, always supervise your dog while chewing and select appropriate sizes for their mouth. Some dogs may have protein sensitivities, so introduce new chews gradually. Consult your veterinarian if your dog has dietary restrictions.

How often can I give my dog training treats during summer?

Training treats should comprise no more than 10% of your dog's daily caloric intake. During active summer training sessions, portion them appropriately to avoid overfeeding while maintaining effectiveness as rewards.

What type of summer apparel is best for my dog?

Choose lightweight, breathable materials with secure fastenings. Consider your dog's activities—cooling wear for hot days, UV protection for beach visits, and reflective collars for evening walks. Ensure proper fit to prevent chafing or discomfort.
