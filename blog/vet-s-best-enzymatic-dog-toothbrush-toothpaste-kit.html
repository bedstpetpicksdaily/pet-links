Frequently Asked Questions

Does this actually work without daily brushing?

Not entirely. The enzymatic formula does break down plaque chemically, but you'll still see the best results with 4-5 days per week of actual brushing. Skipping brushing completely defeats the purpose—you're better off with enzymatic water additives if consistency is impossible for you.

How does this compare to just taking my dog to the vet for a cleaning?

Completely different purposes. Professional vet cleanings ($300-800) remove tartar that's already calcified on teeth. This kit prevents tartar from calcifying in the first place. Think of it as preventative maintenance, not replacement for professional care. Using this kit might extend the time between professional cleanings from annually to every 2-3 years, which saves significant money long-term.

Is it safe to use daily on sensitive teeth or older dogs?

Yes, actually safer than you might think. The enzymatic formula is gentler than mechanical brushing, and there's no abrasive grit like some dog toothpastes include. That said, the soft bristles are the real star here—if your dog has receding gums or gingivitis, consult your vet before starting any brushing routine, regardless of brand.
