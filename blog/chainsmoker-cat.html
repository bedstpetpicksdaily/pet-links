Is the Chainsmoker Cat safe for cats to play with?

No, the Chainsmoker Cat is not designed as a pet toy and should be kept away from actual cats. It's a decorative novelty item for adults, and the small moving parts and smoke effects could pose a choking hazard or stress to animals.

How long does the Chainsmoker Cat smoke for?
