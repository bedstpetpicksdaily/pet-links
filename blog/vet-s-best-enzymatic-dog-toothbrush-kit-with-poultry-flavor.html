Frequently Asked Questions

How often do I need to brush with this kit to see results?

Most owners see visible plaque reduction within 2-3 weeks of daily brushing, or 4-6 weeks with 3-4 times weekly brushing. The enzymatic formula accelerates results compared to regular toothpaste, but consistency matters more than frequency. Occasional brushing once a week won't produce noticeable improvements in tartar buildup.

Will this kit actually work for dogs that hate dental care?

The poultry flavor is the game-changer here—most resistant dogs accept it within 2-3 applications. Start by letting your dog lick the paste off your finger for a few days before introducing the brush. The enzymatic formula also means less aggressive brushing pressure needed, which keeps sessions shorter and less stressful for anxious dogs.

Is this suitable for puppies or only adult dogs?

This kit works for dogs 8 weeks and older. For very young puppies under 6 months, use less paste and focus on getting them comfortable with the brush in their mouth rather than aggressive cleaning. The enzymatic formula is safe for puppies to swallow in small amounts, so don't stress if they ingest some during early training sessions.

Does the kit include enough toothpaste for long-term use?

The included toothpaste tube (typically 2.5 oz) lasts 2-3 months with daily brushing for a medium-sized dog. You'll need to purchase refill tubes afterward, which are inexpensive. Many owners buy this kit for the brush quality and then stock up on replacement paste tubes since they're economical.
