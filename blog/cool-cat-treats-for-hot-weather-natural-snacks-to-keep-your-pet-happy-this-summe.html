Why Summer Treats Matter for Cats

During hot weather, cats often experience reduced appetite and may become less active. This is a natural response to heat, but it's crucial to ensure they still receive adequate nutrition and hydration. Offering cooling treats can encourage your cat to eat and drink more, maintaining their energy levels and overall health. Natural pet snacks from PetTreatery are formulated to be nutritious without being heavy, making them perfect for summer consumption. These treats can also serve as positive reinforcement during training sessions or simply as a bonding activity between you and your pet.

Hydration-Focused Treat Options

One of the best ways to keep your cat cool is through treats that naturally contain moisture. PetTreatery specializes in natural snacks that support hydration while satisfying your cat's cravings. Treats made from fish-based ingredients are excellent choices as they're both hydrating and nutrient-dense. Frequently Asked Questions

Can I give my cat frozen treats during summer?

Yes, freezing natural treats like those from PetTreatery can provide a refreshing, cooling effect. However, avoid giving extremely hard frozen treats that could potentially damage teeth. Slightly chilled treats are the safest option for most cats.

Are natural pet treats better for cats than regular treats?

Natural treats from premium brands like PetTreatery offer several advantages: no artificial additives, better digestibility, and higher nutritional value. They're especially beneficial during summer when your cat's digestive system may be more sensitive to heat.

How often should I give my cat treats during hot weather?

Treats should comprise no more than 10% of your cat's daily caloric intake. During summer, you may give treats more frequently in smaller portions to encourage hydration and eating, but monitor overall calorie consumption to prevent weight gain.

What ingredients should I look for in summer cat treats?

Look for high-protein ingredients like freeze-dried chicken or fish, minimal fillers, and no artificial preservatives. PetTreatery's natural formulations include these beneficial ingredients while avoiding unnecessary additives that could upset sensitive summer stomachs.

Can natural treats help with my cat's hydration in summer?

Yes, treats with higher moisture content and fish-based formulas can contribute to hydration. Combining treats with fresh water and wet food creates a comprehensive approach to keeping your cat hydrated during hot months.
