Frequently Asked Questions

Is Zeal Pet Food raw food safe for dogs and cats?

Yes. Zeal Pet Food raw formulas are carefully balanced to meet AAFCO nutritional standards and sourced from ethically raised animals. Always introduce raw food gradually and follow feeding guidelines on packaging. Consult your veterinarian before transitioning to raw diets, especially for pets with health conditions.

How long do Zeal freeze-dried treats last?

Freeze-dried treats maintain freshness for 12-24 months when stored in a cool, dry place away from moisture and direct sunlight. Once opened, consume within 3-4 weeks for optimal nutritional value. No refrigeration required, making them ideal for travel and outdoor adventures.

Can I mix Zeal raw food with regular kibble?

Yes, many pet owners blend raw and kibble as part of a transitional diet. However, some prefer feeding them separately to avoid digestive upset. Start with 10-20% raw food and gradually increase over 7-10 days. Monitor your pet's digestion and energy levels, and consult your vet for personalized recommendations.

Are Zeal products grain-free and suitable for allergies?

Zeal Pet Food specializes in grain-free formulations designed for pets with sensitivities. Their recipes eliminate corn, wheat, soy, and artificial additives. However, individual pets may react differently. Always check ingredient lists carefully and introduce new foods gradually, especially if your pet has known allergies.
