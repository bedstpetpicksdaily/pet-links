Frequently Asked Questions

What treats work best with Furbo's toss feature?

Standard small kibble works perfectly—Furbo's official treat recommendations are your safest bet. Hard biscuits cut to kibble size also work. Avoid anything soft, sticky, or larger than a pea. During my testing, premium training treats like Zuke's mini naturals jammed the mechanism consistently, so stick with dry, uniform-sized options.

How does Furbo compare to cheaper alternatives like Wyze or Arlo?

Basic video and audio quality are comparable—all three offer 1080p, night vision, and two-way audio. The core difference is the treat toss (Furbo exclusive) and app responsiveness (Furbo slightly faster). Wyze Cam Pan costs $60-80 less and covers wider angles, but can't toss treats. Arlo Essential matches Furbo's price but lacks treat dispensing. Choose based on whether treat tossing genuinely helps your dog's behavior or training.

Is the treat toss worth the extra cost, or is it just a gimmick?

It's situational. If your dog has separation anxiety or you're training them to self-soothe when you leave, the treat toss becomes a legitimate behavioral tool—I used it successfully with my retriever to associate my departure with positive rewards. If you just want to watch your dog nap all day, it's absolutely a gimmick. Budget-conscious owners should ask themselves: 'Will I actually use treat tossing 2-3 times weekly?' If no, save the $60-80.
