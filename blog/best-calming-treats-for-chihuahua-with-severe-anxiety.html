Chihuahuas with severe anxiety require thoughtful, targeted solutions that address their unique neurological and physical needs. What to Look For

  • Gentle, Single-Source Proteins – Chihuahuas with anxiety often have sensitive digestive systems that can worsen stress responses. Look for treats with limited, easily digestible proteins like chicken or turkey rather than complex multi-ingredient formulas that may cause gut inflammation.
  • Calming Ingredients Specifically Dosed for Small Bodies – Standard doses of L-theanine, ashwagandha, or CBD are calibrated for larger dogs. Your chihuahua needs treats formulated for dogs under 10 pounds to ensure therapeutic doses aren't too concentrated or too diluted.
  • Soft, Easy-to-Consume Texture – Anxious chihuahuas may have difficulty chewing hard treats due to tension and stress. Soft, quick-dissolving treats minimize additional stress from chewing and allow faster absorption of calming compounds.
