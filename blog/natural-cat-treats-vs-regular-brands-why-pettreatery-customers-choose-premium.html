Frequently Asked Questions

Are natural cat treats more expensive than regular brands?

Yes, premium natural treats typically cost 30-50% more due to higher-quality ingredients and rigorous sourcing standards. However, many owners find the investment justified by improved cat health and reduced veterinary expenses over time.

Can switching to natural treats help with my cat's digestive issues?

Many cats experience improved digestion when switching from regular to natural treats, as premium options contain fewer fillers and artificial additives. However, consult your veterinarian before making significant dietary changes, especially if your cat has existing health conditions.

What ingredients should I avoid in cat treats?

Avoid treats containing artificial colors, BHA/BHT preservatives, corn syrup, excessive grains, and meat by-products. Look for treats listing real meat as the first ingredient, just like PetTreatery products do.

How often should I give my cat treats?

Treats should comprise no more than 10% of your cat's daily caloric intake. Whether natural or regular, moderation is key to maintaining a healthy weight and diet balance.

Does PetTreatery offer treats for cats with allergies?

PetTreatery provides various formulations designed for different dietary needs. Check their website for allergen-friendly options, and always consult your vet about treats suitable for cats with specific allergies.
