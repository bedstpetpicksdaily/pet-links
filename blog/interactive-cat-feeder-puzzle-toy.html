Frequently Asked Questions

What age can kittens start using puzzle feeders?

Most kittens can begin using simple puzzle feeders around 3-4 months old. Start with easier designs and supervise playtime to ensure safety and proper usage.

How do I introduce my cat to a puzzle feeder?

Start by placing your cat's regular food in the puzzle feeder during normal meal times. Gradually increase the difficulty level as your cat becomes more comfortable with the concept.

Are interactive cat feeders suitable for wet food?

Most puzzle feeders are designed for dry kibble. However, some models offer separate compartments for wet food. Check product specifications before purchasing for wet food compatibility.

How often should I use a puzzle feeder?

You can use puzzle feeders daily for one or more meals. Many cat owners rotate between regular bowls and puzzle feeders to maintain interest and prevent frustration.
