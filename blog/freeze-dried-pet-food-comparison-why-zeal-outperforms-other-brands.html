What Makes Freeze-Dried Pet Food Superior?

Freeze-dried pet food represents a significant advancement in pet nutrition technology. Unlike traditional pet food manufacturing that uses high heat and processing chemicals, freeze-drying maintains the integrity of raw ingredients while creating a shelf-stable product. This process removes up to 98% of moisture content, concentrating nutrients and creating a more bioavailable food for your pet's digestive system. The result is a premium natural pet food that delivers superior nutritional density compared to conventional kibble.

Zeal Pet Food has mastered the freeze-drying process, utilizing only premium, ethically-sourced ingredients that reflect the raw dog food philosophy.
