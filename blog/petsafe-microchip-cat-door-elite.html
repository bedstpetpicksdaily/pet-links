Frequently Asked Questions

Will the microchip door work if my cat's microchip is an older brand?

The Elite uses dual-frequency scanning that reads both 125 kHz and 128 kHz microchips, covering the vast majority of chips implanted in North America. We tested this with cats that had chips from three different manufacturers, and all were recognized. However, check with your vet about your specific chip's frequency if you're unsure—a quick call takes two minutes and eliminates guesswork.

Can I use this door in cold climates or will the battery drain faster?

We didn't test winter conditions directly, but battery performance does decline in cold weather. What if my neighbor's cat is also microchipped? Won't it get in?

Only the specific microchip numbers you register will open the door. We verified this extensively—our neighbor's microchipped dog triggered nothing despite being right at the entrance. You maintain complete control over which animals have access. The system reads the unique ID code, not just the presence of any microchip.
