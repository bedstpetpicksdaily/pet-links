Why Cold-Pressed Dog Food is Perfect for Summer

Cold-pressed dog food stands out because it's manufactured at low temperatures, preserving the nutritional integrity of raw ingredients. This gentle processing method maintains beneficial enzymes and probiotics that aid digestion—something especially important when summer heat can upset sensitive stomachs. Cold-pressed formulas also contain higher moisture content than traditional kibble, helping keep your dog hydrated during hot weather. Many dogs experience reduced appetite in summer, and the nutrient-dense, easily digestible nature of cold-pressed food makes it an excellent choice for maintaining their nutritional intake even when they're less interested in eating.

Is cold-pressed dog food better than regular kibble?

Cold-pressed dog food retains more nutrients and enzymes through gentle, low-temperature processing compared to high-heat kibble production. This results in better digestibility, improved gut health, and often higher-quality ingredients, making it a superior choice for most dogs—especially those with sensitive digestion.
