Frequently Asked Questions

Are natural cat treats worth the extra cost?

Yes. While natural treats cost more upfront, they prevent health issues that lead to expensive veterinary bills. Plus, their superior palatability means you can use fewer treats for the same satisfaction, offsetting the price difference.

Can I give my cat natural treats every day?

Natural treats should comprise no more than 10% of your cat's daily caloric intake. PetTreatery's premium selection is designed for regular use within this guideline, supporting a balanced diet without overfeeding.

What ingredients should I avoid in cat treats?

Avoid treats containing meat by-products, corn syrup, artificial colors, BHA/BHT preservatives, and excessive fillers. PetTreatery's transparent labeling makes it easy to identify exactly what you're giving your cat.

How do I transition my cat to natural treats?

Introduce PetTreatery's natural treats gradually by mixing small amounts with your cat's current treats over 7-10 days. This allows their digestive system to adjust to the new ingredients without upset.

Are grain-free natural treats better for cats?

Not necessarily. While some cats benefit from grain-free options, others thrive with whole grains. PetTreatery offers both grain-inclusive and grain-free natural treats, so you can choose based on your cat's individual needs.
