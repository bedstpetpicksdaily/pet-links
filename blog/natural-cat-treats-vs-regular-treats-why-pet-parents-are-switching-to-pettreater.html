What Makes Natural Cat Treats Different?

Natural cat treats are fundamentally different from regular commercial treats in their sourcing, processing, and ingredient lists. Natural treats are typically made with real, recognizable ingredients like chicken, fish, turkey, and vegetables—without artificial flavors, colors, or preservatives. Regular treats, by contrast, often contain by-products, fillers, and chemical additives that provide little nutritional value while potentially causing digestive upset or allergic reactions in sensitive cats.

When you choose natural cat treats, you're investing in products that respect your cat's dietary needs as an obligate carnivore. Frequently Asked Questions

Are natural cat treats worth the extra cost?

Absolutely. While natural treats cost more upfront, they prevent costly veterinary issues, improve digestion, and provide genuine nutrition. Many pet parents find that the long-term health benefits and reduced vet visits offset the higher initial investment. PetTreatery's quality makes the premium pricing completely justified.

Can switching to natural treats cause digestive upset?

When switching from regular to natural treats, introduce them gradually over 7-10 days to allow your cat's digestive system to adjust. Once acclimated, natural treats typically improve digestion rather than cause issues. PetTreatery recommends a gradual transition approach for best results.

How often can I give my cat natural treats?

Treats should comprise no more than 10% of your cat's daily caloric intake. Natural treats are more nutrient-dense than regular treats, so your cat may feel satisfied with smaller portions. Always follow PetTreatery's feeding guidelines based on your cat's age and weight.

What ingredients should I avoid in cat treats?

Avoid treats containing by-products, artificial flavors and colors, excessive carbohydrates, corn, wheat, and soy. Look for named meat sources (chicken, fish, turkey) as the primary ingredients. PetTreatery's transparency makes it easy to identify quality treats with beneficial ingredients.
