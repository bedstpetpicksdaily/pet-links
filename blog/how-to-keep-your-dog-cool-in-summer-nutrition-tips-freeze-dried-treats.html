Frequently Asked Questions

Can changing my dog's diet really help them stay cooler in summer?

Yes, absolutely. A high-quality, nutrient-dense diet reduces metabolic heat production and supports efficient temperature regulation. Raw and freeze-dried foods with natural moisture content provide added hydration benefits. Grain-free formulations reduce inflammation, further supporting your dog's ability to stay cool and comfortable.

What are the benefits of freeze-dried dog treats over traditional treats?

Freeze-dried treats preserve nutrients through low-temperature processing, avoiding the nutrient degradation caused by high-heat cooking. They're lighter, shelf-stable, and can be rehydrated for extra moisture. For summer, they provide refreshing snacking without generating metabolic heat like heavily processed treats.

Is raw dog food safe for summer feeding?

Yes, when handled properly. Raw dog food requires appropriate storage and handling, but it offers superior nutritional benefits and natural moisture content that supports hydration. Many veterinarians recommend premium raw options like Zeal Pet Food for dogs that tolerate them well, especially during summer months.

How does grain-free pet nutrition support summer wellness?

Grains can cause inflammation and digestive issues that impair temperature regulation. Grain-free formulas eliminate these stressors and provide cleaner energy sources through high-quality proteins and healthy fats, supporting immune function and better overall health during hot weather.

Can freeze-dried pet food from Zeal be mixed with regular kibble?

Yes, Zeal's freeze-dried products can be mixed with kibble or used as toppers to enhance nutrition and moisture content. This flexibility allows you to gradually transition your dog to premium nutrition while maintaining digestive stability.
