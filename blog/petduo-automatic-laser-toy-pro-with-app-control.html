This is the most asked question I see in the 500+ reviews. The PetDuo Pro uses a Class 3A laser (standard for pet toys), which is considered safe for incidental eye exposure. That said, the intent is to chase the dot, not stare at the source. My vet confirmed this is safe with normal use, but avoid pointing it directly into your pet's eyes intentionally. The app's random patterns actually help since they prevent obsessive fixation.
