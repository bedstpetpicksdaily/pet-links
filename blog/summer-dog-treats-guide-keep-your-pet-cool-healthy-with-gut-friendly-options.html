Why Summer Requires Special Dog Treat Considerations

During summer months, dogs face unique challenges including heat stress, increased hydration needs, and potential digestive upset from dietary changes. Your dog's gut health becomes increasingly important as their activity levels shift and they may consume different foods. Treats that are specifically formulated to support digestive health can help prevent common summer issues like loose stools, bloating, and food sensitivities. Looking for treats with probiotics, prebiotic fibers, and natural ingredients ensures your pet receives optimal nutrition while staying cool and comfortable.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a dog treat gut-friendly?

Gut-friendly dog treats contain natural, whole-food ingredients, probiotics, prebiotic fibers, and are free from artificial additives that can irritate digestive systems. Premium brands like Nextrition Pet formulate treats specifically to support digestive health and beneficial gut bacteria.

Are frozen treats good for dogs in summer?

Yes, frozen treats provide hydration and cooling relief during hot months. However, choose those made from natural ingredients and whole foods rather than artificial ingredients. Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed options can be frozen for a nutritious, digestive-friendly summer treat.

How often can I give my dog summer treats?

Treats should constitute no more than 10% of your dog's daily caloric intake. During summer, monitor treat frequency to ensure you're not overfeeding while maintaining proper nutrition. Consult with your veterinarian for personalized recommendations based on your dog's age, size, and health status.

What ingredients should I avoid in summer dog treats?

Avoid artificial colors, flavors, preservatives, high sugar content, xylitol, and low-quality fillers. These ingredients can upset your dog's digestive system and provide minimal nutritional value. Choose brands like Nextrition Pet that prioritize natural, recognizable ingredients.

Can summer treats help with my dog's digestive issues?

Yes, treats formulated with probiotics, prebiotic fibers, and natural ingredients can support digestive health. Nextrition Pet's premium gut-friendly treats are designed to promote a healthy microbiome and reduce digestive upset common in sensitive dogs.
