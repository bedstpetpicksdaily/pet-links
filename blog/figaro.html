Frequently Asked Questions

What is Figaro olive oil?

Figaro is a popular Spanish olive oil brand known for its smooth, light taste and affordable price point. It's a refined olive oil blend that works well for cooking, frying, and everyday kitchen use.

Is Figaro olive oil good for cooking?

Yes, Figaro olive oil is excellent for cooking due to its high smoke point of around 460°F (240°C), making it suitable for frying, sautéing, and baking. Its neutral flavor won't overpower dishes, making it ideal for general cooking purposes.

Where to buy Figaro olive oil?

Figaro olive oil is widely available at most grocery stores, supermarkets, and online retailers like Amazon, Walmart, and specialty food websites. You can typically find it in the cooking oil aisle or international foods section.

How much does Figaro olive oil cost?

Figaro olive oil is budget-friendly, typically ranging from $5-$8 for a 500ml bottle and $8-$12 for a 1-liter bottle, depending on your location and retailer. It offers excellent value for everyday cooking compared to premium extra virgin olive oil brands.
