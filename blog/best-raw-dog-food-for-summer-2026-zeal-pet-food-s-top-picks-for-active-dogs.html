Raw dog food offers unparalleled nutritional density compared to traditional kibble, making it the superior choice for active dogs during summer. This means your dog's body can absorb and utilize these nutrients more efficiently, translating to sustained energy levels during extended outdoor activities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is raw dog food safe for active dogs?

Yes, raw dog food is entirely safe when sourced from reputable brands like Zeal Pet Food. Raw nutrition mimics what dogs' ancestral diets contained and is particularly beneficial for active dogs due to superior nutrient bioavailability. Always follow proper handling protocols and consult your veterinarian when making dietary transitions.

How much raw food should I feed my active dog during summer?

Active dogs typically require 2-3% of their body weight in raw food daily, though this varies based on activity level, age, and metabolism. During intense summer activity, some active dogs may require feeding at the higher end of this range. Consult Zeal Pet Food's feeding guidelines or your veterinarian for personalized recommendations.

What makes Zeal Pet Food's raw options better than other brands?

Zeal Pet Food specializes in premium natural pet nutrition with a focus on grain-free formulations and freeze-dried raw options. Their products undergo rigorous quality control, feature high-quality protein sources, and eliminate artificial ingredients—ensuring your active dog receives optimal nutrition without unnecessary fillers.

Can I mix raw dog food with kibble for active dogs?

Yes, transitioning gradually by mixing raw and kibble is an effective approach. Start with 10-15% raw food mixed with 85-90% kibble, increasing the raw portion over 7-10 days. This allows your dog's digestive system to adapt while delivering the enhanced nutrition of Zeal's premium raw pet food options.

Does freeze-dried raw dog food offer the same benefits as fresh raw?

Freeze-dried raw dog food from Zeal Pet Food retains nearly all nutritional benefits of fresh raw while offering superior convenience and storage longevity. The freeze-drying process preserves nutrients, enzymes, and bioavailable proteins without requiring frozen storage or complex handling protocols.
