Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Placing it in high-traffic areas — Position your cat tree in a quieter corner where your cat can observe without feeling overwhelmed, rather than near doors or busy walkways.
  • Neglecting regular maintenance — Dust and vacuum the tree weekly to prevent odors and keep the sisal rope clean, which encourages continued use and extends the product's life.
  • Expecting instant adoption — Some cats need time to adjust to new furniture; use treats and catnip to encourage exploration rather than forcing interaction during the first few days.

Expert Tips for Best Results

  • Place it near a window or favorite spot — Position the tree where your cat naturally wants to be, whether that's near a sunny window for bird watching or close to their favorite resting area, to maximize usage.
  • Rotate toys and refresh with catnip monthly — Keep interest high by swapping out attached toys and reapplying catnip to maintain your cat's enthusiasm over time.
  • Pair with consistent play sessions — Use wand toys to encourage climbing and jumping on the tree during interactive play, which builds positive associations and keeps your cat physically active.
