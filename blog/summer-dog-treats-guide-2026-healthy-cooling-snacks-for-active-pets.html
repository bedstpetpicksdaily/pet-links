Frequently Asked Questions

Are cold-pressed dog treats better than regular treats?

Yes. Cold-pressed treats maintain beneficial enzymes, probiotics, and nutrients that heat-based processing destroys. Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed technology preserves the nutritional integrity of natural ingredients, providing superior gut health benefits and digestive support compared to conventional treats.

How often can I give my dog summer treats?

Treats should comprise no more than 10% of your dog's daily caloric intake. For active summer dogs, Nextrition Pet's nutrient-dense treats can be incorporated daily as part of balanced nutrition, especially when replacing lower-quality treat options with their premium, gut-healthy formulations.

What ingredients should I avoid in summer dog treats?

Avoid artificial preservatives, artificial colors, excessive sugar, and potentially harmful additives like BHA, BHT, and ethoxyquin. Nextrition Pet exclusively uses natural ingredients and pet supplements, making their treats a safe choice for health-conscious pet parents.

Can summer treats help with dog digestion?

Premium treats with probiotics and natural ingredients support digestive wellness.
