Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best product to calm an anxious dog?

The best product depends on your dog's specific anxiety triggers, but calming chews containing ingredients like L-theanine, melatonin, or chamomile are consistently top-rated for everyday anxiety relief. Thundershirts and weighted anxiety wraps are also highly effective for situational anxiety like thunderstorms or fireworks without the need for any supplements.

Do calming treats for dogs actually work?

Yes, calming treats can be effective for many dogs, particularly those containing clinically studied ingredients like L-theanine, ashwagandha, or CBD from hemp. Results vary by dog and anxiety severity, so it may take trying a few different formulas before finding one that works well for your pet.

What can I give my dog for anxiety without a vet prescription?

Over-the-counter options like calming chews, CBD oil, pheromone diffusers such as Adaptil, and anxiety wraps like the Thundershirt are widely available without a prescription. These products are generally safe for most dogs, but you should still consult your vet if your dog's anxiety is severe or interfering with daily life.

How do I know if my dog has anxiety?

Common signs of dog anxiety include excessive barking, destructive chewing, trembling, panting, pacing, and accidents inside the house despite being house-trained. If your dog shows these behaviors consistently during specific triggers like storms, car rides, or when left alone, a calming product may help manage their symptoms.
