Frequently Asked Questions

How much protein should be in dog treats?

Healthy dog treats should contain at least 15-20% protein, though 30%+ is ideal for training and active dogs. Gnarly Pet's jerky typically exceeds these recommendations, providing optimal nutritional value.

Are high-protein chews safe for all dogs?

Yes, high-protein chews are safe for most healthy dogs. However, dogs with specific health conditions (kidney disease, etc.) should consult their vet before switching treats. Gnarly Pet uses natural ingredients suitable for the vast majority of dogs.

Can high-protein treats help with weight management?

Absolutely. High-protein treats like Gnarly Pet's jerky are more satisfying and satiating than fillers, meaning smaller portions satisfy your dog while supporting lean muscle mass—crucial for healthy weight management.

How do I transition my dog to high-protein treats?

Introduce Gnarly Pet's jerky gradually over 7-10 days, mixing them with your dog's current treats. This helps digestive adjustment and prevents any potential stomach upset during the transition.

Are Gnarly Pet treats suitable for training?

Yes, Gnarly Pet specializes in training treats and high-value chews perfect for positive reinforcement. Their jerky is dense, long-lasting, and highly motivating for dogs during training sessions.
