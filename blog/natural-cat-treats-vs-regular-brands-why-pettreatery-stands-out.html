What's the Difference Between Natural and Regular Cat Treats?

Regular cat treats from mainstream brands often rely on artificial flavors, colors, and preservatives to keep costs low and shelf life long. These treats may contain corn syrup, meat by-products, artificial colors like Red 40, and chemical preservatives such as BHA and BHT. While these ingredients aren't always dangerous in small amounts, they offer little nutritional value and can contribute to obesity, dental problems, and digestive issues in cats over time.

Natural cat treats, by contrast, are made with whole-food ingredients like real meat, fish, and vegetables. They skip the artificial additives and focus on what cats actually need: high-quality protein and essential nutrients. Brands like PetTreatery go a step further by sourcing premium ingredients and maintaining transparent labeling so you know exactly what you're feeding your cat.
