Frequently Asked Questions

Is raw dog food safe for active dogs?

Yes, raw dog food is safe when sourced from reputable providers like Smallbatch Pets. Raw diets provide high bioavailability of nutrients, which supports muscle recovery and sustained energy in active dogs. Always consult your veterinarian before transitioning to raw feeding.

Can I use Smallbatch freeze-dried treats for training during outdoor activities?

Absolutely. Freeze-dried treats are lightweight, shelf-stable, and don't require refrigeration, making them perfect for training during hikes, beach days, or camping trips. They're highly palatable and nutrient-dense, rewarding good behavior without extra mess.

What's the difference between Smallbatch's raw meals and lightly cooked meals?

Smallbatch raw meals are minimally processed to retain maximum enzymes and nutrients, ideal for dogs with robust digestion. Lightly cooked meals are gently heated for added digestibility while preserving most nutrients, making them suitable for dogs transitioning from kibble or those with sensitive systems.

How does bone broth benefit active dogs?

Bone broth is rich in collagen, glucosamine, and amino acids that support joint health, skin condition, and hydration. For active dogs engaging in summer sports or long outdoor adventures, bone broth helps with recovery and maintains overall wellness.
