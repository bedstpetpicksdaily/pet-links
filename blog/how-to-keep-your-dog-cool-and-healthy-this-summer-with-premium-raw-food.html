Why Raw Food is Ideal for Summer Nutrition

Raw dog food is naturally rich in moisture content and essential nutrients that support optimal body temperature regulation. Unlike dry kibble, which requires significant water absorption during digestion, raw food is already moisture-rich, helping your dog stay naturally hydrated. This is crucial during summer when dehydration risks are higher. Zeal Pet Food's raw dog food options contain whole, natural ingredients without artificial additives, making them an excellent choice for dogs with sensitive digestive systems. The freeze-dried varieties also preserve nutritional integrity while offering convenient storage and feeding options.
