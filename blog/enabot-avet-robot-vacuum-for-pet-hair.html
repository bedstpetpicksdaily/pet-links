Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Enabot Avet good for dog and cat hair?

Yes, the Enabot Avet is specifically designed for pet hair with powerful suction and a brush system that effectively captures dog and cat fur without tangling.

Can I control the Enabot Avet with my phone?

Yes, the robot vacuum includes a mobile app for scheduling, starting, stopping, and monitoring cleaning sessions remotely from your smartphone.

How often do I need to empty the dust bin?

In homes with pets, we recommend emptying the dust bin after each cleaning cycle or every 1-2 days for optimal performance.

Does it work on carpet?

The Enabot Avet performs well on low to medium pile carpets and hard floors but may struggle with thick, high-pile carpet common in some homes.
