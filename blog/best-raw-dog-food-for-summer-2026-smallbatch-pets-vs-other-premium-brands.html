Why Raw Dog Food Matters in Summer 2026

Raw dog food diets have gained significant traction among pet owners seeking species-appropriate nutrition for their canine companions. Unlike traditional kibble, raw diets provide whole prey models, organ meats, and nutrient-dense ingredients that more closely mimic what dogs would eat in nature. During summer months, raw food can offer superior digestibility, improved coat quality, and better energy management—especially important when your dog is more active outdoors.

The raw pet food market has evolved dramatically, with brands now offering convenient formats beyond traditional frozen meals. Freeze-dried raw food, bone broth supplements, and specialized treats have made the raw feeding lifestyle more accessible to busy pet parents who want premium nutrition without the hassle of handling and storing large quantities of frozen meat.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Smallbatch Pets raw dog food safe for puppies?

Yes, Smallbatch Pets offers formulations designed for puppies and different life stages. However, consult your veterinarian before transitioning puppies to raw diets, as proper calcium-phosphorus ratios and nutrient balance are critical during growth phases.

How should I store Smallbatch Pets freeze-dried dog food?

Freeze-dried raw food requires cool, dry storage away from direct sunlight and humidity. Unlike frozen options, freeze-dried formats maintain stability at room temperature, making them ideal for travel or dogs with limited freezer space.

Can I mix Smallbatch Pets raw food with kibble?

While possible, mixing raw and kibble isn't typically recommended due to different digestion rates. Many owners transition gradually or feed raw meals separately. Consult your vet about your specific dog's dietary needs.

How does Smallbatch Pets pricing compare to other raw brands?

Smallbatch Pets offers competitive pricing, typically $2-4 per pound for frozen raw meals and $8-12 for freeze-dried options—comparable to or slightly below Primal, while offering more variety than many competitors.
