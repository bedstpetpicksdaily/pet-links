Frequently Asked Questions

Are organic dog treats better for training than regular treats?

Yes, organic treats are superior for training because they're made with whole-food ingredients that are easier to digest and provide sustained energy. PetTreatery's organic options are formulated to keep your dog focused and energized without the digestive upset that artificial ingredients can cause.

How should I store organic dog treats during summer?

Most organic treats, especially freeze-dried varieties from PetTreatery, are shelf-stable and don't require refrigeration. Store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. For extended trips, keep them in an airtight container to maintain freshness and prevent moisture absorption.

Can I give my dog organic treats more frequently than regular treats?

While organic treats are healthier, treats should still represent no more than 10% of your dog's daily caloric intake. PetTreatery's training-size treats are specifically designed to allow frequent rewards without overfeeding, making them perfect for summer training sessions.

What ingredients should I avoid in dog treats during hot weather?

Avoid treats with artificial preservatives, excessive fats, high sodium, and fillers like corn and soy. These can cause digestive upset and increase thirst in hot weather. PetTreatery eliminates all these problematic ingredients, focusing instead on clean, recognizable natural ingredients.
