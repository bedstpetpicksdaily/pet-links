Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not clearing the floor first: Pet toys, cords, and loose items will jam the vacuum or stop it in its tracks. Spend 5 minutes doing a quick sweep before each run to maximize efficiency.
  • Ignoring the dustbin and brushes: Pet hair wraps around the side brush and clogs the bin faster than regular dust. Empty it after every 2-3 runs and check for tangled hair weekly to maintain suction power.
  • Running it on thick carpets: This nano model isn't designed for plush or shag carpeting. Using it on surfaces beyond its capability will reduce battery life and cleaning effectiveness without delivering results.

Expert Tips for Best Results

  • Create a schedule around shedding: Run the vacuum daily during heavy shedding seasons and every other day otherwise. Frequent, shorter cleaning sessions prevent hair from settling into fibers better than occasional deep cleans.
  • Pair it with a handheld vacuum: Use the Evo Nano for maintenance cleaning, then do a weekly quick pass with a handheld stick vacuum on problem areas. This hybrid approach catches what the robot misses without the effort of full manual vacuuming.
  • Position the dock strategically: Place the charging dock in a central, easily accessible location away from bedding areas. This ensures the vacuum can return quickly and gives you flexibility to run it multiple times daily without extra effort.
