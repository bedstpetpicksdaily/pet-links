Frequently Asked Questions

Is raw dog food safe for summer feeding?

Yes, freeze-dried raw dog food like Zeal Pet Food is safe for summer. The freeze-drying process eliminates pathogens while preserving nutrients. Simply rehydrate with water before serving and consume within recommended timeframes after preparation.

How does Zeal Pet Food compare to fresh raw brands?

Zeal Pet Food's freeze-dried format offers superior convenience and shelf stability compared to fresh raw brands. It requires no refrigeration, is perfect for travel, and delivers the same nutritional benefits as fresh raw food without the storage limitations or shorter shelf life.

What makes grain-free raw food better for dogs?

Grain-free raw formulations like those from Zeal Pet Food better align with dogs' ancestral diets and digestive systems. They reduce inflammatory responses, support easier digestion, and provide protein-focused nutrition without unnecessary fillers.

Can I mix Zeal Pet Food with other foods?

Yes, Zeal freeze-dried raw can be mixed with other foods, though many recommend feeding it as a complete meal for optimal nutritional balance. Consult your veterinarian about transition protocols when introducing new foods.

How do I store Zeal Pet Food during summer?

Store unopened Zeal packages in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once rehydrated, refrigerate prepared meals and serve within 24 hours. The freeze-dried format's stability makes it ideal for summer camping and outdoor activities.
