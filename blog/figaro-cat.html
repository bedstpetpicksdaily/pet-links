What to Look For

1. Authentic Tuxedo Markings

Look for the classic Figaro pattern: a predominantly black coat with white on the chest, paws, and face. The most sought-after Figaro cats have a distinctive white blaze down the center of the face and white "socks" on their paws. Check breeding documentation or adoption profiles that specifically describe these markings.

2. Health Certifications and Background

Whether adopting or purchasing, verify health screenings for genetic issues common in cats. Request vaccination records, spay/neuter documentation, and any available genetic testing. Reputable sources will provide a health guarantee or return policy.

3. Temperament Match

Figaro cats are known for being intelligent, playful, and social. Make sure the specific cat's personality matches your lifestyle. Ask shelters and breeders about energy levels, socialization with other pets, and whether they're suited for apartment living or need space to roam.

4. Source Reputation

Buy from certified animal shelters, breed-specific rescues, or TICA/CFA-registered breeders. Avoid any sources without verifiable reviews or transparent adoption/sales processes. The safest option is always local adoption centers where you can meet the cat in person first.
