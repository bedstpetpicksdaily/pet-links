How much raw dog food should I feed during summer?

Generally, feed 2-3% of your dog's body weight daily, adjusted for increased summer activity. Zeal's formulations are nutrient-dense, so portions are typically smaller than kibble-based diets. Consult their feeding guidelines for specific recommendations based on your dog's age and activity level.
