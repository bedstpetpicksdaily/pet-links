What to Look For

  • Clinically-Proven Ingredients: Look for treats containing L-theanine, melatonin, or adaptogenic herbs like ashwagandha and chamomile. These ingredients have research backing them for anxiety reduction in dogs, unlike trendy but unproven alternatives. Rescue dogs need solutions that actually work, not just feel-good marketing.

Soft chews, bite-sized pieces, or treats that dissolve easily prevent adding frustration to an already anxious situation.
