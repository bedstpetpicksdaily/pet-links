Frequently Asked Questions

Is the elevation height adjustable?

No. Most Frisco models come at a fixed height (typically 5-7 inches). If your dog is exceptionally tall or short, or if you're looking to adjust as they age, this is a limitation. Brands like PetFusion offer adjustable heights, but at higher price points.

Will this work for multiple dogs?

Only if both dogs are similarly sized and eat at the same time without competing. The double-bowl design assumes peaceful coexistence. If you have a food-aggressive dog or significant size differences, separate feeding stations prevent stress and guarding behaviors—this design won't solve that.

How does the Frisco compare to stainless steel stands from other brands?

Frisco competes on price. Similar feeders from OXO or Healthymart cost $20-30 more but use reinforced metal or wood frames that last longer. If durability matters more than budget, those justify the premium. If you're replacing every 2-3 years anyway, Frisco's cost-per-year is hard to beat.
