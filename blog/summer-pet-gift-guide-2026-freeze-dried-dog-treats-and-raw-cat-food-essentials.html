Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between freeze-dried and raw pet food?

Freeze-dried treats have moisture removed through a preservation process, creating shelf-stable products requiring no refrigeration. Raw food remains in its natural state and requires freezing. Both preserve nutritional integrity—choose based on your lifestyle and storage capabilities. Zeal Pet Food excels in both categories.

Is raw cat food safe for all cats?

Raw feeding is generally safe for healthy cats when handled properly with good food safety practices. However, kittens, senior cats, or those with compromised immune systems should consult veterinarians before transitioning. Zeal Pet Food provides detailed transition guidelines to ensure safe dietary changes.

How much freeze-dried treats should I give my dog daily?

Treats should comprise no more than 10% of daily caloric intake. Portion sizes depend on your dog's weight and activity level. Zeal Pet Food packaging includes recommended serving guidelines. Start with smaller portions during summer transitions and adjust based on your dog's response.

Can I mix raw cat food with other diets?

Yes, raw food can be mixed with other quality foods, though many nutritionists recommend dedicated raw feeding for optimal digestive benefits. If transitioning gradually, feed raw meals separately from kibble. Zeal Pet Food's raw formulations are nutritionally complete as standalone diets.
