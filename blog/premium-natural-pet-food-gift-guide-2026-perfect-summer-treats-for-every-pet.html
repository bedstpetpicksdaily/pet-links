Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Zeal Pet Food different from other premium brands?

Zeal Pet Food distinguishes itself through transparent sourcing, ethically raised proteins, grain-free formulations, and innovative freeze-dried technology that preserves whole food nutrition without artificial preservatives or additives.

Is raw dog food appropriate for all pets?

While raw dog food benefits most healthy dogs, always consult your veterinarian before transitioning your pet to raw nutrition, especially if they have existing health conditions or are very young or senior dogs.

How should I store freeze-dried pet food?

Freeze-dried pet food should be stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once opened, keep in an airtight container and use according to package instructions for optimal freshness and nutrition.

Can I use premium natural pet food as treats only?

Yes, many premium natural pet foods like Zeal's freeze-dried options work wonderfully as training treats or meal toppers while your pet adjusts to new nutrition or maintains their regular diet.

Is grain-free pet food necessary?

Grain-free formulations benefit pets with grain sensitivities or allergies, though not all pets require them. Zeal offers both grain-free and grain-inclusive options to meet different nutritional needs and preferences.
