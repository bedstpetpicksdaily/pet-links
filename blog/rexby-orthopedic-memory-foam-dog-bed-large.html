Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Rexby memory foam bed suitable for all dog sizes?

The Rexby Large is specifically designed for large breed dogs. If you have a small or medium dog, consider checking their size guide or looking for smaller variants to ensure proper fit and support.

How often should I clean the Rexby dog bed?

The waterproof cover is removable and machine washable. We recommend washing it every 2-4 weeks, depending on use and any accidents. Spot cleaning between washes helps maintain freshness.

Will the memory foam flatten over time?

High-quality memory foam like Rexby's is designed to be durable, but some compression over time is normal with regular use. Most owners report the bed maintains its supportive properties for 2-3 years of daily use.

Can this bed help with my dog's arthritis?

Yes, orthopedic memory foam reduces pressure on joints and provides superior support compared to regular beds. Many dog owners with arthritic pets report improved mobility and comfort after switching to orthopedic beds like the Rexby.
