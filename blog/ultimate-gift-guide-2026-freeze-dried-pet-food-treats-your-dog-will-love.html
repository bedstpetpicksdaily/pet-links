Frequently Asked Questions

Are freeze-dried dog treats safe for all dog breeds and ages?

Yes, Zeal Pet Food freeze-dried treats are formulated for dogs of all ages and sizes. However, always introduce new foods gradually and consult your veterinarian if your dog has specific health conditions or dietary restrictions.

How should I store freeze-dried pet food to maintain freshness?

Store Zeal's freeze-dried products in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once opened, keep the package sealed or transfer contents to an airtight container to prevent moisture absorption and maintain nutritional integrity.

Can freeze-dried treats be used for training purposes?

Absolutely! Zeal's freeze-dried treats are lightweight, portable, and highly palatable, making them excellent training rewards. Many pet trainers prefer them because they're less messy than other treats and dogs respond enthusiastically.

What makes Zeal Pet Food different from other premium pet food brands?

Zeal specializes in freeze-dried raw nutrition using only premium natural ingredients with zero grains and artificial additives. Their commitment to preserving raw nutritional value while ensuring convenience sets them apart in the premium pet food market.

Is freeze-dried pet food more nutritious than traditional kibble?

Freeze-dried pet food retains significantly more nutritional content than heat-processed kibble, with up to 95% nutrient preservation. This makes it closer to a raw diet while offering superior convenience and storage benefits.
