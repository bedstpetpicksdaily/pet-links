Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I give my dog Vet's Best Dental Chews?

Most manufacturers recommend one chew per day for best results. Check your product packaging for specific dosage recommendations based on your dog's weight and size.

Are Vet's Best Dental Chews safe for all dog breeds?

These chews are generally safe for most dogs, but they're best suited for dogs that can chew properly. Consult your vet before giving them to puppies, senior dogs, or those with dental issues.

How long does it take to see results?

Most dog owners notice improvements in breath and reduced plaque after 2-4 weeks of consistent daily use. Results vary based on your dog's current dental health.

No, dental chews work best as a supplement to regular tooth brushing and professional vet cleanings, not as a complete replacement for proper dental care.
