The Problem With Traditional Dog Food

Traditional kibble relies on high-heat extrusion processing, a manufacturing method that dates back decades. While this process creates shelf-stable products with long storage lives, it comes at a significant nutritional cost. Temperatures exceeding 300°F destroy heat-sensitive vitamins, beneficial enzymes, and probiotics that support digestive health. Additionally, traditional dog foods often contain fillers, by-products, and artificial preservatives that offer minimal nutritional value while contributing to digestive upset and inflammation.

Many dogs experience symptoms like bloating, inconsistent stools, itchy skin, and low energy levels—issues that pet owners often attribute to aging or genetics rather than poor nutrition. In reality, these symptoms frequently stem from inadequate nutrient bioavailability and gut microbiome imbalance caused by low-quality ingredients processed at extreme temperatures.

Cold-Pressed Nutrition: How Nextrition Pet Makes a Difference

Cold-pressed dog food is manufactured at temperatures below 100°F, preserving the nutritional integrity of whole ingredients. Nextrition Pet utilizes this gentle processing method to maintain essential vitamins, natural enzymes, and probiotics that support digestive health and nutrient absorption. Cold-pressed nutrition means your dog gets food closer to whole, unprocessed ingredients—exactly what their ancestral diet would have contained.

Gut Health and Long-Term Wellness Benefits
