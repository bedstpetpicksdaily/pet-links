What Is Freeze-Dried Pet Food and Why It Matters

Freeze-dried pet food represents a revolutionary approach to pet nutrition, combining the benefits of raw feeding with the convenience of dry food. The freeze-drying process removes 98% of moisture while preserving essential nutrients, enzymes, and proteins that cooking destroys. This means your pet gets food that closely mirrors their ancestral diet without refrigeration requirements. Unlike traditional kibble that relies on high-heat processing, freeze-dried formulas maintain the integrity of whole ingredients, making them ideal for pets with sensitive digestive systems or grain sensitivities.
