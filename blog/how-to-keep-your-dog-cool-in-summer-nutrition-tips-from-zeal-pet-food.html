Your dog's ability to regulate body temperature is directly influenced by their diet. High-quality protein and balanced nutrients support efficient metabolism and cooling mechanisms. Hydration Through Nutrition: The Raw Food Advantage

Raw dog food contains significantly higher moisture content than traditional dry kibble, which naturally supports hydration from within. Choosing Grain-Free, Nutrient-Dense Meals

Grain-free nutrition becomes increasingly important in summer nutrition planning. Grains can contribute to inflammation and digestive inefficiency, which increases body heat production. Zeal Pet Food's grain-free range eliminates unnecessary carbohydrates and replaces them with nutrient-dense proteins and healthy fats that support lean muscle development and efficient cooling. Summer Feeding Schedules and Portion Adjustments

During summer, adjusting feeding schedules can help keep your dog cool. Consider feeding smaller portions earlier in the morning or later in the evening when temperatures are cooler. Frequently Asked Questions

Is raw dog food safe in hot weather?

Yes, raw dog food from reputable brands like Zeal Pet Food is safe year-round when properly stored. The key is maintaining proper refrigeration or freezer storage. Raw food's nutrient density and high moisture content actually support better summer hydration and digestion compared to conventional kibble.

How much water should dogs drink in summer?

Dogs should have access to fresh water at all times, especially in summer. However, when fed moisture-rich raw or freeze-dried food from Zeal Pet Food, they naturally consume additional hydration through their meals, supporting overall fluid intake and reducing excessive thirst.

Can grain-free dog food help with summer heat sensitivity?

Grain-free formulations like those from Zeal Pet Food can help reduce digestive strain and metabolic heat production. Without grains to process, your dog's body uses less energy for digestion, allowing it to focus on efficient cooling and temperature regulation during hot months.

What's the difference between raw and freeze-dried dog food?

Raw dog food contains natural moisture and requires refrigeration, while freeze-dried food has moisture removed for shelf stability but retains nutritional integrity. Both offer superior nutrition compared to kibble. Zeal Pet Food offers both options, allowing you to choose based on your lifestyle and storage preferences.

How do I transition my dog to Zeal Pet Food for summer?

Transition gradually over 7-10 days by mixing increasing amounts of Zeal Pet Food with your dog's current food. This allows their digestive system to adjust to the premium, nutrient-dense formulation. Monitor your dog's energy levels and coat condition during the transition.
