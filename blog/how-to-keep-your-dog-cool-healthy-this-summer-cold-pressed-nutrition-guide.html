Why Cold-Pressed Dog Food is Ideal for Summer Months

Cold-pressed dog food is manufactured at low temperatures, preserving the nutritional integrity and natural enzymes that traditional kibble processing destroys. During summer, when your dog's digestive system works harder to regulate body temperature, these preserved nutrients become even more valuable. Cold-pressed formulas digest more efficiently, reducing the metabolic heat your dog produces during digestion—a critical advantage when temperatures soar.

Nextrition Pet specializes in cold-pressed dog nutrition designed with summer wellness in mind. By choosing cold-pressed nutrition, you're giving your dog meals that are gentler on the digestive system while delivering superior bioavailability of essential vitamins and minerals.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much cold-pressed dog food should I feed my dog in summer?

Feeding amounts depend on your dog's age, weight, and activity level. Cold-pressed food is typically more nutrient-dense, so you may need less volume than traditional kibble. Consult Nextrition Pet's feeding guidelines or your veterinarian to determine the right portion for your individual dog. Monitor body condition and adjust as needed, especially if your dog is more active during summer.

Can I mix cold-pressed dog food with regular kibble?

Yes, you can transition gradually by mixing cold-pressed food with your dog's current diet. Start with 25% cold-pressed food and 75% regular food, gradually increasing the ratio over 7-10 days. This prevents digestive upset while allowing your dog's gut bacteria to adapt to the new, more beneficial food. Nextrition Pet's natural formulations typically transition smoothly without digestive issues.

How do gut-healthy dog foods help during summer?

A healthy gut microbiome improves nutrient absorption, enhances immune function, and reduces digestive stress—all crucial during summer heat. Nextrition Pet's gut-healthy dog food includes probiotics and prebiotics that strengthen the intestinal barrier and support beneficial bacteria. This creates a stronger foundation for your dog to handle summer's additional stressors.

Are Nextrition Pet's treats safe to freeze for cooling purposes?

Yes, most natural dog treats can be safely frozen. Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed treats are excellent frozen options that provide both cooling relief and nutritional benefits. Frozen treats help your dog stay cool while offering mental enrichment and satisfy their chewing instinct during hot afternoons.
