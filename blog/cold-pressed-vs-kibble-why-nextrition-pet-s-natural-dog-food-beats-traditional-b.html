What is Cold-Pressed Dog Food?

Cold-pressed dog food is manufactured using a low-temperature pressing process that preserves the nutritional integrity of raw ingredients. Unlike traditional kibble, which undergoes high-heat extrusion (often exceeding 300°F), cold-pressing keeps temperatures below 118°F. This gentle process maintains essential enzymes, probiotics, and nutrients that would otherwise be destroyed by heat.

Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed formula uses this advanced technique to lock in the benefits of whole meats, vegetables, and superfoods. The result is a nutrient-dense, easily digestible food that your dog's body can actually utilize efficiently. Many pet owners report seeing improvements in their dog's coat quality, energy levels, and digestive regularity within just a few weeks of switching.

Traditional Kibble: The Processing Problem

Conventional dry kibble relies on high-heat extrusion as its manufacturing method. While this process is cost-effective and extends shelf life, it comes with significant drawbacks. The extreme heat damages heat-sensitive nutrients, destroys beneficial probiotics, and can create harmful compounds during processing. Additionally, kibble often contains fillers, artificial preservatives, and by-products that provide minimal nutritional value.

Many dogs struggle to digest traditional kibble efficiently, leading to symptoms like bloating, gas, poor coat condition, and inconsistent stools. This is where Nextrition Pet's gut-healthy dog food stands apart. By using cold-pressed technology and natural ingredients, Nextrition Pet creates a formula that's not just nutritious—it's genuinely digestible and kind to your dog's intestinal system.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is cold-pressed dog food safe for all dogs?

Yes, cold-pressed dog food is generally safe for all dogs, though it's always best to transition gradually over 7-10 days. If your dog has specific health conditions, consult with your veterinarian. Nextrition Pet formulas are designed to support digestive health for dogs of all ages and sizes.

How does Nextrition Pet cold-pressed food stay fresh without artificial preservatives?

Nextrition Pet uses natural preservation methods and requires refrigeration to maintain freshness. This is actually a benefit—it means you're feeding your dog fresh food without harmful synthetic preservatives like BHA or BHT found in traditional kibble.

Can I mix cold-pressed food with kibble?

You can, but it's not recommended. Cold-pressed and kibble have different digestion rates and nutrient profiles. If transitioning from kibble, gradually replace kibble with Nextrition Pet's cold-pressed formula over 7-10 days for best results.
