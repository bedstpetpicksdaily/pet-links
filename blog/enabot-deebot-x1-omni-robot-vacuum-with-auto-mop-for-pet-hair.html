Frequently Asked Questions

How often do you need to empty the dock with multiple pets?

With two large dogs and a cat, I emptied the dock every 3–4 weeks during peak shedding season. During lighter shedding months, it stretched to 4 weeks. The 3.2L capacity handles daily pet hair volume surprisingly well, though the actual frequency depends on your pets' coat density. Hairier breeds might see emptying every 2–3 weeks.

Does the Deebot X1 Omni handle pet hair tangles around the brush?

Significantly better than my previous model. The brush design minimizes hair wrapping, though I still cleaned it every 1–2 weeks rather than waiting until visible tangling. With a Roomba, I was doing this every 5–7 days. Pet hair does accumulate, but the X1 Omni's engineering reduces the problem materially.

Is the mopping feature actually useful for pet owners, or is it just a gimmick?

It's genuinely useful but limited in scope. For daily spills, pet water accidents, and light dust on tile, it performs well enough to reduce your manual mopping frequency. For deep grout cleaning or stubborn pet stains, you'll still need a traditional mop. Think of it as handling 60–70% of your mopping needs, not replacing manual cleaning entirely.

How does it perform on different pet hair types—short vs. long fur?

Excellent on long, fine fur like golden retrievers. Short-haired breeds like labs and boxers also perform well, though sometimes short, dense hairs require brush cleaning more frequently. Cat hair was handled effectively. The real variable is shedding volume more than hair type—heavier shedders mean more frequent maintenance regardless of fur length.

Can it handle stairs or multi-level homes?

No, it cannot navigate stairs independently. You'll need to carry it between levels manually or purchase multiple units. For true multi-story homes with pets, this becomes a limitation worth considering. If your main pet living areas are single-level, it's less of a concern.
