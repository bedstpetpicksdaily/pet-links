Frequently Asked Questions

What makes cold-pressed dog treats better than regular treats?

Cold-pressed treats retain more nutrients, enzymes, and beneficial compounds compared to heat-processed alternatives. This preservation of nutritional integrity makes them particularly beneficial for dogs with sensitive digestive systems.

Can cooling treats help with my dog's digestion?

Yes, when made with natural, digestible ingredients like those found in Nextrition Pet products, cooling treats can support gut health. Many include probiotics and fiber that promote healthy digestion and beneficial gut bacteria.

How often should I give my dog summer cooling treats?

Treats should comprise no more than 10% of your dog's daily caloric intake. During summer, you can offer cooling treats more frequently as part of hydration and heat management, but always monitor overall calorie consumption.

Are natural dog treats safe for all dogs?

Most natural treats are well-tolerated, but introduce new products gradually, especially for dogs with known sensitivities. Always check ingredient lists for potential allergens and consult your veterinarian if your dog has digestive concerns.
