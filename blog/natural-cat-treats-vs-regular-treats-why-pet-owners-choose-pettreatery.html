Understanding the Difference: Natural vs. Regular Cat Treats

Regular cat treats are often made with artificial flavors, colors, and preservatives designed to extend shelf life and reduce production costs. These conventional treats may contain meat by-products, fillers, and chemical additives that provide little nutritional value. While they might keep your cat entertained temporarily, they can contribute to weight gain, digestive issues, and long-term health problems.

Natural cat treats, on the other hand, are formulated with real, recognizable ingredients like real meat, fish, and whole grains. They skip artificial additives and rely on natural preservation methods. Brands like PetTreatery specialize in crafting premium cat treats using ethically sourced ingredients without artificial flavors, colors, or preservatives, ensuring your cat gets nutrition with every bite.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are natural cat treats really worth the extra cost?

Yes. Natural treats like those from PetTreatery contain higher-quality ingredients with better nutritional density, meaning your cat needs fewer treats to feel satisfied. Plus, improved digestion and health reduce potential veterinary bills down the road.

Can natural cat treats help with weight management?

Absolutely. Natural treats from brands like PetTreatery are typically lower in fillers and empty calories. Real protein helps cats feel fuller longer, and without artificial additives, they support healthy metabolism and weight control.

Will my cat like natural treats if they're used to regular ones?

Most cats prefer natural treats once they try them, thanks to real meat flavors. PetTreatery offers a variety of flavors from salmon to chicken, so you can find options your cat loves while making a healthier choice.

How do I transition my cat to natural treats?

Start by mixing small amounts of PetTreatery's natural treats with your cat's current treats, gradually increasing the natural treat ratio over 1-2 weeks. This helps prevent digestive upset and allows your cat to adjust to the new tastes.
