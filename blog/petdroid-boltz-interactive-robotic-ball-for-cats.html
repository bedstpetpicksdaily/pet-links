Frequently Asked Questions

Will the PetDroid Boltz work on carpet or only hard floors?

It works on both, but movement is noticeably smoother and faster on hard floors. Carpet creates friction that slows the ball and makes its patterns more predictable. If your home is mostly carpeted, the toy will still function—just with less erratic movement that some cats find less stimulating.

How long do the batteries last?

Most users report 2-3 weeks of regular play per charge, depending on your cat's activity level and how often the ball is moving. The charging cable is included. Some owners keep two Boltz balls and rotate them to avoid downtime.

Is it loud enough to bother me while I sleep?

The motor produces a quiet humming sound, similar to a small robot vacuum. It's noticeable in a quiet room but won't disturb most sleepers. If your cat plays at night, you might hear the occasional bump against furniture, but it's not loud enough to warrant concern for most households.

What if my cat doesn't like it?
