Is it safe to leave running unattended?

Technically yes, but we don't recommend it for extended periods. The launcher continues throwing until the battery dies or you manually stop it. In a yard without fencing, a ball could roll into the street. In enclosed spaces, unsupervised play is fine—we left it running in a secured yard for 20 minutes while the dog played solo, and nothing went wrong. Just be realistic about your yard setup and your dog's tendencies. Some dogs chase balls into traffic; some happily retrieve them from the same spot repeatedly.
