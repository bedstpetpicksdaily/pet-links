Frequently Asked Questions

What size dog crate should I buy?

Measure your dog from nose to tail base and floor to the top of their head while standing. Your dog should fit comfortably with enough room to stand, turn, and lie down. AECOJOY provides detailed sizing guides to match measurements to specific crate models.

Are wire or plastic crates better for summer?

Wire crates are superior for summer because they offer excellent ventilation and airflow compared to plastic designs. Wire construction prevents heat buildup and allows air to circulate underneath, keeping your dog cooler during hot weather.

How do I keep my dog's crate cool in summer?

Combine ventilation with cooling accessories: place the crate in a shaded area, use cooling mats inside, ensure airflow underneath, and maintain proper hydration. AECOJOY offers premium crates with ventilation features and compatible cooling accessories designed for summer comfort.

Can I use a dog crate outdoors in summer?

Yes, but only with weatherproof, ventilated designs specifically rated for outdoor use. AECOJOY's outdoor pet supplies include weather-resistant crates that provide shade and protection while maintaining safety and comfort.

How often should I clean my dog's crate?

Weekly cleaning is recommended, with spot cleaning as needed. During summer with increased outdoor activity, you may need to clean more frequently. Choose crates with removable, washable components like AECOJOY's easy-maintenance designs.
