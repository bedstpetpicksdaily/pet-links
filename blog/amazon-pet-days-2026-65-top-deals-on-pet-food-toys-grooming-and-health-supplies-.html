What Pet Owners Should Know

  • Stock up strategically: Non-perishable items like dry pet food, treats, and health supplements are ideal to buy in bulk during sales events, as they have long shelf lives.
  • Check expiration dates: When ordering food and supplements, verify delivery dates and expiration windows to ensure freshness.
  • Compare subscription savings: Amazon Subscribe & Save often offers additional discounts beyond sale prices—layer these for maximum savings.
  • Read reviews before trying new brands: Pet food and health products should match your pet's specific needs; use customer reviews to gauge suitability.
  • Verify product authenticity: Purchase from Amazon directly or authorized sellers to avoid counterfeit pet health products.
