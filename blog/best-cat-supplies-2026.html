Buying Guide: 3 Things to Look For in Cat Supplies

  • Safety and Non-Toxicity: Always verify that toys, bowls, and litter boxes are made from cat-safe materials free of BPA, phthalates, and other harmful chemicals that could affect your cat's health.
  • Durability and Easy Maintenance: Choose supplies that withstand regular use and are simple to clean. Stainless steel, ceramic, and high-quality plastic are easier to sanitize than porous materials.
  • Size and Space Requirements: Consider your cat's size and your available space. A litter box should be 1.5 times your cat's length, and cat trees should fit comfortably in your home without overwhelming the space.

FAQ: Common Questions About Cat Supplies

What are the most essential cat supplies every owner needs?

The basics include a litter box and litter, food and water bowls, a bed or cat tree, toys for enrichment, a scratching post, grooming supplies, and a collar with ID tag. From there, you can add other supplies based on your cat's personality and needs.

How often should I replace cat supplies?

Litter boxes should be replaced every 1-2 years (or when damaged), toys need rotation and replacement as they wear out, and food bowls should be washed daily and replaced if cracked. Cat trees and scratching posts last 2-5 years depending on quality and use.
