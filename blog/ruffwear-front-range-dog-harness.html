Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Ruffwear Front Range Dog Harness good for no-pull training?

Yes! The front leash attachment clip helps redirect your dog's attention back toward you when they pull, making it a great tool for loose-leash walking and no-pull training. It's not a dedicated training harness, but the front clip adds meaningful control for everyday walks.

How do I choose the right size Ruffwear Front Range for my dog?

Ruffwear recommends measuring your dog's girth — the widest part of their chest — and using their official sizing chart to select the best fit. Because the harness offers four adjustment points, there's significant flexibility, but accurate measurements are key to ensuring a snug and safe fit.
