How long do these chew sticks actually last with heavy chewers?

With truly aggressive chewers (like rabbits that destroy cardboard boxes in hours), expect 1-3 days per stick. Moderate chewers get 1-2 weeks. Very light chewers might stretch one stick to 3 weeks. Are these safe for all small pets or just rabbits?

They're safe for rabbits, guinea pigs, chinchillas, and degus. However, I wouldn't recommend them for hamsters or gerbils—the sticks are too large and they prefer smaller chewing options. Always supervise initially and watch for any choking hazards with smaller species.

Personal preference matters. My rabbit devours them immediately while my guinea pig treats them like background furniture for weeks.
