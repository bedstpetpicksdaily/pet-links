Frequently Asked Questions

How large of an area can this system actually cover?

The PetSafe Wireless system typically covers a circular area up to half an acre, depending on your specific model. I tested ours across our three-quarter acre property and found reliable coverage in the open yard sections. If you have a larger property, you may need to invest in a wider-range model or accept that coverage has limits. Check the product specs for your exact yard dimensions before ordering.

Will my dog get shocked if they're just standing at the boundary?

No—that's actually one of the smartest design features. The system uses a progressive warning system: first your dog hears an audible beep when approaching the boundary, then a vibration, and only then a mild static correction if they keep moving forward. Murphy learned the beep meant 'stop' within two weeks, so she rarely triggered the actual correction. This gradual escalation is humane and effective.

Can this work in July heat without overheating the collar?

The collar stays reasonably cool, but I do notice battery drain accelerates in hot weather. During our hottest July weeks, I charged every 5-6 days instead of the usual 7. If you live in intense heat climates, plan on more frequent charging during summer months. Consider removing the collar during peak afternoon hours if your dog spends time indoors—they don't need containment monitoring while inside anyway.
