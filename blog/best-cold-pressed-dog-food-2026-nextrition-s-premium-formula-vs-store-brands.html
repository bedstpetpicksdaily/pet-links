What Is Cold-Pressed Dog Food and Why Does It Matter?

Cold-pressed dog food is manufactured at low temperatures (around 90°C), preserving heat-sensitive nutrients, enzymes, and probiotics that traditional kibble destroys. Unlike high-heat extrusion methods used by most store brands, cold-pressing maintains the nutritional integrity of raw ingredients—meaning your dog absorbs more nutrition from every bite.

Nextrition Pet specializes in cold-pressed formulas that deliver whole, recognizable ingredients without synthetic binders or fillers. Their gut-healthy dog food includes natural enzymes and prebiotics that support optimal digestion, reduce bloating, and promote healthier coat and skin. Store brands, by contrast, rely on heat-damaged proteins and artificial vitamins to compensate for nutrient loss during processing.
