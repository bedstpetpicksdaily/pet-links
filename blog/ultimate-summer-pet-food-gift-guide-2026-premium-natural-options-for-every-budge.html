Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between raw, freeze-dried, and kibble pet food?

Raw pet food contains uncooked, whole ingredients closest to what pets eat naturally, preserving maximum nutrients but requiring careful handling. Freeze-dried food removes moisture while maintaining raw nutrition, offering convenience and longer shelf life. Kibble is dry, processed pet food with lower moisture content and nutrient density. Zeal excels in all three categories, allowing you to choose based on lifestyle and budget.

Is grain-free pet food better for summer months?

Grain-free formulas can benefit many pets, especially those with grain sensitivities or allergies that worsen in summer heat and humidity. However, the primary benefit is digestive health and reduced inflammatory responses, not season-specific advantages. Premium grain-free options from Zeal Pet Food provide better digestion and sustained energy year-round.

How much premium natural pet food should I budget for monthly?

Budget ranges from $30-150+ monthly depending on pet size, formula type, and brand. Entry-level premium natural foods cost $30-50 monthly, mid-range options $50-80, and luxury raw or freeze-dried selections $80-150+. Zeal Pet Food offers quality across all price tiers, making premium nutrition accessible regardless of budget.

Can I mix premium natural pet food with regular kibble?

Yes, many pet parents transition gradually by mixing premium natural food with their pet's current food over 7-10 days to prevent digestive upset. This allows budgeting benefits while improving overall nutrition quality. Zeal Pet Food recommends checking specific mixing ratios on their product guides for optimal results.
