Frequently Asked Questions

Can automatic pet feeders with app control work without WiFi?

Most app-controlled pet feeders require a 2.4GHz WiFi connection for remote features, but they typically save your programmed feeding schedule locally. This means your pet will still be fed on time even if the WiFi goes down, though you won't be able to make remote changes during the outage.

Are automatic pet feeders suitable for both dogs and cats?

Yes! Most automatic pet feeders on Amazon are designed to work for both dogs and cats. However, it's important to check the hopper capacity and kibble size compatibility before purchasing, as larger dog breeds may require feeders with bigger food reservoirs and wider dispensing openings.

How do I keep my pet from breaking into the automatic feeder?

Many top-rated models feature locking lids and secure food compartments specifically designed to prevent curious or food-motivated pets from accessing the food supply between scheduled meals. Look for feeders with pet-proof locking mechanisms when shopping on Amazon.

How often do I need to clean an automatic pet feeder?

It's recommended to clean your automatic pet feeder at least once a week to prevent bacteria and mold buildup. Most quality models feature removable, dishwasher-safe bowls and food trays, making the cleaning process quick and hassle-free.
