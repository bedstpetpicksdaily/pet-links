Why Natural Pet Treats Matter in Summer

When temperatures rise, your pet's nutritional needs change. Natural pet treats free from artificial preservatives, colors, and flavors are gentler on sensitive stomachs and less likely to cause digestive upset. Summer is also when pets are more active outdoors, requiring snacks that provide sustained energy without excessive sugar or fillers. PetTreatery's natural pet snacks are formulated to deliver optimal nutrition while supporting hydration and digestive health during warm weather months.

Look for treats that are low in fat—as heavy foods can make pets uncomfortable in heat—and free from artificial ingredients that could cause allergic reactions. Choosing Between Dog Treats and Cat Treats

Dogs and cats have different nutritional requirements, and summer treats should reflect these differences. Dog treats can incorporate more variety with vegetables and grains, while cat treats should remain protein-focused and meat-based. Both species benefit from hydrating treats during hot weather. Storage and Safety Tips

Natural pet treats require proper storage, especially in summer heat. Keep treats in cool, dry places away from direct sunlight, and consider refrigerating freeze-dried or moisture-rich options. Always check expiration dates, as natural treats without artificial preservatives may have shorter shelf lives. Frequently Asked Questions

Are natural pet treats safe for dogs and cats with allergies?

Natural pet treats from reputable brands like PetTreatery are generally safer for allergic pets since they lack artificial additives and fillers that commonly trigger reactions. However, always check ingredient lists carefully and introduce new treats gradually. If your pet has known allergies, consult your veterinarian before switching treat brands.

How often can I give my pet treats during hot weather?

Treats should comprise no more than 10% of your pet's daily caloric intake, even in summer. Use natural treats strategically—perhaps as training rewards or afternoon snacks—rather than supplements to regular meals. Ensure your pet has constant access to fresh water, especially when consuming treats.

What's the difference between freeze-dried and traditional pet treats?

Freeze-dried treats retain more nutrients and have a longer shelf life than fresh treats, while containing less moisture (which can make pets feel fuller longer). Traditional treats may have better texture appeal for some pets. PetTreatery offers both types, allowing you to choose based on your pet's preferences and your storage capabilities.

Can natural pet treats help with pet hydration in summer?

Yes! Treats containing moisture-rich ingredients like freeze-dried fruits and vegetables can contribute to overall hydration. However, treats should never replace fresh water. They're a supplementary way to add variety and nutrition, not a hydration solution. Always ensure your pet has unlimited access to clean, fresh water.
