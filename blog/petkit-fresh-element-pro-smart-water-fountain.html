Frequently Asked Questions

How often do you actually need to refill it?

The Fresh Element Pro holds enough water for 2-4 days depending on your pet's drinking habits and number of pets. During summer (July specifically), daily refills are more likely for multi-pet or active households. Check your specific situation before assuming it's truly hands-off—the app alerts help, but you still need to physically refill it regularly.

Does the app work without WiFi or do you need constant internet?

You need WiFi for both setup and ongoing monitoring. If your router is far from the fountain location, connectivity becomes spotty. Some owners report needing to position the fountain closer to their WiFi router for stable connections. This is worth testing in your home setup before committing to the purchase.

How often do filters need replacing and what's the real cost?

Replacement filters are sold separately and typically need changing every 2-4 weeks depending on water quality and usage. This ongoing cost adds up—factor replacement filters into your total ownership cost, not just the upfront price. Check current filter pricing on Amazon before buying the fountain itself.

Will my cat actually use a smart fountain over a regular bowl?

Most cats prefer moving water regardless of whether it's 'smart.' The circulation and aeration matter more than app control. However, the app doesn't make your cat drink more—it just lets you verify they're drinking. If your cat ignores fountains entirely, being able to monitor it via app won't change their behavior.
