Frequently Asked Questions

What makes natural pet treats better than conventional treats?

Natural pet treats are made without artificial preservatives, colors, or flavors, making them easier to digest and gentler on your pet's system. They typically contain whole food ingredients that provide better nutritional value and can improve your pet's coat quality, energy levels, and overall health compared to mass-produced alternatives.

Are PetTreatery treats suitable for pets with food sensitivities?

PetTreatery offers a wide variety of treats with different ingredient profiles, including grain-free, limited ingredient, and allergen-specific options. However, always check the ingredient list carefully and consult your veterinarian if your pet has known sensitivities before purchasing new treats.

How should I store premium natural treats to keep them fresh?

Most PetTreatery treats come in resealable packaging designed for optimal freshness. Store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Check individual product instructions, as some treats may benefit from refrigeration after opening, especially those without artificial preservatives.

Can I give the same treats to both my dog and cat?

While many treats are formulated for either dogs or cats, some multi-pet treats exist. PetTreatery clearly labels treats by species, so always check packaging to ensure the treat is appropriate for your specific pet type.

What's the difference between PetTreatery's budget and luxury treat options?

Budget options ($15-$30) offer excellent quality natural treats in standard flavors, while mid-range ($30-$75) and luxury ($75+) options feature specialty ingredients, functional add-ins like probiotics, limited-edition flavors, and premium gift packaging suitable for special occasions.
