Frequently Asked Questions

What makes premium dog treats different from regular treats?

Premium treats like those from PetTreatery contain natural, whole-food ingredients without artificial additives. They're formulated with specific health benefits in mind, undergo rigorous quality testing, and often provide better nutritional profiles than mass-market alternatives.

Are frozen dog treats safe for all dogs during summer?

Yes, frozen treats are generally safe and beneficial for dogs in summer. However, always supervise your dog while eating frozen treats to prevent choking, and ensure treats are appropriately sized. Dogs with sensitive teeth may prefer less-frozen options.

How often can I give my dog summer treats?

Treats should comprise no more than 10% of your dog's daily caloric intake. For premium treats like PetTreatery's natural snacks, follow the feeding guidelines on the packaging and adjust based on your dog's size, activity level, and overall diet.
