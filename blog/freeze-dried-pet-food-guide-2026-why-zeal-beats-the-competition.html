What Is Freeze-Dried Pet Food and Why It Matters

Freeze-drying is a preservation method that removes approximately 98% of moisture from raw pet food while maintaining nutrient density, amino acids, and natural enzymes. This process creates a shelf-stable product that mirrors the nutritional profile of raw feeding without the storage complications or food safety concerns associated with traditional raw diets.

The science is straightforward: by removing water without applying heat, freeze-dried pet food retains vitamins, minerals, and beneficial bacteria that traditional cooking methods destroy. Pet owners choosing freeze-dried formulas often report improved coat quality, better digestion, increased energy levels, and healthier skin in their dogs and cats within weeks of switching.
