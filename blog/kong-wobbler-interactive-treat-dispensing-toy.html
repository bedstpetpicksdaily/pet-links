Frequently Asked Questions

What size dogs is the KONG Wobbler suitable for?

The KONG Wobbler comes in multiple sizes to accommodate small, medium, and large dogs. Choose the appropriate size based on your dog's weight and jaw strength for optimal safety and enjoyment.

How do you fill the KONG Wobbler with treats?

Simply twist open the top section of the wobbler, pour in dry kibble or small treats, and twist it closed. The adjustable openings control how easily treats fall out during play.

Can the KONG Wobbler help with weight management?

Yes, using the wobbler for meals or treats can slow down eating speed and provide portion control, supporting weight management efforts when used as part of a balanced diet plan.

How should I clean the KONG Wobbler?

Hand wash with warm soapy water after each use, or place it in the dishwasher on the top rack. Regular cleaning prevents treat residue buildup and odor development.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Overfilling with treats: Packing the Wobbler too full makes it frustratingly difficult for your dog, leading to abandonment of the toy. Start with fewer treats and adjust based on your dog's engagement level.
  • Using it as a meal replacement without adjustment: If using the Wobbler for regular meals, you'll need to reduce portion sizes in your dog's normal bowls to prevent overfeeding and weight gain.
  • Leaving it out constantly: The novelty and appeal fade quickly if your dog has 24/7 access. Rotate toys and use the Wobbler strategically during times when your dog needs the most engagement.

Expert Tips for Best Results

  • Experiment with treat size and shape: Mix kibble with slightly larger treats to create variety in difficulty levels. Odd-shaped treats roll differently than round ones, keeping the challenge fresh and interesting.
  • Use it before walks or training: Introduce the Wobbler 15-20 minutes before you leave or before a training session to tire out your dog mentally, making them calmer and more focused.
  • Clean it regularly: Residual treat dust and moisture can accumulate inside, creating an environment for bacteria. Hand wash it weekly with warm soapy water, especially if you're using wet treats or toppers.
