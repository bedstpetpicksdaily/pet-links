Frequently Asked Questions

When is Amazon Prime Day and how long do the deals last?

Amazon Prime Day typically occurs in July each year and lasts for 48 hours for Prime members. Some deals may extend beyond the official event window, but the best prices are usually available during the main Prime Day period. Check Amazon's homepage for exact dates and set up deal alerts for your favorite pet products.

Do I need an Amazon Prime membership to access Prime Day pet deals?

Yes, you need an active Amazon Prime membership to access most Prime Day deals. However, Amazon occasionally offers free trial memberships for new customers, and students can get discounted Prime membership through Amazon Prime Student. If you're not a member, consider signing up before Prime Day to access exclusive discounts on pet products.

What are the best types of pet products to buy during Prime Day?

Prime Day offers excellent deals on pet beds, toys, food and treats, grooming supplies, health products, and accessories. Items with higher regular prices typically see deeper discounts, making luxury orthopedic beds, interactive feeders, and premium pet food great value picks. Stock up on non-perishable items like toys and supplies you use regularly.
