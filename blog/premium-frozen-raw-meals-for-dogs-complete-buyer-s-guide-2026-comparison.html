What Are Premium Frozen Raw Dog Meals?

Premium frozen raw dog meals are minimally processed food products made from raw muscle meat, organ meat, and sometimes bones, preserved through freezing rather than cooking or drying. These meals aim to replicate what dogs would naturally eat in the wild, providing complete and balanced nutrition without fillers, artificial preservatives, or by-products. Quality brands source human-grade or USDA-approved ingredients and follow strict safety protocols to prevent contamination.

The key difference between raw and cooked diets lies in enzyme preservation and nutrient bioavailability. Freezing maintains these beneficial compounds, which heat processing destroys. However, premium frozen raw meals must still meet AAFCO standards for complete nutrition, ensuring your dog receives appropriate calcium, phosphorus, and other essential nutrients in proper ratios.
