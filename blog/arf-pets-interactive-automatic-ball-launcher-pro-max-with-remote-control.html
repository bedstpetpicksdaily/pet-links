Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the battery actually last between charges?

Expect 4-5 days of regular use (20-30 minute sessions) before needing a recharge, particularly in warm weather like July when battery efficiency drops. Cold seasons perform slightly better. Budget for recharging twice weekly if you use it daily. Consider buying a second rechargeable battery for uninterrupted play.

Will this work with my senior dog or puppy?

Yes, but with setup caveats. The adjustable speed settings mean puppies can learn at slower launch speeds without intimidation. For seniors, low-speed mode reduces impact. However, dogs must already understand fetch basics—this launcher won't teach the command itself. Arthritic dogs may benefit from reduced throwing distances.

Does it really need the specific ball brand or just standard tennis balls?

Standard tennis balls work fine, and that's your best cost option. Proprietary balls aren't required, though the hopper does prefer newer balls over worn, frayed ones that catch in the mechanism. Smaller toy balls work too depending on your dog's size, making it versatile across different breeds in multi-dog homes.
