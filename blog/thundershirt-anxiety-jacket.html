Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Thundershirt Anxiety Jacket work?

The Thundershirt applies gentle, constant pressure to your pet's torso, similar to the concept of swaddling. This pressure is believed to release calming neurotransmitters, reducing anxiety caused by thunderstorms, fireworks, travel, separation, and more — all without any drugs or sedatives.

What sizes does the Thundershirt come in, and how do I pick the right one?

The Thundershirt is available in sizes ranging from XXS to XXL for dogs, and also comes in a version designed specifically for cats. To choose the correct size, measure your dog's chest girth (the widest part of the ribcage) and compare it to the size chart provided on the Amazon listing. A proper snug fit is essential for the best calming results.

How long can my dog wear the Thundershirt at one time?

Most manufacturers and vets recommend not leaving the Thundershirt on for more than 1-2 hours continuously, especially during warm weather. Give your pet breaks to prevent overheating or skin irritation. For ongoing anxiety events like long travel days, monitor your pet closely and remove the jacket periodically.

Is the Thundershirt machine washable?

Yes! The Thundershirt is machine washable, making it easy to keep clean and fresh for your pet. It's recommended to wash it on a gentle cycle with cold water and air dry to maintain the fabric quality and velcro functionality over time.
