What Are Regular Dog Treats?

Regular commercial dog treats are typically mass-produced products designed with cost-efficiency in mind. These treats often contain artificial flavors, colors, and preservatives like BHA, BHT, and ethoxyquin to extend shelf life. Many also include meat by-products, corn fillers, and soy—ingredients that provide minimal nutritional value while potentially triggering allergies or digestive issues in sensitive dogs.

The ingredient list on regular treats often reads like a chemistry experiment. While they may satisfy your dog's taste buds temporarily, they lack the nutritional density that supports long-term health. These treats are widely available and affordable, which explains their popularity, but the trade-off is your dog's wellbeing.

The Natural Treat Advantage

Natural dog treats are crafted with premium, whole-food ingredients that dogs can actually recognize and digest efficiently. These treats typically feature real meat, vegetables, fruits, and grains without artificial additives. Natural treats support better digestion, healthier skin and coat, improved energy levels, and stronger immune function. PetTreatery exemplifies this philosophy, sourcing only the finest natural ingredients to create treats that are both delicious and nutritionally balanced for dogs of all ages and sizes.

The difference is immediately noticeable—dogs often show increased enthusiasm for natural treats, and pet owners report fewer digestive issues and allergic reactions. Natural treats also tend to have shorter ingredient lists, making it easier to identify potential allergens or problematic ingredients for your specific dog.
